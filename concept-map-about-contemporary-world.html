6. make a speech about contemporary world​


Good Day, everyone.

As we gather here today, I want to talk to you about the state of our contemporary world. We live in a time of great change and uncertainty, with challenges and opportunities that are unprecedented in human history.

One of the most significant developments of our time is the rapid pace of technological advancement. In just the last few decades, we have seen the rise of the internet, mobile phones, and other technologies that have transformed the way we communicate, access information, and do business. These technologies have brought us closer together and have opened up new possibilities for human progress.

But with these advances also come new challenges. The rapid spread of technology has led to the proliferation of fake news, online harassment, and other forms of digital manipulation. It has also contributed to the growing gap between the rich and the poor, as well as the erosion of privacy and other personal rights.

Another key issue facing our world today is the ongoing threat of climate change. The scientific evidence is clear: human activities, such as the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation, are contributing to the warming of the planet and the disruption of natural ecosystems. If we do not act now to address this crisis, we risk catastrophic consequences for ourselves and future generations.

In addition to these challenges, we must also confront the reality of inequality and injustice. Despite the progress we have made in recent years, many people around the world still suffer from discrimination, poverty, and other forms of oppression. We must work together to create a more equitable and fair society for all.

So where do we go from here? The first step is to recognize the gravity of the situation and to take action. We must use our collective power as citizens, consumers, and voters to demand change from our leaders and institutions. We must also do our part as individuals to reduce our own impact on the environment and to support the causes we believe in.

We are at a crossroads in human history. The choices we make today will determine the course of the future. Let us work together to build a better world for ourselves and for generations to come.

Thank you.


7. what is the Important you learned about globalization in contemporary world


Answer:

One of the most important things I have learned about globalization in the contemporary world is that it has had a significant impact on our economies, societies, and cultures. Globalization refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of the world's economies, societies, and cultures. This process has been facilitated by advances in technology, transportation, and communication, which have made it easier for people, goods, and ideas to move across borders.

Globalization has led to many benefits, such as increased economic growth, higher living standards, and greater cultural exchange. However, it has also created challenges, such as rising income inequality, environmental degradation, and cultural homogenization.

Another important aspect of globalization is that it has created both winners and losers. While some people and countries have benefited greatly from globalization, others have been left behind or have experienced negative consequences, such as job losses or cultural displacement.

Overall, the key takeaway from globalization in the contemporary world is that it is a complex and multifaceted phenomenon that has both benefits and challenges. As such, it requires careful management and policy solutions to ensure that its benefits are maximized and its challenges are mitigated.

14. How can you relate your course (BS Criminology) to the concepts introduced about Contemporary World? Cite a specific example or scenario.


Answer:

BS Criminology, as a field of study, is closely related to the concepts introduced about the contemporary world in several ways. One of the key concepts in the contemporary world is the relationship between society and crime. Criminology seeks to understand the causes of crime, the criminal justice system, and the various approaches to addressing crime in society.

A specific example of how BS Criminology relates to the contemporary world is through the examination of the role of technology in crime. The rapid advancement of technology has created new opportunities for criminal behavior, such as cybercrime, and has also changed the way crime is committed, investigated, and punished. BS Criminology provides the necessary skills and knowledge to understand the impact of technology on crime and to develop effective strategies for addressing this new form of criminal behavior.

In conclusion, BS Criminology provides a critical understanding of the relationships between crime, society, and technology in the contemporary world and equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge to address the challenges posed by these relationships.
