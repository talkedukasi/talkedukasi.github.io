9. characteristic of the musical style Rosendo Santos jr.


Characteristic of the Musical Style of Rosendo Santos Jr.

Rosendo Santos Jr. is a well-known Filipino composer in the world of contemporary music. In the Philippines, contemporary music is a blend of different styles from the 20th century music in the West. Such revolutionary styles include impressionism, expressionism, and neoclassicism. According to National Commission for Culture and the Arts, the musical style of Rosendo Santos Jr. is predominantly characterized by neoclassicism.

Neoclassicism and its Characteristics

The musical style, neoclassicism, is developed by Igor Stravinsky and Paul Hindemeth. It was said that neoclassicism is a reaction to emotionalism and romanticism.

The characteristics of neoclassicism as musical style is most notable for having:

balanceclarityeconomyemotional restraintorder
