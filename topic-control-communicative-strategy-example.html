Nomination Definition: Nomination is part of the process of selecting a candidate for either election to a public office, or the bestowing of an honor or award.

RESTRICTION:

Restriction Definition: The definition of a restriction is a limitation

Restriction Example: An example of a restriction is not being allowed to drink alcohol until you're 21 years old.

Turn-taking Definition: Turn-taking is a type of organization in conversation and discourse where participants speak one at a time in alternating turns.

Turn-taking Example: Christine Cagney: "I'm being quiet now. That means it's your turn to talk."

TOPIC CONTROL:

Topic Control Definition: Topic control is a communicative strategy used to control and prevent unnecessary interruptions and topic shifts in a certain conversation.

Topic Control Example: : “ Let's go back to the topic.”

TOPIC SHIFTING:

Topic Shifting Definition: Types of communicative strategies Topic Shifting Topic shifting, as the name suggests, involves moving from one topic to another.

Topic Shifting Example: This is a battle with corporations that continue to pollute the environment.

REPAIR:

Repair Definition: To repair is defined as to fix something.

Repair Example: To do whatever is necessary to make a non-working watch keep accurate time.

TERMINATION:

Termination Definition: The termination or cancellation of a contract signifies the process whereby an end is put to whatever remains to be performed thereunder.

Termination Example: Stealing, lying, failing a drug or alcohol test, falsifying records, embezzlement, insubordination, fraud, and felonious conduct.
