2. What is media-based arts and design in the Philippines?


Media-based arts and design is a type of creative arts and design that uses media as its primary medium. This can include everything from traditional arts and crafts to digital media and animation.


3. What are the characteristic of media based art and design in the philippines?


Answer:

The media-based art and design trends are photography, film, print media, digital media, and product and industrial design (also called applied arts).

You can learn more about applied arts on this link: brainly.ph/question/1046624

Here are the characteristics of each trend:

Photography

Today, photography is associated with the art of lighting. The Profession in Photography is often related to coverage of special events like weddings, prenups, graduations, and so on. Photography is also one of the most stable income in media-based businesses.  

Film

The Philippine film industry today is in a dire situation because locally produced films are not patronized well by the Filipinos. The PH film industry is still behind our ASEAN neighbors in terms of VFX and producing international quality films. However, The Philippines is recognized as one of the best sources of animation production.

Print Media

The oldest form of Media Industry in the Philippines. This includes newspapers, magazines, newsletters, tarpaulins, banners, and anything related to printing. Printing is a good business venture as long as you know the printing supplier industry.

Digital Media

The rising star of Media-based arts and design in the Philippines. Many of our fellow Filipino artists works abroad on big-time companies like Pixar and Disney. The Philippines also has plenty of medium and small animation studios that work with other international production companies.

Product and Industrial Design

Recently, Philippine furniture gained international attention and is now one of the most demanded products in Europe. Philippine still has more room to develop its industrial sector. Especially in producing local branded products.
