4. Water is running through a cast iron pipe of diameter 750 mm at 10 MPa. Calculate the thickness of the pipe, if the maximum permissible stress is 120 MPa.


Using Longitudinal Stress Formula (Pressure Vessel):

[tex]Sl = \frac{PD}{4t} [/tex]

where:

Sl = longitudinal stress

P = internal pressure

D = diameter

t = thickness

Solution:

[tex]t = \frac{(10)(750)}{4(120)} [/tex]

[tex]t = 15.63 \: mm[/tex]

21. Definition of Cast iron


The Cast Iron

Here are some definition and description for cast iron:

The cast iron is a group of iron-carbon alloys with a carbon content greater than 2%. Its usefulness derived from its relatively low melting temperature.The cast iron is made from pig iron, which is the product of melting iron ore in a blast furnace. It can be made directly from the molten pig iron or by re-molting pig iron.Cast iron has four types - grey cast iron, white cast iron, malleable cast iron, and ductile cast iron. Each of these types has unique physical and chemical properties.Cast iron sometimes melted in special type of blast furnace known as cupula but, it is more often melted in electric induction furnaces or by electric arc furnace.

Explanation:

Adaptor – is fittings that joints two different types of pipes together, such as ABS to cast iron or threaded to non- threaded.

Nipple – is a short length of pipe installed between couplings or other fittings.

MSS – refers to Manufacturer's Standardization Society.

Faucet – is a point of installation system where flow of water or gas could be manipulated
