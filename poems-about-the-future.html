In the years to come, when we look back

On the moments that shaped us and kept us on track

For they are the pillars of hope and care

The ones who make this world a better place to share.

5. please make a poem about "Our Home, Our Future"​


Our home, our future, intertwined,

A place of peace, a space defined,

Where we can dream and make our plans,

And build a life with our own hands.

The walls that hold us safe and sound,

Are more than bricks that we have found,

They're symbols of the love we share,

And memories that we'll always bear.

The roof above, it shields our dreams,

And shelters us from rain and beams,

It's where we rest and find our peace,

And where our joys and sorrows cease.

Within our home, we find our strength,

And bond with loved ones at great length,

We build our future, day by day,

And pave the path, in our own way.

Our home, our future, it's up to us,

To make it strong, to make it just,

To keep it pure, and keep it bright,

And make it a haven of delight.

So let us cherish, every brick,

And every beam, let's make them stick,

And make our home a sacred space,

Where love and hope can take their place.

26. Create a Poem about I Am Proud To Be Future Accountancy ​


Answer:

Accountants hover over the earth like helicopters,

Dropping bits of paper engraved with Hegel's name.

Badgers carry the papers on their fur

To their den, where the entire family dies in the night.

A chorus girl stands for hours behind her curtains

Looking out at the street.

In a window of a trucking service

There is a branch painted white.

A stuffed baby alligator grips that branch tightly

To keep away from the dry leaves on the floor.

The honeycomb at night has strange dreams:

Small black trains going round and round--

Old warships drowning in the raindrop.
