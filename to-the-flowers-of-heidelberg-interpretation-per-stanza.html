4. why did Rizal wrote a poem entitled to the flower of Heidelberg?​


Jose Rizal wrote “To the Flowers of Heidelberg” on April 24th 1886 while he was in Germany and studying opthalmology. He was fascinated by the flowers in the spring in Heidelberg and wrote this poem feeling a deep longing for his family and his country.
