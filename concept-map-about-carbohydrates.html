14. conclusion about organic compound (carbohydrates) ​


Answer

Carbohydrates constitute a group of chemically defined substances with a range of physical and physiological properties and health benefits for consumers. Their main function is to provide energy, but they also play an important role in the structure and function of cells, tissues, and organs.

Carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, and nucleic acids are organic molecules found in every living organism. These macromolecules are large carbon based structures.
The macromolecules are assembled by joining several smaller units, called monomers, together through a chemical reaction called dehydration synthesis.
