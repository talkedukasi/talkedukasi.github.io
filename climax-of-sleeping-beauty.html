4. plot story of beauty and the beast (Exposition,Rising Action,Climax,Faliing Action and denouement) please answer​


Answer:

Exposition

Learning the history of the Prince/Beast, and then Belle walking through the town singing; other characters (including the villain, Gaston) are introduced and some history is given as to who Belle is and where she and others fit in this town.

Rising Action

Gaston proposes and Belle refuses, he swears to have her as his wife no matter what he has to do; Belle searches for her father, finds him locked in the Beast’s castle, and she offers to become the Beast’s prisoner; we learn about the curse on the castle/Beast; Belle runs away and the Beast saves her; they become friends; the Beast tries to win her love; Belle’s father is sick and he lets her go; the townspeople find out about the Beast, and they go to kill him, while Belle tries to stop them.

Climax

Gaston is beating up the Beast, but then Belle arrives (the turning point) and instead of losing, he starts to fight back and defeats Gaston

Falling Action

Belle and the Beast are together again, and she professes her love for him; the Beast changes magically back into the Prince; all the enchanted objects turn back into people

Denouement

Belle confessed her love for him then the spell ended, and the beast returned to his original appearance, a handsome Prince.

13. plot summary of How the moon Became beautiful Give the 1.exposition2.Rising action3.Climax4.falling action5.Resolution6.Moral lesson​


Answer:

1. Exposition: At the beginning of the story, characters, setting, and the main conflict are typically introduced.

2. Rising Action: The main character is in crisis and events leading up to facing the conflict begin to unfold. The story becomes complicated.

3. Climax: At the peak of the story, a major event occurs in which the main character faces a major enemy, fear, challenge, or other source of conflict. The most action, drama, change, and excitement occurs here.

4. Falling Action: The story begins to slow down and work towards its end, tying up loose ends.

5. Resolution/Denoument: Also known as the denouement, the resolution is like a concluding paragraph that resolves any remaining issues and ends the story.

6.A moral is the lesson of a story. ... The moral of a story is supposed to teach you how to be a better person. If moral is used as an adjective, it means good, or ethical. If you have a strong moral character, you are a good member of society.

Plots, also known as storylines, include the most significant events of the story and how the characters and their problems change over time.

30. what is the climax of Black Beauty Chapter 1?​


The climax of Black Beauty Chapter 1 occurs when Black Beauty is separated from his mother. As the chapter progresses, the reader sees the bond between the young foal and his mother, and how they both enjoy the freedom and beauty of the meadow where they live. However, this all changes when the farmer comes to take the foal away from his mother and sell him at a fair. The separation is emotional and traumatic for both the mare and the foal, and marks a turning point in Black Beauty's life as he begins a new chapter in his journey.
