5. What's More 1.2: Poem AnalysisLet's Begin! You will interpret the message of this poem."When you love you should not think you can direct the course of love, for love, if it finds you worthy, directs your course". - Khalil Gibranneed answer please ​


The message of this poem is about the nature of love and how it should be approached. The poem suggests that when one is in love, they should not try to control or direct the course of the love, as love is something that cannot be controlled or manipulated. Instead, the poem suggests that if one is worthy of love, it will guide and direct their course.

The poet is emphasizing that love is a powerful and unpredictable force, and that one should not try to control it. Instead, they should let it guide them. The poem also implies that not everyone is worthy of love, it is a privilege, and it should be treated with respect and humility.

In summary, the poem is encouraging the reader to let go of their need to control and direct the course of their love, and to trust in the power of love to guide them. It also reminds us that love is a privilege, not a right and should be treated accordingly.
