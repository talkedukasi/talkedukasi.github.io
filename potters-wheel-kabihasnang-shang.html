9. anong kabihasnan ang potter's wheel​


Answer:

English answer: In pottery, a potter's wheel is a machine used in the shaping of round ceramic ware. The wheel may also be used during the process of trimming the excess body from dried ware, and for applying incised decoration or rings of colour

Explanation:

tagalog answer: Sa palayok, ang gulong ng magpapalyok ay isang makina na ginagamit sa paghuhubog ng bilog na ceramic ware. Answer:

Burnay are earthen jars with small openings while those with bigger mouths are called wangging. In the early years, the burnay, locally known as tapayan or banga, was used for storage of water, rice grains, basi (sugarcane wine) and condiments like salt and bagoong (fish paste).
