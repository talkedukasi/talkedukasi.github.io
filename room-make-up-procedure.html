Tuck the elasticized ends of the sheet around the mattress's corners to do this.Place the top sheet on top of the bottom sheet. Take the top sheet and place it on top of the bottom sheet. Remember to place the side with the large hem at the top of the bed, with the hem aligned with the mattress's head.Make a corner for the hospital. The term "hospital corner" refers to a particular manner of tucking the top sheet's end under the mattress. It's the trickiest aspect of making a bed, but it's also the one that makes it look the best.On top of that, place the duvet or comforter. After you've tucked the top sheets in, you may add your blankets, duvet, or comforter on top.Fold the top sheet and place the duvet on top. Fold the top sheet at the head of the bed over the top edge of the duvet, blanket, or comforter in a nice manner.Pillows should be fluffed. Grab your pillows and fluff them up before placing them on the bed. To fluff a pillow, hold it on both sides and squeeze the sides together before releasing - similar to accordion playing.Put the finishing touches on the project. Your bed is almost finished! To conclude, lean against the sleeping pillows for support and lay any ornamental cushions or pillows you may have in an upright posture at the top of the bed.
