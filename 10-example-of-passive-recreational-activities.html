20. what are the similarities of active recreation and passive recreation


Answer:

"Active recreation"

Active recreation is leisure time physical activity undertaken outside of structured, competition sport. It is a set of activities within the wider range of physical activity options that also include active living, active transport and sport.

"Passive recreation"

Passive recreation refers to non-consumptive uses such as wildlife observation, walking, biking, and canoeing. In the HLD restoration plan, the goal of providing passive. recreational uses is to ensure the least impact on the wetland ecosystem. Accessibility is a primary issue in the recreational plan.
