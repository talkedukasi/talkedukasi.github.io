9. effects of having strict parents ​


Answer w/ Explanation:

Having strict parents can have both positive and negative effects on children. Here are some possible effects:

Positive effects:

Discipline: Strict parents set rules and boundaries for their children, which helps to instill discipline and self-control in them.Academic success: Strict parents often place a strong emphasis on education and academic achievement, which can lead to their children performing well in school.Responsibility: Children of strict parents are often given more responsibilities at a younger age, which can help them develop a sense of responsibility and independence.

Negative effects:

Low self-esteem: Strict parents may be critical of their children, which can lead to low self-esteem and self-doubt.Anxiety and stress: Children of strict parents may feel constant pressure to meet their parents' high standards, which can cause anxiety and stress.Rebellion: Some children may rebel against their parents' strict rules and boundaries, leading to conflicts and strained relationships.

It's important for parents to find a balance between being strict and being supportive and nurturing. Children need structure and discipline, but they also need love, understanding, and a sense of acceptance.
