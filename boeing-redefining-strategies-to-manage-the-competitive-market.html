10. What is the difference between competitive strategy and competitive environment?​


Competitive strategy and competitive environment are two important concepts in the field of strategic management. Here are their differences:

Competitive strategy: This refers to the specific approach a company takes to achieve a competitive advantage over its rivals in the marketplace. It involves identifying and selecting a set of activities that the company will undertake to create a unique value proposition and differentiate itself from its competitors. Examples of competitive strategies include cost leadership, differentiation, and focus strategies.

Competitive environment: This refers to the external factors and conditions that affect a company's ability to compete in the marketplace. It includes factors such as the number and strength of competitors, the threat of new entrants, the bargaining power of customers and suppliers, and the threat of substitutes. The competitive environment can be analyzed using tools such as Porter's Five Forces analysis.

In summary, competitive strategy is the specific approach a company takes to achieve a competitive advantage, while the competitive environment refers to the external factors that impact a company's ability to compete. Understanding both of these concepts is crucial for developing a successful business strategy.

19. pagkakaiba at pagkakapareho ng (pcm)perfectly competitive market at (icm)imperfectly competitive market ​


Answer:

Pagkakaiba:

1. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may maraming mga kalakal at mga serbisyo na magagamit sa pamilihan, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mas maliit na bilang ng mga kalakal at mga serbisyo.

2. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may malinaw na presyo na nakikipag-ugnayan sa kalakal at serbisyo, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mga presyo na hindi malinaw at maaaring lumikha ng oligopoli.

3. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market ay may mga maliliit na tagagawa at konsyumer na nag-aangkin ng pag-aari, habang ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay may mga malalaking tagagawa at konsyumer na nag-aangkin ng pag-aari.

Pagkakapareho:

1. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market at ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay parehong hindi makontrol ang presyo ng mga kalakal at serbisyo.

2. Ang Perfectly Competitive Market at ang Imperfectly Competitive Market ay parehong naglalayong makamit ang pinakamataas na kita para sa mga tagagawa at konsyumer.
