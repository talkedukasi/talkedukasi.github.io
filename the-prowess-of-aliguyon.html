2. fantasy in the prowess of aliguyon​


Answer:

In the fantasy world of the Philippines, Aliguyon was a legendary hero known for his incredible prowess in combat and his deep sense of justice. Born to a noble family, Aliguyon was raised from a young age to be a great warrior and leader, and he took to his training with unmatched passion and determination.

As he grew older, Aliguyon became known throughout the land for his incredible strength and speed, and his skill with the sword was said to be unmatched by any living man. He became a fearsome figure to all who knew him, and even the most hardened of his enemies trembled at the mere mention of his name.

Yet despite his fearsome reputation, Aliguyon was also known for his great fairness and his deep compassion for those who were oppressed. He railed against injustice wherever he found it, and his sword flashed in righteous anger as he fought to protect the innocent and bring wicked tyrants to justice.

In countless battles and against all manner of foes, Aliguyon's prowess in combat never faltered. His sword cut through armor like butter, and his lightning-fast reflexes allowed him to dodge blows that would have killed a lesser man. He fought with such skill and ferocity that even the greatest warriors were helpless to stand against him.

And yet, for all his valor and skill, Aliguyon never forgot the importance of compassion, mercy, and justice. He stood against evil wherever he found it, and his reputation as a warrior king and a champion of the oppressed spread far and wide across the land.

In the end, Aliguyon's legacy as a hero of mythic proportions lived on long after he had laid down his sword and passed into legend. His feats of skill and courage were whispered about in the taverns and campfires of generations to come, and his memory was enshrined in the hearts of all who longed for a champion to fight for their cause.

23. One theme emphasized in the story, "prowess of aliguyon is family solidarity


HUDHOD NI ALIGUYON (THE PROWESS OF ALIGUYON)SummaryA hudhud is a protracted narrative performed during important events in the mountainous areas of Northern Luzon. It is the epic story Aliguyon. During harvest, a special lengthy story is sung. A heroic warrior called Aliguyon is a favorite subject of the hudhud.ThemesExemplifying romances glorifying Ifugao principles of love, marriage, and riches is the main focus of the Hudhud tales.This story emphasizes nonviolent conflict resolution and a heroic heritage. The celebration of the double wedding is a symbol of the eradication of animosity, i.e., these foes will not exist in the future generation.Family SolidarityAnother subject in the hudhud that is expressed is family solidarity. The hudhud would begin with a father-to-father argument that would gradually trickle down to the kids. Any opponent or outsider who committed offenses against the family must be avenged, and the children must continue the struggle.RespectThe correct words for parents and elders were used, such as Aman for a father and Innan hi for a mother. Additionally, the mother of the hero is honored and given importance. It is made clear in one passage of the epic when Pumbakhayon's mother intervenes to break up a conflict between the two characters by insisting that he eat first.
