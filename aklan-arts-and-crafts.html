Explanation:

1. mindoro

PROVINCE: Mindoro is known for their world-famous splendid beaches, coral reefs, and diving sites for new and experienced divers. It is been cared of by both local and national government to preserve the natural setting of Mindoro

ARTS AND CRAFTS: The arts and crafts in Mindoro is an amalgamation of indigenours people that lived in the region pre-colonization era and the vivid influence of the Spanish conquistadors.

2. marinduque

PROVINCE: The main source of economy in Marinduque is the agricultural sector such as rice and coconuts.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Since agriculture is the top source of Economy in Marinduque, it is not surprise that the materials for their arts are coming from rice and coconuts, too. They thrive in traditional weaving and basketry.

3. romblon

PROVINCE: Romblon is where marbles come from. They are rich in marble deposits.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Known for traditional weaving and using of marbles for architecture.

4. palawan

PROVINCE: Placed on the world map as one of the province with the best beaches.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Known for their basketry such as tingkop

5. panay island

PROVINCE: Consisting of Aklan, Antique, Capiz and Iloilo; these provinces have gained a lot of attraction for the development for the natural resources. Iloilo itself is a wonder as it has developed into a multicultural area with a lot of infrastructure developments.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Known for its Pinya fibers, they have delved into the fashion industry for creating Traditional Filipino Attire.

6. aklan:

PROVINCE: Aklan is famous for Boracay. So, in recent decades, companies invested in Aklan and it is now roaring with malls, hotels, and clubs.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Abaca is their main export to produce cloth, bags and wall decors, and toher hand-crafts.

7. negros island

PROVINCE: Negros Island suffered from extreme poverty in the 70’s and 80’s. Now its pride is its sugar industry.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Hacienda Crafts is a form of profiting that sugarcane workers do to augment their income by producing sustainable products.

8. samar

PROVINCE:  Home to it’s cave system and longest bridge.

ARTS AND CRAFTS: Mat weaving or banig is the main exports from Samar.
