2. How do you choose a template in PowerPoint?​


Answer:

1. In PowerPoint, click File, and then click New.

2. Do one of the following: Type a key word or phrase into the Search for online templates and themes field, and press Enter. Choose a template.

3. When you find the template that you want, click it to see the details, and then click Create.

30. what is the smallest unit of a PowerPoint presentation? It is similar to a page of a book or documenta. file c. templateb. slide. d. layout


Answer:

b. slide

Explanation:

PowerPoint slides can be equated to MS-Word’s pages.  A slide is equivalent to a single page.  PowerPoint slide is where contents are created.  A typical slide consists of slide title, slide header and/or footer, images/photos, icons and even media contents such as video and audio files.  A slide can be customized using the slide master.  The slide master is very useful especially when you are dealing with large number of slides because this is where the user can customize or change the formatting of title, header/footer, color palette, or font style on all slides by just editing once in the master slide section.
