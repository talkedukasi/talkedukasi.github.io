19. what is the role of an artist in the community and nation ? ​


Every artist has a different and important role to play in contributing to the overall health, growth and well-being of our community.

Artists build a sense of community There are many positions that the artist plays. But in smaller cities, having local artists gives the city a sense of pride. It also sets examples for young people who might consider careers in the arts. Artists benefit their societies by teaching their arts and crafts.

Creative thinkers and makers give joy, engagement and motivation to their communities, but they also give thoughtful criticism of our political, economic and social systems—inducing communities to participate thoughtfully and take steps towards social change.
