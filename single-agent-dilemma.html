Individual - conflict arrives when a person is asked to choose between two important values for him or her for example, choosing between one’s duties to his or her family one’s love for another person.

Organizational - encountered by institutions, business, or organizations in their decision-making process, at this level the dilemmas that the organizations’ experiences usually affect more than one person and they can be part of the internal group or part of an external stakeholder.

Structural - affect a network of institutions and operative theoretical paradigms like universal care, juvenile laws, and immigration. this type of dilemma can affect a community and even a society at large.
