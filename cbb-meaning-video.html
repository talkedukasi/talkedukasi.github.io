2. It is a short clip usually 15-30 seconds found in the beginning of the video lesson. a. B-roll b. Brand bug c. OBB d. CBB​


Answer: c. OBBIt is a short clip usually 15-30 seconds found in the beginning of the video lesson.OBB is short for Opening BillboardIt is used to introduce a show or a segment.

Let's know the meaning of the other options:

B-rollB roll, B-reel, or B reel refers to extra or alternate video that is intercut with the main picture.Brand bugIt's a graphical element that normally appears in or around a specific corner of the video's visible area for the duration of the show. Its placement keeps the bug out of the way of the video's main emphasis, and it's normally stylized in a way that doesn't bring too much attention to itself.CBBCBB is short for Closing BillboardIt is used to close a show or a segment.
