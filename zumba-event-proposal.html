Physical activity, also known as exercise, can help you stay healthy and lower your chance of acquiring diseases including type 2 diabetes, cancer, and heart disease. Physical activity and exercise can improve your health both now and in the future. Most significantly, regular exercise can help you live a better life.

You can reap these benefits if you exercise for at least 30 minutes a day.

Regular physical activity has several advantages. If you exercise on a regular basis, you may be able to:

1.spare yourself from a heart attack

2.take better control of your weight

3.have a lower amount of cholesterol in your blood

4.The risk of type 2 diabetes and several malignancies is reduced.

five. your blood pressure is lower

5. a lower risk of getting osteoporosis by having stronger bones, muscles, and joints

6.reduce your chances of falling

7. better recuperate from moments of stress

8.recover more quickly after hospitalization or bed rest

9.feel better - you'll have more energy, be happier, sleep better, and have a better mood.

10.A better mental condition

Exercise has been shown to aid with depression in a number of studies. There are a variety of perspectives on how exercise can benefit persons who are depressed:

Exercise might help you forget about your troubles or filter out bad thoughts.

When you exercise with others, you'll have more social interaction.

Your mood may improve and your sleep patterns may improve as a result of increased fitness.

Serotonin, endorphins, and stress hormones are all substances that can be altered by exercise in the brain.
