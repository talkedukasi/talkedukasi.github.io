How is the language of academic texts different?​


Answer:

1. We wear formal clothes when we attend church or formal parties,and we can wear casual clothes whenever we are in our home or going to mall.

2. When we attend formal occasions, we should act polite,do proper etiquette and use formal language.

3. We can distinguish the tone of a language used in a text by use of syntax, point of view and the level of formality in your tone.

4.Formal language is characterized by the use of standard English, more complex sentence structures, infrequent use of personal pronouns, and lack of colloquial or slang terms. Informal language allows the use of nonstandard English forms, colloquial vocabulary and typically shorter sentence structures.

5.Academic writing uses formal language that minimizes the use of contractions and colloquialisms and avoids slang terminology whenever possible not like informal language.
