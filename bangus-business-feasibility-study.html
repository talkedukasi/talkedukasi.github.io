1. 1. What is the Feasibility study of agri-business?the feasibility of agribusiness?​


Answer:

A feasibility study of agribusiness is an analysis of the potential profitability and sustainability of a proposed agricultural business venture. It typically includes an examination of factors such as market demand for the products or services, production and operating costs, competition, and potential risks and opportunities. The study may also include a review of the availability and suitability of land, labor, and other resources required for the business. The goal of the study is to determine whether the proposed venture is viable and worth pursuing, and to identify any potential challenges or issues that need to be addressed before proceeding.

4. what is the difference between feasibility study and business plan?


Answer:

A feasibility study is done before starting a business, when you have the idea for the business but you want to make sure it's feasible, or advisable. On the other hand, a business plan details how the business will operate. It assumes your feasibility study has been completed and it was determined the idea is viable.

Swine production is the process of raising pigs for meat, which can be a profitable business venture. A feasibility study for a swine production business would involve evaluating the market demand for pork products, the competition from other pig farmers, the resources needed to raise pigs, and the financial projections for the business.

Market demand: The demand for pork products can be evaluated by researching the local and regional markets for pork products. This can include analyzing consumer trends, the availability of pork products, and the pricing of pork products.

Competition: The competition from other pig farmers can be evaluated by researching the number of pig farmers in the area, their production capacity, and their pricing strategies. This can help determine whether there is room in the market for a new pig farmer.

Resources: The resources needed for swine production include land for raising pigs, pig feed, water supply, and veterinary services. A feasibility study would evaluate the availability and cost of these resources in the local area.

Financial projections: The financial projections for a swine production business would include the cost of raising pigs, the revenue from selling pigs and pork products, and the potential profits from the business. This can help determine whether the business is financially viable.

Example: A farmer is considering starting a swine production business in a rural area. They also find that there are only a few pig farmers in the area, which indicates that there is room in the market for a new pig farmer. The farmer evaluates the resources needed for swine production and finds that they have access to land, water, and veterinary services. They also find that the cost of pig feed is reasonable. Based on these findings, the farmer decides to start a swine production business and creates a financial projection that shows the business will be profitable.

28. 2.Conduct a Feasibility Study >conduct a feasibility study to determine whether the business idea is viable. A feasibility study involves evaluating the market demand, the competition, the required resources, and the financial projection for the business.?Can anybody will explain this in simple explanation? Or paki explain na lang Po sa Tagalog para maintindihan?​


Answer:

Ang feasibility study ay isang pagsusuri o pag-aaral upang malaman kung viable o feasible ang isang business idea o plano. Ito ay ginagawa para malaman kung mayroong sapat na merkado o demand, kung anong kumpetisyon ang makakaharap ng negosyo, kung ano ang mga kailangang resources (kagamitan, tao, puhunan) para sa negosyo, at kung magkano ang posibleng kikitain o magastos ng negosyo sa hinaharap.

Sa pamamagitan ng paggawa ng feasibility study, maiiwasan ang posibleng mga problema at risk sa negosyo, at magbibigay ng sapat na kaalaman sa mga negosyante upang makagawa ng tamang desisyon. Ang feasibility study ay mahalaga upang malaman kung worth it ba ang magtayo ng isang negosyo o hindi, kung mayroong sapat na oportunidad o demand para sa produkto o serbisyo na ihahandog ng negosyo, at kung kaya ng negosyo na magpakatagal at magkaroon ng matagumpay na kita.

Ang mga tampok ng feasibility study ay ang pagsusuri sa merkado at demand, pag-aaral sa kumpetisyon at trend sa industriya, pagtaya sa mga kinakailangang resources, pagtasa sa financial projection o posibleng kita at gastos ng negosyo, at pagbibigay ng mga rekomendasyon at alternatibong plano kung kinakailangan.

Sa simpleng salita, ang feasibility study ay isang paraan upang malaman kung feasible o may pag-asa ang isang negosyo na magtagumpay sa merkado, at kung magkano ang posibleng kita o magastos ng negosyo sa hinaharap.
