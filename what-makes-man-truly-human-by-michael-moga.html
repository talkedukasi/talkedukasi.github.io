It's the ability to make a psychic and emotional connection with another person, to actually enter into their mind-space. When we experience real empathy or compassion, in a sense our identity actually merges with another person's. 30. 1. In your own idea, what is freedom? 2. What are the different things you consider as a freedom? 3. Is it freedom that makes man truly become a human person?


Answer:

1.)In my understanding, freedom is the ability to make choices and act on them without external restraint or coercion.

2.)Some different forms of freedom include political freedom, personal freedom, and economic freedom. Political freedom is the ability to participate in the political process and have a say in how one's government is run. Personal freedom is the ability to live one's life as one chooses, without interference from others. Economic freedom is the ability to control one's own resources and make economic choices without government interference.

3.)The concept of freedom is closely tied to the idea of human dignity and autonomy, and it is often considered an essential aspect of human flourishing. However, whether freedom is what makes a person truly human is a matter of debate and can be interpreted differently depending on one's perspective
