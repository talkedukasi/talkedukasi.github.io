11. Type of soil used without sunlight day 2​


Answer:

Assuming the context is in regards to plants, the type of soil that can be used without sunlight for Day 2 should be a soilless or hydroponic growing medium. These media do not rely on sunlight for nutrient absorption, as they provide essential nutrients directly to the plant roots through a water solution. Examples include coco coir, peat moss, perlite, vermiculite, and rockwool.

It's important to note that even if the plants are grown without sunlight, they still require appropriate lighting, such as grow lights, for photosynthesis to occur. Without photosynthesis, the plant would not be able to grow or develop properly.
