29. Why is it importants to know the needs and wants of specific community


Answer:

Knowing the needs, wants, and assets can be helpful to your community or organisation at almost any point in your initiative. If your group has a specific goal, such as reducing teen pregnancy, identifying local needs (better communication between parents and teens, education programs, etc.) and resources (youth outreach programs, peer counselors) related to the issue can help you craft a workable, effective goal.


30. needs and wants of the community in business plan​


Answer:

When creating a business plan, it is important to identify the needs and wants of your community. These can range from basic necessities such as food and shelter to more complex desires like entertainment or convenience services.

It's also essential to consider current trends in order to develop products that appeal to modern consumers. Additionally, understanding cultural preferences could help you tailor your offerings accordingly. Ultimately, doing research on the target audience can ensure that any potential venture will be successful by providing what people actually need and want.
