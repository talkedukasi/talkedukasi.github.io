He is using it to trick people, because anybody who has seen that reflection of the man who turns his back to the mirror would most likely return the mirror and ask for a refund.

Since the vendor gives back less than a half the amount Dani paid earlier, the vendor became happy and that's the reason why he said "Back again" to the mirror. I think the vendor managed to trick many people before Dani.

Displacement = d1 + d2

                         = 161 m East + ( - 194 m West )

                         = 33 m due to west
