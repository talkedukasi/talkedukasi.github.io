20. summary of by the railway side ,can't you answer


The summary of Alice Meynell’s By the Railway side is as follows:

The narrator left Tuscany by train and is on her way to Genovesato. As she goes down the train, she hears a man who is shouting, apparently having a spat with train officials, and being calmed down by a woman. However, nothing can be done and she proceeds to her hotel near a roofless theatre presenting Offenbach.
