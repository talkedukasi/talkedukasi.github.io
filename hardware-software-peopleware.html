3. what is hardware software and peopleware


Answer

Hardware

It refers to the physical parts of a computer and also the tool a person uses to interact with the computer. Hardware is something we can touch or feel. Examples of this are the Motherboard, Hard Disk Drive, Keyboard, Mouse, Printers, and other computer parts.

Software

It refers to the stored data on a computer. Software and Programs are intangible meaning, that we can't hold them physically. They are usually made of electrical charges that travel by wires and circuit boards.

Peopleware

They refer to actual people with involvements in the development and improvements of the hardware and the software. These people are commonly known as programmers and technicians.

4. how do hardware and software differ from peopleware​


Answer:

Computers operate using a combination of hardware and software. Peopleware is less tangible than hardware or software, since it can refer to many different things. Examples of peopleware include individual people, groups of people, project teams, businesses, developers, and end users.
