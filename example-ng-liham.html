25. Give me an example of "Bahagi ng Liham" reward: 20 points ​


Answer:

An example of a "Bahagi ng Liham" (Parts of a Letter) could be to identify and correctly label the different parts of a formal letter. Here's an example of how the parts of a formal letter could be labeled:

1. Date - The date the letter is written.

2. Inside Address - The name and address of the recipient of the letter.

3. Salutation - The greeting used to address the recipient (e.g. "Dear Mr. Smith").

4. Body - The main content of the letter.

5. Closing - The final message of the letter (e.g. "Sincerely," "Best regards," etc.).

6. Signature - The author's signature.

7. Typed Name - The author's typed name.
