14. 1. Which program should be based upon understanding the needs and problems of the students,competence and interest of the guidance personnel?A. Guidance toolsC. Guidance principlesB. Guidance servicesD. Guidance techniques​


Answer:

Guidance counseling /Guidance services

Explanation:

Guidance counseling also referred to as Guidance and counseling, is the process of helping individuals recognize and develop their potentials. This could be vocational, educational, and even psychological with the sole purpose to attain the best possible result.

Children and students need guidance counseling to bring out the best in them. The program is encouraged in education institutions to help understand the needs and problems of the students, competence, and how to help them become the best version of themselves.

School counselors and parents are great influencers of guidance counseling on children. A school counselor works in educational institutions such as primary, secondary, and tertiary schools so the students can access social-emotional assistance.
