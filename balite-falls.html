Balite Falls is located in Cavite's coffee capital of Amadeo. It has been developed into a resort by private individuals and is divided into two parts (more on this later.) The waterfalls is not as high as that of Hulugan Falls or Bukal Falls in Laguna, making it relatively safe for diving.
