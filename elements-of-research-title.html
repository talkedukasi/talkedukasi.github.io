8. what elements make up an excellent research title ​


Answer:

Effective titles in academic research papers have several characteristics.

Indicate accurately the subject and scope of the study.

Avoid using abbreviations.

Use words that create a positive impression and stimulate reader interest.

Use current nomenclature from the field of study.

This study aims to identify the difficulties encountered by senior high school STEM students in doing research. The study is primarily based on the theory of challenge and support which explained that the two factors need to be balanced to attain the students' personal development and growth.Utilizing content analysis as a design and latent analysis in analysing data, four categories of difficulties are identified in the study include paper-related, research partners, advisee to adviser, and personal issues and hindrances.
