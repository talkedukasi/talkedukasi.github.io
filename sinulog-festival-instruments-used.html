12. Research the following FestiVAL SINULOG FESTIVAL


Answer:

Sinulog Festival is an annual cultural and religious festival held in Cebu City, Philippines every third Sunday of January. It is one of the grandest and most popular festivals in the country, attracting tourists and locals from all over the Philippines and even abroad.

The Sinulog Festival is a celebration in honor of the Santo Nino de Cebu, a Roman Catholic image of the child Jesus, which was presented as a gift by Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan to Queen Juana of Cebu in 1521.

The festival features colorful street dancing accompanied by the rhythmic beat of traditional drums and music. Participants, dressed in colorful costumes, perform a dance called the "Sinulog" which is characterized by two steps forward and one step backward, in honor of the child Jesus. Other highlights of the festival include the solemn procession of the Santo Nino and a fluvial parade where the statue of the Santo Nino is brought on a boat and paraded along the Mactan Channel.

Aside from the religious aspect, the Sinulog Festival also showcases the Cebuano culture through various food fairs, street parties, concerts, and other cultural activities. It is considered as a major economic boost for Cebu City as it attracts millions of tourists and generates a lot of business for food and souvenir vendors.

Overall, the Sinulog Festival is a colorful and vibrant celebration of faith, culture, and tradition that has become an integral part of Cebuano identity and heritage.
