2. helen finished knitting a blanket. she wanted the length of the blanket to be 9cm longer than twice the width of the blanket. if the area of the blanket is 200cm^2, find the dimensions of the blanket? ​


Answer:

L = 25

W = 8

Solution:[tex]L = 9 +2W[/tex][tex](9+2W)(W)=200[/tex][tex]9W+2W^2=200[/tex]

Subtract 200 to both sides

[tex]9W + 2W^2 - 200 = 0[/tex]

Change the places of [tex]2W^2[/tex] and [tex]9W[/tex] to form it like [tex]ax^2 + bx + c = 0[/tex] that can be solved using quadratic formula: [tex]x = \frac{-b±\sqrt{b^2-4ac} }{2a}[/tex]

[tex]2W^2 + 9W - 200 = 0[/tex]

Thus, [tex]w = \frac{-9±\sqrt{9^2-4(2)(-200)} }{2(2)}[/tex]

[tex]w =\frac{-9±\sqrt{1681} }{2(2)}[/tex]

[tex]w =\frac{-9±41 }{4}[/tex]

We will ignore the negative answer because there are no dimensions of any figure that is negative.

Thus, [tex]w = \frac{32}{4} = 8[/tex]

Since we already found the width, we can now find the length.

[tex]L = 9 + 2W[/tex]

[tex]L = 9 + 2(8) = 25[/tex]

To check if the answer is correct, lets multiply it

25 × 8 = 200, which is the area of the blanket.

25. how to fold the blanket​


Answer:

1.Unfold the blanket completely and lay it on a flat surface. To make it as easy as possible to fold your blanket, it is best to lay it out flat. Hold the blanket in your hands at the shorter edge, and stretch it out flat across your floor.

•This way, you can easily reach each corner as you make your fold.

2.Fold the blanket in half. Hold the blanket by the corners, and bring the corners to the opposite side to make your first fold.

•If your blanket is rectangular, fold it in half lengthwise.

3.Smooth out any folds or wrinkles using your hands. As you make your folds, run your hands over the top of the blanket starting from the middle outward to remove any creases, folds, or wrinkles.

•Your blanket will lay flat and neat as longs as there are no wrinkles.

4.Fold the blanket in half crosswise. Grab the shorter side of the blanket with your hands and bring it over to the opposite side to create your fold.

•Smooth out any creases or wrinkles after you make your second fold.

5.Store the blanket as is or continue to fold it in half to make it smaller. If you want your blanket to be folded up smaller for storage, just keep folding it in half until you're satisfied with the size.

•Keep in mind that if you fold the blanket in half too many times, it may get bulky and not be able to stay folded.
