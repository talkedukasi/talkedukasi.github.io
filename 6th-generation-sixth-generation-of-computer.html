CHOICES:

A. 4th major idea

B. 6th major idea

C. 1st major idea

Answer:

A. 4th major idea

Explanation:

The major idea being described is the 4th major idea of evolution by natural selection, which is "differential survival and reproduction of individuals in a population based on variation in their traits." This idea suggests that individuals with adaptive traits are more likely to survive and reproduce, passing on those traits to their offspring and increasing their frequency in the population over time.


10. Given General Term = n x n x n, find the 6th term of the sequence 1, 8, 27, 64,____


Answer:

The given sequence is formed by raising the positive integers to the third power, which gives the cubes of the numbers. Thus, the next number in the sequence is:

5^3 = 125

So the sequence becomes:

1, 8, 27, 64, 125

The general term of this sequence can be written as:

n^3

So the sixth term in the sequence is:

6^3 = 216

Therefore, the sixth term in the sequence is 216.
