3. 1. What makes the contribution of the local writers important to the development of the national literature?2. Why do we need to understand the literature of each region?​


Answer:

1. The contribution of local writers is important to the development of national literature because it reflects the cultural diversity and richness of the Philippines. It allows for the preservation and promotion of indigenous traditions, beliefs, and values, and provides a platform for the expression of local experiences and perspectives.

2. Understanding the literature of each region is important because it provides insights into the unique culture, history, and identity of the people living in that area. It allows for a deeper understanding and appreciation of their beliefs, customs, and traditions, and can help bridge gaps and foster cultural exchange between different regions in the country.

One of the longest known epics in the world is the ethno-epic Hinilawod of the ancient people of Sulod in Central Panay, the only epic recorded in the Visayas.
