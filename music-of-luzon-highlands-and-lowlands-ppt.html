3. Explain what are the music in the highlands and lowlands in Luzon.


The music of the Highlands of Luzon(Cordillera)helps us discover the way of life of the cordillera people through themes about nature family life work in the field as well as the spiritual matters. The music for liturgy and devotional music of the lowlands of Luzon reflects the impact of Spanish colonization which lasted for almost three centuries and how the introduction of Christianity affected the religious practices of the etnolonguistic groups.

25. What is the difference between the music highland and lowland luzon


The music of highland Luzon is indigenous music which is produced, sung and playes by the ethnic groups. It is closely related to the cultural traditions of Asia

Meanwhile, lowland Luzon musical form consists mostly of orally transmitted genres and compositions performed by rural communities. They are generally called Philippine Folk Music.
