1. 1. Guest Room Classification.​


Answer

Single room – a room with a single bed, and is sold to only one person. Twin room – a room with two single beds, good for two persons. Double room – a room occupied by two persons with one double bed. Double-double (also twin double) – a room with two double beds or two queen beds, occupied by two or more persons.

26. 24 rooms : 48 guests = X : 58 guests​


Answer:

The answer is :

29 rooms : 58 guests

X = 29

⇨ Solution :

24 : 48 = x : 58

24 × 58 = 1,392

48 × x = 48x

1,392 ÷ 48x = 29

X = 29
