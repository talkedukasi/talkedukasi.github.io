According to Aristotle, possibility is to be understood in relation to necessity: whereas a necessary proposition predicates something of a thing’s essence (as in “All humans are mortal”), a possible proposition predicates something of a thing that is merely accidental (as in “Some humans are tall”). Answer:

Anyone can transcend from his current limitations by changing the way he or she thinks. Proverbs 13:7, As the man thinketh in his heart so is he.

Explanation:

Transcending from your current limitations begin with your mind and your thoughts. Therefore, it is all about making a conscious effort to change the way you think. That is called mind setting.

Mindsets are not just important for learning new skills. They can affect the way that we think about everything.

For example, a growth mindset can help you get an A+ grade from a B- grade. How? By changing the way you think and by changing your beliefs. Because everything begins with thoughts and beliefs. Thoughts become things while beliefs spark motivation.  Cultivating a growth mindset could be the single most important thing you ever do to help you achieve success.

As the famous saying that goes, whether you believe you can or you cannot, you are right.

On a broader perspective, you can transcend from your current limitations by stepping up, changing the way you think and your beliefs about yourself, about your life and your circumstances.

In simpler terms, when it is raining, you do not allow yourself or your mood to get affected or get ruined by the thought that it is raining. Your mood does not depend on anything or anyone. Your mood depends on YOU. Whether people around you are unhappy, you decide to be happy. Happiness is a personal decision and so is misery.  

Make a conscious choice to transcend from your current limitation and you will!
