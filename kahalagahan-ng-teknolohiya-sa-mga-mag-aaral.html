11. mga negatibong epekto ng teknolohiya sa mga mag-aaral​


Answer:

Addiction - Many students can become addicted to technology, spending excessive amounts of time on their devices and neglecting other important aspects of their lives.

Decreased Attention Span - Technology can cause students to have a shorter attention span, making it difficult for them to focus on one task for an extended period of time.

Decreased Physical Activity - Students who spend a lot of time on technology may become more sedentary and less active, which can lead to health problems such as obesity and decreased physical fitness.

Decreased Social Interaction - The overuse of technology can lead to decreased face-to-face social interaction, reducing the opportunity for students to develop important social skills such as communication and teamwork.

Cyberbullying - Technology has made it easier for students to engage in cyberbullying, which can have serious consequences on a victim's mental health and well-being.

Lack of Sleep - Students who spend a lot of time on technology may have trouble sleeping, leading to decreased focus and attention in school.

Decreased Creativity - The overuse of technology can lead to a decrease in creativity as students become more reliant on technology to solve problems and complete tasks.

Decreased Critical Thinking Skills - Students may become too dependent on technology and forget to use critical thinking skills to solve problems and make decisions.

Decreased Ability to Multi-task - Overuse of technology can make it difficult for students to effectively manage multiple tasks at once, reducing their ability to multitask and prioritize effectively.
