23. Ang Dabakan ay tubular drum na pinapalo ng stick o ng palad. Ito ay kabilang sa mga instrumentong. * ￼ a. Aerophones b. Chordophones c. Idiophones d. Membranophones ​


Answer:

letter D . Membranophones

Explanation:

* The drum itself is struck with a drumstick, so it can be classified as a struck membranophone.

* However, since the characteristic sound of the drum is the sound of the snare wires rattling against the drumhead, they could also be considered friction membranophones
