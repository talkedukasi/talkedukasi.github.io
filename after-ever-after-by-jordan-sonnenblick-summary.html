3. Jordan's mom bought 6 packs of juice boxes for the team to drink after practice. Each pack had 15 boxes of juice. After the team drank as much juice as they wanted, there were 23 boxes of juice remaining. How many packs of juice did the team drink?


Step-by-step explanation:

Asked: How many packs of juice did the team drink?

Given: 6 packs of juice boxes with 15 juice boxes inside, 23 remaining juice boxes

Solution:

6 x 15 = 90 // Total number of Juice Boxes

90 - 23 = 67 // Juice Boxes consumed by the Team

67 ÷ 15 = 4 r. 7

Answer: 4 packs and 7 juice boxes

6. Today, Tom is .25 of Jordan's age. In 2 years, Tom will be 1/3 of Jordan's age. How old is Jordan today?


0.25 = 1/4

Present Ages:
Jordan : x
Tom: ¹/₄ x  (1/4 of Jordan's age)

Ages in 2 years:
Jordan: x + 2
Tom: ¹/₄ x + 2

Equation: In 2 years, Tom's ages will be ¹/₃ of Jordan's age

¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ (x + 2)
¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ x + ²/₃

LCD: (3)(4) or 12

12 [¹/₄ x + 2 = ¹/₃ x + ²/₃] 12

3x + 24 = 4x + 8

4x - 3x = 24 - 8

x = 16

ANSWER:  Jordan is 16 years old.

Present Ages:
Jordan: x ⇒ 16 years old
Tom: ¹/₄ x ⇒  ¹/₄ (16) = 4 years old

Ages in 2 years: Tom will be ¹/₃ of Jordan's age
4 + 2 = ¹/₃ (16 + 2)
6 = ¹/₃ (18)
6 = 6   True
