2. 1.How can you formulate the formula F= P(1+r)t to derive or to find the other formulas needed in solving word problem?2.How can you formulate the formula I = Prt to derive or to find the other formulas needed in solving word problem?3.What are the steps that you need in solving for the unknown variable in the word problem?​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in performing mathematical operation and deriving linear equation.

Problem 1Given: F= P(1+r)t

To find the other formulas to solve problem.

To get the formula for P and t

F = P(1+rt), divide both side by (1+r)t

F= P(1+r)t → P = F/(1+r)t

F= P(1+r)t , divide both side by P(1+r)

F= P(1+r)t → t = F/P(1+r)

To get the formula for r

F= P(1+r)t , apply distributive property

F = Pt + prt, apply transposition

F - Pt = prt, divide both side by pt

F= P(1+r)t → r = (F-Pt)/(Pt)

Problem 2Given: I = Prt

To get the fomula for P

I = Prt, divide both side by rt

I = Prt → P = I/rt

To get the formula for r

I = Prt, divide both side by Pt

I = Prt → r = I/Pt

To get the formula for t

I = Prt, divide both side by Pr

I = Prt → t = I/Pr
