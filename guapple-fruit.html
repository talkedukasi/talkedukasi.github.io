1. Mother bought 9 ponkans,8 apples,and 7 guapples.what part of the fruits were the (a) ponkans? (B)apples (C)guapples


9+8+7=24
a. 9÷24=0.375
0.375×100=37.5
a=37.5% of ponkans or 9/24=3/8

b. 8÷24=0.333
0.333×100=33.3
b=33.3% of apples or 8/24=1/3

c. 7÷24=0.291
0.291×100=29.1
b=29.1% of guapples or 7/24
