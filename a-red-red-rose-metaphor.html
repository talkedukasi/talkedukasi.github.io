5. Mother's garden has 80 red and white roses. If 60% of the roses are red,how many are white roses?​


Answer:

32 are white roses

Step-by-step explanation:

P = B × R

P = 80 × 60%

P = 80 × 0.60

P = 48

Now , We will subtract the percentage to the base to find how many white roses are there.

White roses = P - B

White roses = 80 - 48

White roses = 32

Answer: There are 32 white roses.Solution:Identify the unknown and the formula needed

Percentage: unknown (number of red roses)

Base: 80 (total number of roses)

Rate: 60% --- turn to decimal ---> 0.6 (rate of the number of red roses)

Formula for Percentage: Base × Rate

Solve for the unknown

Base × Rate

= 80 × 0.6

= 48 (number of red roses)

Now, subtract the number of red roses to the total number of roses.

80 (total number of roses) - 48 (number of red roses)

80 - 48 = 32 (number of white roses)

Therefore, there are 32 white roses.
