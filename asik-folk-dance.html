Filipino Dances. This is a Tiruray dance for girls, performed in Nuro, Cotabato. According to the natives the word "mag-asik" means to sow seeds. A large kerchief or piece of bright colored cloth about a yard square is spread in the middle of the floor.

Singkil- Is a Mindanao folk dance that originated from the maranao people and is based on the story in the darangen ,The pre-Islamic Maranao Interpretation of the ancient Hindu Indian epic,The ramayana

Kini-Kini-A martial arts dance originating from the Tausug people of the sulu archipelago in the southwestern part of the Philippines .Male dancers depict a fight between a cat and hawk. Pangalay-Is a traditional "fingernail" dance of the Tausūg people of the Sulu Archipelago and eastern Coast Bajau of Sabah

Asik-Asik is a dance from Maguindanao Tribe of Mindanao. There is much debate about the popular belief that the Asik is a dance performed by a slave girl to win the favor of her sultan master.
