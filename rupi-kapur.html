How many permutations can be made from the word KAPUR-PURAWAN?

Anagram: It defines to the amount of letter that corresponds to the word.

K = 1 letterA = 3 lettersP = 2 lettersU = 2 lettersR = 2 lettersW = 1 letterN = 1
