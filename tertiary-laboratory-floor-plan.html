29. Maintenance and repair plan for school computer laboratory. Overview: Task​


Answer:

Regular Cleaning including hardware and software, System Backups, and Staff Training

Explanation:

Regular Cleaning: Establish a schedule for regular cleaning of the computer laboratory. This includes dusting keyboards, monitors, and CPU units, as well as ensuring proper ventilation to prevent overheating.

Software Updates: Regularly update the operating systems, antivirus software, and other essential programs on the computers to ensure security and optimal performance. Set up automatic updates whenever possible.

Hardware Maintenance: Conduct routine inspections of hardware components such as cables, connectors, and peripherals. Replace any damaged or malfunctioning equipment promptly. Keep an inventory of spare parts for quick replacements when needed.


System Backups: Implement a regular backup system for important files and data stored in the computer laboratory. This ensures that in the event of a hardware failure or data loss, valuable information can be easily recovered.

Staff Training: Provide training sessions for staff members responsible for the computer laboratory's maintenance and repair. This ensures they have the necessary skills and knowledge to address common issues and perform basic troubleshooting.
