4. what is the factor of 4m² - 4m²​


Answer:

2m²-2m² is my answer but I'm not sure

The answer will be (2m+2m) (2m-2m) OR (2m-2m) (2m+2m).

This binomial is known as "Difference of Two Squares".

In order to get this result, follow the instructions below:

1. Find the square root of every term. Since the terms are just the same, they must have the same root.

4m² = 2m

2. Place them in two parentheses. Write the square root of the two terms in the first parenthesis, where its second term is in positive form (+), while the other one must have a negative form (-), and vice versa.

(2m+2m) (2m-2m)

OR

(2m-2m) (2m+2m)
