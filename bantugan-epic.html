GOOD PRINCE BANTUGAN

Bantugan is a hero from a local literature called Darangen. He is a praised warrior of the Maranoa Tribe. The tribe is an ethnolinguistic group in the Lanao province. Lanao is believed to have been found by the lake.

According to the Legend, Bantugan is able to fly high into the heavens, he can walk on water and breathe under but for a limited time. He possess an energy to posses and /anito/ or soul and spirits that belongs to nature.

Bantugën’s desired mode of transportation is a massive timber ship, that could convey an entire city of warriors. His maximum prized ownership is the kampilan, a single-edged sword awesome for its tapered blade that is wide and skinny on the end. It every now and then has a spikelet on the tip.

MAIN CHARACTERS OF DARANGEN EPIC

1. Tominaman sa Rogong and Lalawanen sa Solog

Parents of Prince Bantugan and rulers of Bembaran

2. Alcat

Sister of Prince Bantugan

STRUCTURE OF THE DARANGEN EPIC

Darangen has 17 cycles. Each of the cycle revolves around him but has different story far from each cycle. A few of which is a story of his siblings. There are 72.000 lines. The folk epic happened before Islam and Christianism even arrive. Thus, this epic is very treasured as this reflects on the ancient beliefs and lifestyle of Filipino native ancestors. It is also a proof that culture is well and alive before the age of exploration and colonisation. Like most of the epics, stories are conveyed through chanting or through the mouth--story tellings, songs, poems, and drawings.

The popular Ilocano epic : Biag ni Lam-ang has 1477 lines which is even shorter than Darangen.

The elements of an epic consists of the following:

The plot is about the tales of a heroThere is a vast setting that involves an extenuating time and moving locationsDisplays of bravery and supernatural abilitiesStories of talking non-living things or animalsSUMMARY OF DARANGEN

Prince Bantugan is the second son of the rulers of Bembaran, Tominaman of Rogong and Lalawanen of Solog. His obligation is to guard the people and not necessarily rule them over. He has 12 siblings and each of them contains a story and adventures of their own.

During the epic stories, an attempt for Spaniard colonization was also featured.
