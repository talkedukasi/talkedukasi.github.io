1. 1. ice-candy business​


Answer:

Starting an ice candy business can be a great way to make some extra income and enjoy the summer season. This type of business requires minimal start-up costs, and can be a fun way to engage with customers and build relationships in the community.

When starting an ice candy business, it is important to have the necessary supplies. This includes containers for the ice candy, a freezer to store it, and a sign to advertise the business. It is also important to have a business plan in place, which should include research on the local market and competitors, as well as your target customers and pricing strategy.

Promotion is key to the success of an ice candy business. You can promote your business through word of mouth, flyers, or social media. As you gain more customers and build relationships, you can expand the business by offering other products and services

23. is ice candy a homogeneous?


Answer:

yes

Ice cream is said to be a homogeneous mixture when it is all the same throughout. That is, nothing has been added to it which is not mixed into it so there are no parts which are different.

For example, if you make "plain" ice cream without, for instance, strawberries, it could still be called "homogeneous," but if you added strawberries which had each been cut in half twice, you would have ice cream with lumps and the mixture would not be "homogeneous."

However, if you put the whole mixture in a blender and chopped the lumps up until the strawberries were in tiny pieces, so it was smooth and blended then the mixture would be "homogeneous." - - Stew
