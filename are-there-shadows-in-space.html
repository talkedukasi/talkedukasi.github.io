3. what couses of shadow to oppear.Are shadow present in space ?yes or no. why?​


Answer:

what couses of shadow to oppear?

Shadows are made by blocking light. Light rays travel from a source in straight lines. If an opaque (solid) object gets in the way, it stops light rays from traveling through it. This results in an area of darkness appearing behind the object.

Are shadow present in space?

Like all worlds orbiting a sun, Earth casts a shadow. Earth's shadow extends about 870,000 miles (1.4 million km) into space. You might not realize it, but, from Earth's surface, you can see the shadow.

Answer:

D. celestial horizon

Explanation:

Gazing up, you get the impression that the sky is a great hollow dome with you at the center (Figure 1), and all the stars are an equal distance from you on the surface of the dome. The top of that dome, the point directly above your head, is called the zenith, and where the dome meets Earth is called the horizon.

29. the dark solid conical shadow that thins to a point far into space​


Answer:

UMBRA

Explanation:

The shadows of Earth and the Moon consist of two parts: a cone where the shadow is darkest, called the umbra, and a lighter, more diffuse region of darkness called the penumbra. As you can imagine, the most spectacular eclipses occur when an object enters the umbra.
