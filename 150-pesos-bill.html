2. Amy paid 1,000 pesos for the telephone bill this month.This is based on an 4 pesos per minute call plus basic charge.If her bill includes 150 minutes worth of call,find the basic charge.


400 is the answer

Given:

Amy paid 1,000 pesos for the telephone bill

4 pesos per minute

150 minutes worth of call

Basic Charge = ?

SOLUTION:

1000 - (4 * 150) = ?

1000 - (600) = ?

400 is the cost of basic charge

Total Cost: 1,000 pesos

Cost per Minute on top of basic charge: 4 pesos/minute

Total minutes used: 150 minutes

Basic charge: y

1,000 = 4(150) + y

y = 1,000 - 600

y = 400

ANSWER: 400 pesos

The function of monthly telephone charges, when x = number of minutes used:

f(x)  = 4x + 400

Check:

when x = 150

f(150) = 4(150) + 400

f(150) = 600 + 400

f(150) = 1,000
