18. Example of topic shifting communicative strategy


Topic-shifting communicative strategy is a communication strategy that is carried out by subtly changing the topic of discussion to other topics that still have a relationship or may not be at all. This aims to explain another topic related to the previous one or change the topic to avoid long pauses when no one is speaking or commenting.

Example of Topic shifting communicative strategy:

When explaining the topic of "go green" the speaker explained that one of the steps that can be taken is implementing zero waste.

The speaker wanted to explain zero waste and said, "By the way, I mentioned the term zero waste before, for some people this term may not be common. So here we go, zero waste is ..." Or the speaker could also say. "We heard the term zero waste before, zero waste is..."

The markers of topic shifting are "By the way" and "We heard the term zero waste before,".

Besides that, we can also use other conversation scenarios for topic shifting, such as:

"Do you realize..."

"Do you remember..."

"What I'm trying to say is..."
