13. What type of propaganda technique is used in Crocs?​


Answer:

Bandwagon, Testimonial, and Plain Folks

Explanation:

The Bandwagon Technique suggests that everyone is using or supporting the product, and you should too, to fit in with the crowd.

The Testimonial Technique uses famous or influential people to endorse the product, creating a positive association.

Finally, the Plain Folks technique shows ordinary people using the product, making it seem relatable and accessible.
