2. Halimbawa ng haypotesis


Answer:

Usually, you don't want to state a hypothesis as a question. You believe in something, and you're seeking to prove it.

For example:

If I raise the temperature of a cup of water, then the amount of sugar that can be dissolved in it will be increased.

6. Halimbawa ng null na haypotesis sa konseptong papel?


Ang null hypothesis ay isang uri ng hypothesis na naghahayag na walang significant o makabuluhang kaibahan sa [agitan ng dalawang populasyon na pinaghahambing. Ang pagtanggap sa null hypothesis ay batay sa naging resulta ng ginawang pag eeksperimento o istatistikang ginawa sa mga variables.

Halimbawa:

Null hypothesis: There is no significant difference between the sample mean of two groups.
