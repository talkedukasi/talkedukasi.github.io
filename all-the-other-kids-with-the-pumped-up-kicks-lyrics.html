Answer:

Screw pumps are used in lube and fuel oil service. On medium speed engines the main lube oil pump is often mounted on the engine and direct driven, whereas the stand-by and pre-lube can be electrically driven. Entrained air in the lube oil can cause pump vibrations if not properly addressed in the pump selection.
