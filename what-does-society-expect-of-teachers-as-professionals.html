18. . What attributes are expected of a professional teacher in his relationship with the Higher Authorities?​


Answer:

When a professional teacher interacts with higher authorities, there are certain attributes expected. They are the following:

Respect: Someone is an authority for a reason, so it is good to acknowledge that they know something more than you. This can be done by taking their requests or suggestions seriously.Punctuality: This is also one way to show respect. A professional teacher is going to show up on time to display that they value the time of others. When meeting higher authorities, punctuality is very important.Honesty: A professional teacher should be honest when dealing with higher authorities. There is no room for lies and misinformation when speaking to someone with more power.Obedience: If you are a professional teacher, it is expected that you will follow the rules set by the authorities. Likewise, you will also be expected to enforce them upon your students.
