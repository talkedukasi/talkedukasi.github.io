14. sandra is 18 years older than paolo. in 13 years, sandra will be twice as old as paolo will be the. how old is sandra now?


First, we should assign variables to the given facts. Let:

 

Sandra’s Age = X + 18

Paolo’s Age = X

 

In 13 years, they will be:

 

(X + 18) + 13 = 2(x + 13)

 

X + 18 + 13 = 2x + 26

X + 31 = 2x + 26

X – 2x = 26 - 31

-x/-1 = -5/-1

X = 5

 

Therefore, Sandra is now:

 

Sandra’s Age = 5 + 18 = 23

 

Sandra is now 23 year’s old.

 

Checking their age:

 

23 (Sandra) – 5 (Paolo) = 18
