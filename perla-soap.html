6. Working together, Jack and Perla can finish polishing a furniture in 12 days. Jack can do 2 times as much as Perla can. How long will it take Jack to finish the job alone? How about Perla?​ANSWER​


Answer:

Jack can finish the job alone in 18 Days and Perla can finish the job alone in 36 Days

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's say Perla can finish the job alone in x days. This means that in one day, she can complete 1/x of the job.

Since we know that Jack can do 2 times as much as Perla can, he can finish the job alone in x/2 days. In one day, he can complete 1/(x/2) or 2/x of the job.

Together, in one day, they can complete 1/x + 2/x of the job, which is 3/x of the job.

Since they can finish the job in 12 days, this means that in one day, they can complete 1/12 of the job.

Equating the two expressions:

3/x = 1/12

Solving for x:

x = 36

This means that Perla can finish the job alone in 36 Days.

To find out how long it will take Jack to finish the job alone, we can use the equation x/2 = 18. Therefore, Jack can finish the job alone in 18 Days
