2. what is kilos loob in english?


Answer:

The English words of "Kilos loob" may either refer to: Will, Free will or, in Psychology, Internal Action. But mostly, it may refer to Internal Action.

Explanation:

The literal translation of "Kilos loob" could be "actions within". Simply put, "Kilos" in English is "action" while, In Filipino Psychology, loob or kalooban refers to the inner self of a person, its literal English translation is "inside". When considering this, "actions within" may refer to a thought or thinking process of a person's mind. This is what is called an Internal Action. The execution of Internal Action is called External Action.

In Tagalog, Internal Action and External Action is called "Kilos" at "Kilos Loob", as seen on this link: https://brainly.ph/question/673797
