21. what aspect in the movie finding nemo​


"Finding Nemo" is an animated movie about a clownfish named Marlin who sets out to find his son Nemo, who has been taken by scuba divers and placed in an aquarium in a dentist's office. The movie explores many aspects of life in the ocean and themes related to family, friendship, and perseverance.

One aspect of the movie that stands out is the importance of family. Marlin is fiercely protective of his son Nemo, and he goes to great lengths to find him when he is taken. Throughout the movie, Marlin and Nemo both learn the importance of trusting each other and working together to overcome obstacles.

Another aspect of the movie is the importance of overcoming fear. Marlin is initially very hesitant to let his son explore the ocean and is fearful of many things that he encounters along the way. However, as the movie progresses, Marlin learns to overcome his fears and becomes a more confident and adventurous fish.

Finally, the movie also explores the importance of friendship and the power of working together. Marlin and Nemo both make friends along the way, such as the forgetful fish Dory, and they are able to accomplish things that they could not have done alone. This aspect of the movie highlights the importance of working together and supporting each other to achieve common goals.

"Finding Nemo" is a heartwarming animated movie that tells the story of a young clownfish named Nemo who gets separated from his father, Marlin, and taken to Sydney, Australia. The movie explores themes of family, friendship, and perseverance as Marlin embarks on a dangerous journey to find his son and encounters a variety of colorful characters along the way.

One of the key aspects of the movie is the bond between parent and child. Marlin is a devoted father who will stop at nothing to find his son, even though he is timid and cautious by nature. Through his journey, Marlin learns to trust his instincts and take risks in order to protect those he loves. The movie also highlights the importance of friendship, as Marlin forms a close bond with a forgetful fish named Dory who helps him navigate the challenges of the ocean.

Overall, "Finding Nemo" is a heartwarming story with relatable characters and important themes that resonate with audiences of all ages.

29. summary ng nemo and batang papel


Ang istorya na pinamagatang Si Nemo, ang batang papel sa papel, ay tungkol sa isang batang papel mula sa pinunit, ginupit, at panagdikit-dikit na diyaryo na naghangad na maging totoong bata. Hiniling niya sa malayong bituin nang mataimtim na sana ay maging totoong bata siya. Natupad ang kaniyang hiling sa isang iglap. Napadpad siya sa piling ng kaniyang totoong mga magulang at mga kapatid. 

Ngunit, ang kaniyang buhay ng pamilyang napadparan niya ay hindi masagana at maginhawa. Siya at ang kaniyang mga kapatid ay dapat na magtrabaho at magsikap araw araw para sa kanilang ikabubuhay. Araw-araw na pagod ang katawan ni Nemo, at naisip niya na sumilip sa dati niyang pinanggalingan.

Nang mapunta siya sa eskwelahan ay pinandirihan lamang siya ng mga libro, at nang mapunta siya sa dagat upang magmuni-muni ay pinagtakpan lamang siya ng alon ng ilong dahil sa kanyang hindi kaaya-ayang amoy.

Naglalakad-lakad muli si Nemo at nakakita siya ng iba pang mga bata na nasa kalye. Ang mga batang ito ay nakararanas ng parehos pighati katulad niya. Kinausap niya ang mga ito at sinabing upang makamit nila ang kanilang mga hiling ay dapat na taimtim silang manalangin sa pinakamalayong bituin.

Pinikit ng mga bata ang kanilang mga mata at sabay-sabay na humiling na sila ay maging maligaya. Sa isang iglap, ang lahat ng mga bata ay naging batang papel. Sila ay naging masaya sapagkat hindi na nila kailangan pang makibaka at mamuhay sa kalsada.
