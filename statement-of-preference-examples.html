4. It is common practice to say "please" and "thank you" when making a request or expressing gratitude.

5. Many businesses operate from 9am to 5pm, Monday through Friday.

Statement of Fact:

1. The Earth orbits around the Sun.

2. Water freezes at 0 degrees Celsius.

3. The capital of France is Paris.

4. The human body has 206 bones.

5. The Great Wall of China is the longest wall in the world.

Statement of Opinion:

1. Chocolate ice cream is the best flavor.

2. Soccer is more exciting than baseball.

3. The color blue is the prettiest color.

4. The Beatles are the greatest band of all time.

5. Learning a second language is important for personal growth.

Statement of Preference:

1. I prefer coffee over tea.

2. I like action movies more than romantic comedies.

3. I enjoy listening to classical music more than pop music.

4. I would rather stay home and read a book than go out to a party.

5. I find hiking to be more enjoyable than going to the gym.

Statement of Convention:

5. It is a social norm to say "please" and "thank you" when requesting or receiving something.

2. Water boils at 100°C under normal atmospheric pressure.
3. The Great Wall of China is the longest wall in the world.
4. The human body has 206 bones.
5. The Titanic sank in 1912 after hitting an iceberg.

Statement of Opinion:

2. I think that the new movie release is worth watching.
3. Mobile phones have made our lives easier, in my view.
4. I believe that exercise is good for both physical and mental health.
5. In my opinion, the government should provide free healthcare to its citizens.

Statement of Preference:
1. I like to wake up early in the morning and have a cup of coffee.
2. I prefer eating spicy food over bland food.
3. My favorite color is blue.
4. I enjoy listening to classical music more than pop music.
5. My favorite restaurant is the one that serves Italian cuisine.
