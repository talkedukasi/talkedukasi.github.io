20. what are the similarities of ancient era, classical era and madieval era?​


Answer:

‘Ancient’ refers to any time prior to the collapse of the Western Roman Empire in the 5th century AD; sometimes referred to as ‘antiquity’.

‘Mediaeval’ literally means ‘middle ages’ and refers to a period of European history between the collapse of the Western Roman Empire and the onset of the Age of Exploration.

‘Classical’ in this context is a period of ancient history lasting roughly from the 8th century BC to the Early Middle Ages. The term is used most often when discussing Greco-Roman history, mostly because that was the dominant civilisation in Europe throughout this time period.
