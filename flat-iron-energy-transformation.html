19. Identify the energy transformation of the following appliance 1) Rice cooker - 2) Flat Iron- 3) lamp shade- 4) own toaster- 5) Rodio-​


1.)Rice cooker-Essentially, the rice cooker does what other cooking instruments in your kitchen do: it uses electric energy and converts it to heat to break molecular links in food, making it easier to digest for humans.

2.)Flat Iron-A flat iron, an electric toaster and a stove all convert electrical energy into thermal energy.

3.)lamp shade-a lamp converts electrical energy into heat and light energy.

4.)toaster-The toaster consist of the heating element. When electrical energy passes through this heating element, it converts this electrical energy to heat energy which helps to prepare toast (bread that turns brown on exposure to heat). Hence electrical energy is transformed to heat energy in a electric toaster.

5.) Radio-In radio, The electrical energy is converted into sound energy.
