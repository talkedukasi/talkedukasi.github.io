3. state the origin of the mag asik


Filipino Dances. This is a Tiruray dance for girls, performed in Nuro, Cotabato. According to the natives the word "mag-asik" means to sow seeds. A large kerchief or piece of bright colored cloth about a yard square is spread in the middle of the floor.
