It is a type of Propaganda Technique, which the writer

3. TESTIMONIAL- It endorses an idea or a product by a famous person.

It is a type of Propaganda Technique, which the writer uses

some respected person or a famous celebrity to become the

image of the idea or product.

4. PLAIN FOLKS - It identifies a product or an idea within

 a locality or country.

It is a type of Propaganda Technique, which the writer

trying to convince the audience that the idea/ product

is good for everyone else since they all both belong

in the majority of the group in one place.

5. CARD STACKING- It manipulates information so that

one product looks better than the other.

It is a type of Propaganda Technique, which the writer

creates a side of the information in a deceiving manner

17. Ang _________ ay mayroong directa positibong ugnayan Ang presyo SA quantity supplied Ng Isang produkto​


Answer:

Ang Batas ng Supply ay mayroong directa positibong ugnayan Ang presyo SA quantity supplied Ng Isang produkto​

Explanation:

- Ang supply ay tumutukoy sa dami ng mga produktong handing ibenta ng mga negosyante sa isang takdang panahon. Kung dumami ang supply ng produkto, asahan ang pagbaba ng demand at presyo nito.

- Kapag mataas ang presyo ng isang bilihin, maipagbibili ang produkto sa tamang oras. Kapag mas mababa ang presyo ng isang bilihin, nababawasan din ang dami nito.
