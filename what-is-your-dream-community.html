My Dream Community should contain these things:

A good leadership Responsible people (and not ignorant) Selfless/ helpful people Have a nice continuing growth Doesn’t settle for “that is enough for now”

Explanation:

Good leadership requires a good leader, if not good at first then he/she is open for opinions and learning not just from people with the same standing as him/her but also those people that voice is hidden. There is a term “bayanihan”, people who are willing to help others without expecting something in return.  Wants to grow more and more, we strive for a consistent action. A Community that wants to be one of those well known  cities or places in the country. Doesn’t settle with “that will do’ or “that’s good for now”. Our community is like rising and falling waves of an ocean, nothing is for certain whether something will happen or not.
