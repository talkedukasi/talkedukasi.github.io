18. body exercise1.body twist2.leg spilts​


Answer:

Body Twist - This exercise involves rotating your torso to the left and right while keeping your hips and legs stable. It's often performed while standing or sitting, with your arms extended in front of your body or holding a weight.

Leg Splits - This exercise involves stretching your legs in opposite directions, with one leg in front and one leg behind you, until you feel a stretch in your inner thighs and hips. It's often used in dance and gymnastics training and requires flexibility in the legs and hips. There are different variations of leg splits, including side splits and front splits.
