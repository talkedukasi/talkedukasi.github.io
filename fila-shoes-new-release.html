20. 1. The price of a pair of shoes has increased by 15%. The original price of theshoes was Php150. What is the new price?​


Answer:

P177.5

Step-by-step explanation:

Get the amount of increase.

150 × 0.15 = 22.5

Add the increase to the original amount.

150 + 22.5 = 177.5

Answer:

Php 172.50

Step-by-step explanation:

MARKUP :In order to get the new sale price, you have to follow the formula in the given below

[tex]M__{up} = (SP)(R)[/tex]

Wherein,

SP = Sale Price

R = Rate of Markup ( usually follows with a percentage)

[tex]M_{up} =(150)(0.15)[/tex]

[tex]M_{up} =22.5[/tex]

Then the second formula

[tex]SP_{n} =M_{up} + SP[/tex]

[tex]SP = 22.50 + 150.00\\SP = Php\ 172.50[/tex]
