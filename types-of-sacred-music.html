Mass

Is a sacrament of the Eucharist mostly celebrated by the Roman Catholic Church

Pastores

Is a Christmas season folk dance and song

Pabasang Pasyon

a verse narrative on the life and sufferings of Jesus Christ.

Senakulo

a Lenten play that describes events from the Old and New Testaments related to the life, suffering and Death of Christ.

Salubong

An Eastern Sunday pre-dawn ritual that reenacts the Risen Christ's meeting with His mother.

Flores de Mayo

Celebrated in honor of the Virgin Mary.

Santacruzan

Devotion of honoring St. Helena and Emperor Constantine finding the true Cross in Jerusalem
