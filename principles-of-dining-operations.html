5. 2 A dining table has a volume of 112 500 cm³. If its length is 100 cm 75 cm, what is its height? Asked in the problem: Operation/s Number Sentence Solution​


Asked in the Problem: The height of the dining table.

Given Facts: 112,500 cm³ volume, 100 cm length and 75 cm width

Operation/s: Multiplication, Division

Number Sentence: 100 × 75 = N

112,500 ÷ N = N

Solution: 100 × 75 = 7,500

112,500 ÷ 7,500 = 15

10. With the food service establishments that you have dined at before, reflect on the strategies they adopted to be able to push through with their operations amid different circumstances and different markets. Write down a narrative differentiating their strategies.. Don't forget to mention the food service establishments that you have dined at.​


One strategy that many food service establishments have adopted is to offer delivery and takeout options. This strategy became especially important during the COVID-19 pandemic, as many people were hesitant to dine in at restaurants due to health and safety concerns. Restaurants and other food service establishments that already had delivery and takeout options in place were able to pivot quickly to meet the increased demand for these services. Other establishments that did not have delivery and takeout options prior to the pandemic had to quickly develop these services in order to stay in business.

Another strategy that some food service establishments have adopted is to diversify their offerings. For example, some restaurants may have added grocery items to their menu, such as bread, produce, and pantry staples, in order to appeal to customers who were doing more cooking at home during the pandemic. Others may have started offering meal kits or cooking classes to provide an additional revenue stream.

In some cases, food service establishments have also adjusted their menus to reflect changing consumer preferences. For example, during the pandemic, many people were looking for comfort food and dishes that reminded them of home. Some restaurants responded by adding more comfort food items to their menus, such as mac and cheese, meatloaf, and pot pies.

In conclusion, food service establishments have adopted a variety of strategies to push through with their operations amid different circumstances and markets. These strategies include offering delivery and takeout options, diversifying their offerings, and adjusting their menus to reflect changing consumer preferences.

Accessibility - making the restaurant easy to navigate for customers of all abilities.


Comfort - providing comfortable seating and a pleasant atmosphere to make customers feel relaxed and at home.


Functionality - arranging tables and chairs in a way that maximizes the use of space and allows for efficient service.


B. Not implemented design principles:


Sustainability - incorporating eco-friendly practices such as composting, recycling, and reducing food waste.


Universal design - creating a space that is inclusive and accessible for people of all ages and abilities.


Multi-sensory design - engaging all the senses, such as using music or art to enhance the dining experience.
