21. 1.) Conan has two numbers. He gives two clues about his numbers:Clue #1: The larger number is 10 more than the smaller number.Clue #2: Three times the larger number is five times the smaller numbeWhat are Conan's two numbers?​


Answer:

The two numbers are 15 and 25.

Step-by-step explanation:

Clue #1:  The larger number is 10 more than the smaller number.

Let:

Smaller number :  xLarger number: x + 10

Clue #2: Three times the larger number is five times the smaller number.

3(larger number) = 5(smaller number)

Equation:

3(x + 10) = 5(x)

Find x, the smaller number:

3(x + 10) = 5(x)

3x + 30 = 5x

5x - 3x = 30

2x = 30

2x/2 = 30/2

x = 15

When x = 15, find the larger number:

x + 10  ⇒  (15) + 10 = 25

The two numbers are 15 and 25.

Conan O'Brien Needs a Friend premiered on November 18, 2018, when O'Brien's talk show was on hiatus and being retooled from an hour-long format into a half-hour format. O'Brien announced, "After 25 years of extensive market research, we have learned that people want to hear my voice without seeing my face. So rejoice, America." The first 36 episodes of the podcast were packaged together as one season. A second season consisting of another 36 episodes premiered on October 7, 2019,  after a run of six special episodes titled Deep Dive with Dana Carvey. A third season premiered on August 3, 2020.
