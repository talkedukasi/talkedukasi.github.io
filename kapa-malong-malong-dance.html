19. A TYPE OF TRADITIONAL DANCE WEARING MALONG


Answer:

Malongs are traditional clothes that are woven with intricate patterns using cotton and thread. These Malongs are usually worn by the Muslims. It can be worn by both men and women.

Most often they use these in traditions and festivities but they also use it in performing a traditional dance called Kapa Malong-malong or sometimes just called Malong-malong. Malong-malong uses the traditional cloth in many ways. It can be worn by female dancers as a skirt, as a shawl, or as a headpiece. It can also be worn by the male as sashes or waistbands.

The intricate patterns add element to the overall performance of the dance.

25. 8. This type of malong features the langkit, which are strips with hand-sewn geometric designs. A. malong a andon B. malong a landap C. malong a ampik D. malong a patola​


Answer:

B. malong a landap

Explanation:

The "malong a landap" is characterized by "langkit", or decorative strips of profuse geometric designs, hand-sewn on the malong. This type is considered the most popular. The two narrow "langkit" are called "tobiran" while the wider band is called the "lakban".
