21. Is a travel agency a common carrier?


Answer:

No, as a general rule. A travel agency is not an entity engaged in the business of transporting either passengers or goods. Its covenant with its customers is simply to make travel arrangements in their behalf. Its services include procuring tickets and facilitating travel permits or visas as well as booking customers for tours.

With the interest of securing their money spent in the pre-paid travels, now a days, clients would always opt for travel insurance. Travel agencies with travel insurance would usually get more clients considering that their travel packages along with the insurance are more secured and beneficial at their end . Clients will be reimbursed with their travel investment if a trip is interrupted or canceled

With a good travel insurance, every traveler is medically and financially secured during the travel.
