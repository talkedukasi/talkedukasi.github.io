1. Symbolism: The mountain is a symbol of strength and power, and its voice is a metaphor for the power of nature.

2. Imagery: The vivid descriptions of the mountain, its surroundings, and the characters evoke a strong sense of place and atmosphere.

3. Metaphor: The mountain is described as a living being with its own voice, which speaks to the characters in their dreams.

B. The literary approach used in this story helps to build up the plot by creating an atmosphere of mystery and suspense.
