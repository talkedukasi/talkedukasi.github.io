2. Unified Plot and Episodic Plot​


Answer:

Unified: one main character, takes place in a single place of time. Episodic: central characters take place in a long period of time. exposition, rising action, climax, falling action , resolution. ... the part of of a dramatic plot that leads through a series of events increasing interest to the climax.

Explanation:

• Unified Plot. sequence of events in a story or play as the author chooses to arrange them. These events bring about the solution to the conflict or problem. Exposition.

• The Episodic plot structure is made up of a series of chapters or stories linked together by the same character, place, or theme but held apart by their individual plot, purpose, and subtext. A Parallel Plot: The writer weaves two or more dramatic plots that are usually linked by a common character and a similar theme.
