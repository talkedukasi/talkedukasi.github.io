1. Maya visits her grandmother every 3 days while her cousin Laya visits their grandmother every saturday. If both Maya and Laya visit their grandmother on november 5 which is a saturday, when would they visit their grandmother on the same day again.​


Answer:

The answer is November 12

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us just find what is ask in the problem and start analyzing the data given.

Maya will visit her grandmother every 3 days.

Laya will visit her grandmother every Saturday.

Maya and Laya decided to visit their grandmother together on Saturday November 5 . The question is, when would they visit their grandmother on the same day again?

if November 5 is Saturday the next day will be Sunday November 6 and following days November 7,8,9,10,11 marked as Monday to Friday therefore November 12 is Saturday and this will be the next visiting day of Maya and Laya for their grandmother.

Take note: The given date is just an assumption.
