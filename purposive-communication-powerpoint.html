19. What is the purpose of embedding an Excel chart in a PowerPoint presentation?


When you embed an Excel chart in PowerPoint, any updates you make to the original Excel chart will automatically update in your presentation, as long as the files remain in the same location. This helps the data stay in sync, so you won't have incorrect or out-of-date information in your chart.
