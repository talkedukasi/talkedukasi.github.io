Answer:

I disagree with Bamby's opinion that she does not need to write a business plan to purchase her uncle's computer shop. A business plan is an important document that outlines the goals, strategies, and financial projections of a business. It provides a clear and detailed overview of the business, its operations, and its financial performance.

In the case of purchasing an existing business, a business plan can help Bamby to understand the current state of the business, identify potential challenges or opportunities, and develop a plan for success. It can also provide valuable information to potential investors or lenders, who may require a business plan before providing funding for the business.

Additionally, a business plan can serve as a valuable tool for managing and growing the business once it is purchased. It can help Bamby to set clear goals and objectives, develop strategies for achieving them, and track the business's progress over time.

Lastly, I believe that writing a business plan is essential for anyone who is considering purchasing an existing business, including Bamby's uncle's computer shop. It can provide valuable insights and guidance, and help to ensure the success and sustainability of the business.
