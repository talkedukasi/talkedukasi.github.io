7. In what way do the hilot and albularyo help people in isolated areas with no health professionals?


Hilot and Albularyo help people in isolated areas with no health professionals by using traditional medicine. Most of the hilot and albularyo in the provinces learned traditional medicine from their ancestors. They usually prescribe herbal medicine and other non-Western practices such as tambal and paghilot. While some of these practices are not recommended by Western medicine, they serve as help for people with no access to health professionals. For example, someone who has been bitten by a dog will typically go to an albularyo first.
