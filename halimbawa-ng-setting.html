Answer:

Let us say, there are three sets named as A, B and C. The elements of all sets A, B and C is defined as;

A={1,3,6,8}

B={2,3,4,5}

C={5,8,9}

Find the universal set for all the three sets A, B and C.

Answer: By the definition we know, the universal set includes all the elements of the given sets. Therefore, Universal set for sets A, B and C will be,

U={1,2,3,4,5,6,8,9}

Explanation: From the above example, we can see that the elements of sets A, B and C are altogether available in Universal set ‘U’. Also, if you observe, no elements in the universal set are repeated and all the elements are unique.

Note: If Universal set contains Sets A, B and C, then these sets are also called subsets of Universal set. Denoted by;

A ⊂ U (A subset of U)

B ⊂ U (B subset of U)

C ⊂ U (C subset of U)

Paghahati Gamit Ang Repeated Subtraction

Ang paghahati ay ang paraan ng pamamahagi ng isang pangkat ng mga bagay sa pantay-pantay na sukat o bilang. Sa Ingles, ito ay kilala na division. Isa sa mga paraan upang makuha ang sagot sa paghahati o division ay ang repeated subtraction. Ito naman ang paraan ng pagbabawas ng pantay na bilang o numero mula sa isang mas malaking bilang o numero.

Para sa gawain sa itaas, kailangan mong ibigay ang repeated subtraction at division equation upang makita ang paghahati. Gamitin ang binigay na halimbawa bilang gabay.

1. Ibinahagi ang 25 na mangga sa 5 na bata.

Repeated Subtraction:

25 - 5 = 20

20 - 5 = 15

15 - 5 = 10

10 - 5 = 5

5 - 5 = 0

Division Equation:

25 ÷ 5 = 5

2. May 16 na lapis at tig-apat sa bawat pangkat o set.

Repeated Subtraction:

16 - 4 = 12

12 - 4 = 8

8 - 4 = 4

4 - 4 = 0

Division Equation:

16 ÷ 4 = 4

3. May 24 na pinyang ipinamigay sa 6 na pamilya.

Repeated Subtraction:

24 - 4 = 20

20 - 4 = 16

16 - 4 = 12

12 - 4 = 8

8 - 4 = 4

4 - 4 = 0

Division Equation:

24 ÷ 6 = 4

4. Ibinahagi ang 12 na lata ng sardinas sa 6 pamilya.

Repeated Subtraction:

12 - 2 = 10

10 - 2 = 8

8 - 2 = 6

6 - 2 = 4

4 - 2 = 2

2 - 2 = 0

Division Equation:

12 ÷ 6 = 2

5. Pinaghatian ng 2 magkapatid ang 10 sagutang papel.

Repeated Subtraction:

10 - 5 = 5

5 - 5 = 0

Division Equation:

10 ÷ 2 = 5
