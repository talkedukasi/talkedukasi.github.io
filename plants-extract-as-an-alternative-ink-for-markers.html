15. How to separate plant extract


first plant powder were extracted with 90% methanol in a Soxhlet apparatus until exhaustion (tested with Mayer’s reagent). The alcoholic extract of the plant was concentrated under vacuum until dryness. The dried extract was dissolved in 2 N-hydrochloric acid on a water bath, shaken and filtered. The obtained filtrate was extracted with chloroform to remove undesirable matters. The acidic aqueous layer was adjusted to alkaline pH with ammonia and the liberated alkaloid bases were extracted by chloroform till exhausted and then tested by Mayer’s, Dragendorff’s and Wagner’s reagents. then bu using TLC with different solvent system followed by preparative TLC help you to isolate the major alkaloidal compounds from the crude extract
