He took it and began talking with her, at first he came out as a over excited man as he calls her nicknames. She rebuts to him and he quickly straighten his way of speaking. They both were engrossed on speaking of matters from being a rick kind and its typical advantages to someone.

The young woman insists that she wants to get out of that kind of lifestyle and pursue a guy plain enough. The young man therefore shares that he is a plain guy working in a restaurant just across the street.

The woman then hurries to leave; he was left there on the park bench. Then, he starts to follow vision of the young woman till he finally saw her destination. She went inside the backdoor of a restaurant and came out wearing the same clothes as those inside. The woman then takes her place on the cashier. After that he proceeds to an automobile and demanded the Chauffeur to bring him to a club.
