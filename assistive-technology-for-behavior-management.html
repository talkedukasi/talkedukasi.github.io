29. 2. Manpower Example: In the production of pandesal, manpower resources include the baker, and his assistants who will implement the recipe using the available equipment, and technology. The manager, sales clerks, and janitors are also part of the manpower of the bakery.​


Answer:

Yes, in the production of pandesal or any other bakery product, manpower resources play a crucial role. Here's an example of manpower resources involved in the production of pandesal:

1. Baker: The baker is the key individual responsible for preparing the pandesal dough, shaping it into rolls, and baking them to perfection. They have the necessary skills and expertise to follow the recipe, ensure the right texture and taste, and monitor the baking process.

2. Assistants: The baker may have assistants who help in various tasks, such as measuring ingredients, mixing the dough, operating baking equipment, and maintaining cleanliness in the bakery. These assistants provide support to the baker and contribute to the efficiency of the production process.

3. Manager: The bakery may have a manager who oversees the overall operations, including coordinating with suppliers to ensure a steady supply of ingredients, managing the inventory, scheduling shifts for the manpower, and addressing any issues that may arise during production.

4. Sales clerks: Sales clerks are responsible for interacting with customers, taking their orders, packaging the pandesal, and handling payments. They ensure that customers receive their desired products and provide a positive customer experience.

5. Janitors: Janitors or cleaning staff play a crucial role in maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in the bakery. They clean the baking area, equipment, and the overall premises, ensuring a safe and sanitary environment for production.

It's important to have a well-coordinated and skilled manpower team in the bakery to ensure the smooth and efficient production of pandesal, as well as providing excellent customer service.
