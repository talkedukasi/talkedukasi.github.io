6. On the map, accurately draw the routes of Christopher Columbus and Ferdinand Magellan. Label each route with the last name of the explorer and the dates of his expedition.


Answer:

Christopher Columbus and Ferdinand Magellan.

Christopher Columbus embarked on his first voyage in 1492, which took him across the Atlantic Ocean from Spain to the Caribbean. He made four voyages in total, exploring various Caribbean islands and parts of Central and South America.

Ferdinand Magellan, on the other hand, led the first expedition to circumnavigate the globe, which began in 1519 and ended in 1522. His route took him from Spain through the Strait of Magellan at the southern tip of South America, across the Pacific Ocean, and past the Philippines before finally returning to Spain.

8. Who is antonio pigafetta? What is his role in the magellan-elcano expedition


Antonio Pigafetta: His Role in the Expedition

Antonio Pigafetta is from a rich family in Italy. During his 30's he joined the expedition to the Spice Islands together with Ferdinand Magellan. He served as Magellan's assistant during the expedition. During their expedition in the Philippines, he helped Magellan in translating the Cebuano language. He learned to speak Cebuano because of his journal. After the death of Magellan, Pigafetta returned to Spain together with other 17 men. Their return was under the command of Juan Sebastian Elcano. The source of information about their expedition was all recorded in Pigafetta's journal.

30. 1.What remarkable activity did the coming of Magellan?2.what was the most important contribution of Magellan expedition?3.What were two important achievements of Magellan expedition?4.What are the 5 expeditions to the Philippines after Magellan?5.What is the purpose of Magellan expedition in the Philippines?6.What are the accomplishment of Ferdinand Magellan?​


Answer:

1. The most remarkable activity of the coming of Magellan was the first circumnavigation of the globe, which was completed by his expedition.

2. The most important contribution of Magellan's expedition was the first circumnavigation of the globe, which proved that the earth is round and opened up new trade routes and possibilities for exploration.

3. Two important achievements of Magellan's expedition were the first circumnavigation of the globe and the discovery of a passage through South America, now known as the Strait of Magellan, which greatly reduced the time and difficulty of sailing from the Atlantic to the Pacific Ocean.

4. Five expeditions to the Philippines after Magellan are:

-Ruy López de Villalobos (1542–1546)

-Martín de Goiti and Juan de Salcedo (1570–1574)

-Lope de Vega (1583–1593)

-Sebastián Vizcaíno (1602–1603)

-Juan de la Isla (1668)

5. The purpose of Magellan's expedition in the Philippines was to find a route to the Spice Islands of the East Indies, which were considered a valuable source of spices and other valuable goods.

6. The accomplishments of Ferdinand Magellan were the first circumnavigation of the globe, the discovery of a passage through South America, now known as the Strait of Magellan, the discovery of the Philippines, and the opening of the Philippines to Spanish colonization.
