So, the 2nd letter is F is the 6th letter in the alphabet.

Solve the 3rd letter:
[tex]1x3 =3[/tex], [tex]3x4=12[/tex], [tex]12x5= 60[/tex], devide 60 by 26 and get the remainder which is 8 equivalent to H, [tex]8x6=48[/tex]

So, the 3rd letter is V which is the 22nd in alphabet.
