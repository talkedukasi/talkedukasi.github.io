16. Explain the future of tourism and hospitality in the next 10 years. 150 words or less​


Answer:

It is difficult to predict the future of tourism and hospitality with certainty, but there are a few trends that are likely to shape the industry in the coming years. One trend is the increasing use of technology, which is already transforming the way that hotels and other tourism businesses operate. For example, many hotels now offer guests the ability to check in and out using their smartphones, and there are a growing number of online booking platforms that make it easier for travelers to find and book accommodations. Additionally, virtual and augmented reality are likely to become more common in the tourism industry, allowing guests to experience destinations in new and exciting ways.

Another trend that is likely to continue is the growing popularity of sustainable and eco-friendly tourism. More and more travelers are looking for ways to reduce their impact on the environment, and tourism businesses are responding by offering green accommodations and eco-friendly activities. This trend is likely to accelerate in the coming years, as consumers become increasingly conscious of their environmental footprint.

Overall, the future of tourism and hospitality looks bright, with many exciting developments on the horizon. Technology will continue to play a major role, and sustainable tourism will become increasingly important. However, the industry will also face challenges, such as the need to adapt to changing consumer preferences and the ongoing effects of the COVID-19 pandemic.
