Share of Profit for Norman = Php_____ c. Share of Profit for Christian = Php._______​


Answer:

Total capital = 60,000

Christian (30k / 60k) = 50%

Norman (20k / 60k) = 33%

Pablo (10k / 60k) = 17%

Share on the profit

Pablo (30,000 x 17%) = 5,100

Norman (30,000 x 33%) = 9,900

Christian (30,000 x 50%) = 15,000
