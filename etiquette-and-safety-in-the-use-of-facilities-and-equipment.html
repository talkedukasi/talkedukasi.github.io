10. Why it is important to follow the proper etiquette and safety in the use of facilities and equipment?​


Importance of following the proper etiquette and safety in the use of facilities and equipment.

Proper etiquette and safety in the use of facilities and equipment are required in minimizing workplace dangers and hazards. They are made in order to demonstrate respect as well as to maintain equipment working in great condition and shape with no inappropriate activities that may harm it. It is of the key elements for all organizations in order to boost the well-being of both employees and employers and in turn, contribute more to the development and progress of the organization.

It is important to practice safety so that no one gets hurt or becomes ill, whether it is you, others, or both. When we do not observe proper workplace etiquette and safety, we put ourselves and others at risk of losing their jobs and, more importantly, their lives. One thing to ensure this does not happen is to stay alert and be always aware of the surroundings. Identifying possible threats and hazard plays an important role in our safety because we can eliminate these threats before they do damage.
