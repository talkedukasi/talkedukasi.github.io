Answer:

Provide Time and Activities for Practice

Explanation:

Practice is a vital part of learning any new skill, including word processing. According to Education World, students should have as much time and opportunity to practice word processing skills as possible during the first steps of lessons. When students are able to master the basics, they are ready to move onto more advanced skills. Without practice, it takes longer for the lessons to sink in and students are likely to make more mistakes.

21. what do you gain from social activities that she's going to movies with friends playing computer games with your siblings or watching television with your family do you think this are good for your health why​


Engaging in social activities such as going to movies with friends, playing computer games with siblings, or watching television with family can have positive effects on one's health. Here are some potential benefits:

1. Reduces stress - Social activities can help reduce stress by providing an opportunity to relax, have fun, and connect with others.

2. Improves mental health - Social activities can help improve mental health by reducing feelings of loneliness and depression, and by promoting a sense of belonging and connection.

3. Enhances cognitive function - Certain social activities, such as playing games or solving puzzles with others, can help improve cognitive function and memory.

4. Promotes physical activity - Some social activities, such as playing sports or going for a walk with friends, can promote physical activity and improve overall health.

5. Builds social skills - Social activities provide an opportunity to practice social skills such as communication, cooperation, and empathy, which can lead to improved relationships and social support.

Overall, engaging in social activities can have many positive effects on one's health and well-being. However, it's important to balance social activities with other healthy habits such as exercise, healthy eating, and getting enough sleep.
