A U B - (A ∩ B) = S

(A ∩ B) = kid who likes to eat at jollibee and likes to eat at mcdonalds.

24. What is the principles art of jollibee and McDonald's and meralco


These three companies use the color red as the main focal point of their brand or logo.

Red is one of the most attention-grabbing color in nature. It may also be considered among one of the most attractive. This is the reason why many brands incorporate the color red or make it the main color in their logo.

Jollibee uses red, white, and yellow in their brand. These red and yellow are primary colors and provide contrast when placed side-by-side. White is used to set a negative space in their designs.

McDonald's also uses the same colors as Jollibee. However, Jollibee actually just followed in McDo's footsteps.

Meralco uses the color orange but orange is actually derived from the color red and yellow. In a sense, it uses the same idea of grabbing attention by using a familiar and distinct color in its logo.
