30. discrimination about peers?


Discrimination and pressure from peers is common specially among teenagers. In high school, we are usually subjected to a lot of people who would pressure us to try things out. This is a normal phase in human development. But there are things which are considered that are harmful. Things like drugs and alcohol should not be tried even at the expense of being discriminated by our friends. Peers would usually ostracize us if we are "killjoy".

Peer pressure is the undue influence wielded by friends for a person to do something that is usually not  liked by another person. When a person does the challenge, they are generally accepted to the barkada but when they do not, they are discriminated upon and usually shunned from the group. This leads to the person being depressed or trying out things that could eventually damage his or her health.  

Peer pressure, on the extreme side could also be seen as a kind of bullying and bullying should not be tolerated. There are now laws against bullying in order to protect those who are subjected to it. There is also now more awareness on mental health issues which is the one usually affected when a person is subjected to peer pressure or bullying. Remember that for anyone who is bullied, help is available. You can always reach out to the guidance counselor or anyone designated to handle mental health issues at school.
