2. Think any of your own situational example of each theory ? PERENNIALISM, ESSENTIALISM, PROGRESSIVISM, RECONSTRUCTIONISM


Answer:

Philosophies of EducationPerennialism

It is a Philosophy that emphasizes the importance of enduring ideas and knowledge that lasted over centuries. From the word "Perennial" which means "Never- Ending"

Example: A College literature class that focuses on classic works of literature such as Shakespeare's plays.

Essentialism

It is a Philosophy that emphasizes the importance of a core set of knowledge and skills 3r's (Reading, Writing, and Arithmetic) that all students should learn.

Example: A Primary school curriculum that focuses on basic skills such as reading, writing, and arithmetic.

Progressivism

It is a Philosophy that emphasizes the importance of experiential learning and critical thinking.

Example: A science class that uses hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning to help students develop an understanding of scientific concepts.

Reconstructionism

It is a Philosophy that emphasizes the importance of social change and the role of education in promoting social justice.

Example: A college course that focused on social activism and community organizing.


3. Define Social Reconstructionism​


Answer:

Social reconstructionism is a philosophy that emphasizes the addressing of social questions and a quest to create a better society and worldwide democracy. Reconstructionist educators focus on a curriculum that highlights social reform as the aim of education
