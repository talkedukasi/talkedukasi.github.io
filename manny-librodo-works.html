13. describe the photo of "A landscape of emotion by Anny librodo​


Answer:

Many of award-winning photographer Manny Librodo’s students told him they wanted to be like him.

His response? “Be your own photographer. Find your own style. People will always remember you. Work with a lot of passion.”

Manny Librodo, a native of Lambunao, Iloilo used to be a nobody in the photography world but he was already looking at beautiful photographs in airline magazines, wishing that he, too can become a good photographer.

Armed with passion and a point and shoot camera, he took photos of Bangkok (where he resides now) and his European travels and posted them online. These photos have gone viral without his knowledge and before he knew it, he is already famous.

“I posted on pbase.com and waited daily for comments from viewers. I received feedback including suggestions to join competitions. There was even a time when my photo would earn about one million views in a day. 24. You are invited to attend the exhibit of Mr. Manuel “Manny” L. Librodo Jr., a highly decorated photographer, specializing in travel and people, glamour and fashion photography. One of his work caught your attention and that is an image of an old woman covering her other eye with her hand. Discuss the characteristics of media based art of the image and its message.


Answer:

The image by Manuel “Manny” L. Librodo Jr. is a perfect example of media-based art. The image is characterized by its use of light and shadow, which creates depth and adds emotional intensity to the image. The subject of the image, an old woman covering her eye with her hand, adds to the image's emotional impact. The hand gesture creates a sense of mystery, making the audience wonder what the woman might be hiding or protecting.

The message of the image can be interpreted in many ways. It could be interpreted as a representation of the limitations of vision, or a commentary on the aging process. The hand gesture could also be seen as a symbol of strength, resilience, and protection, suggesting that the woman has experienced a lot in life and has overcome challenges.

Overall, this image by Manuel “Manny” L. Librodo Jr. is a powerful and thought-provoking piece of media-based art that captures the essence of its subject and conveys a message through its visual elements.
