2. II. DEVELOPMENT Activity 2: Identify the following elements of the poem "The Road Not Taken": 1. Tone 2. 3. Mood Technique 4. Purpose 5. Symbolism​


Answer:

1. Tone: The tone of the poem is contemplative and reflective.

2. Mood: The mood of the poem is both regretful and hopeful.

3. Technique: The poem makes use of several literary techniques, including metaphor, personification, and symbolism.

4. Purpose: The purpose of the poem is to explore the idea of choice and the impact of choices on our lives.

5. Symbolism: The central symbol of the poem is the fork in the road, which represents the choices we make in life. The two roads represent two different paths that one can take in life, each leading to a different outcome. The poem also uses the seasons of fall and winter to symbolize the passage of time and the inevitability of change.
