30. Give Manifest function and Latent function of CULTURE​


Manifest function of culture is to provide a shared set of values, beliefs, and practices that enable individuals to coexist and communicate effectively within a society. It enables the creation of a shared identity and a sense of belonging for individuals.

Latent function of culture is to provide a basis for social change by challenging existing beliefs and practices, and promoting new ones. It enables individuals to view their society critically and identify areas that require improvement. Culture can also provide a basis for innovation and creativity by encouraging new ideas and ways of thinking.
