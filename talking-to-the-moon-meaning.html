Enumeration and Time Order Text Types

Directions. Read each text and identify if it is enumeration or time order text type.

Time Order 1. There are steps in cooking chicken adobo. First, prepare all the ingredients needed in cooking chicken adobo. Next, marinate it for 30 minutes.Then, drain and reserve marinade. Fry the chicken until golden brown. Combine chicken with marinade including all other ingredients and simmer it until chicken is tender. Add pineapple tidbits and fried garlic and serve hot.

Enumeration 2. Many plants are sources of products that people use, including food items, fibers (for cloth), paper, rubber, and medicines. They also provide some of our energy needs such as coal, oil for cooking, and fuel for cars (ethanol). In some parts of the world, wood is used by people to cook their food and heat their homes.

Time Order 3. The first thing we had to do was build a frame for the floor of the house. Then, we used a rope to raise all the wood up into the tree. Afterward, we carefully nailed the board to the frame, and soon we had a floor.

Enumeration 4. Plants convert light energy from the sun into food. They produce the food that people and animals eat. The animals that give us such as chickens and cows eat grass, oats, corn, and other plants.

Time Order 5. First, the dragonfly lays egg on the water. After a few days, the eggs hatched. They become nymphs and live underwater. As the nymphs grow, they shed their skin many times. Finally, they become full-ground dragonflies.

Time Order 6. Rosario’s older brother, Alberto, was undergoing a training for the summer bicycle race. Last Saturday morning, Alberto got up at 6:30 AM. He preformed his first exercises which were slow, easy stretches to lessen his muscles. Then, he did more active exercises for ten minutes. After that, he rode his bike for a five mile stretch. Back home, Alberto ate a good breakfast. While eating, he talked to Rosario about the exercises he would do in the afternoon.

Enumeration 7. The moon is our closest neighbor. It’s gravity is 1/6 of Earth. In addition, there is no atmosphere on the moon. The lack of atmosphere also means there is no water on the moon, an important problem when travelling there.

Enumeration 8. There are different ways of purifying water for drinking purposes. Distillation is one. Distilled water is made by evaporating water and then condensing the water vapor from boiling water. Bacteria are removed by passing water through a filter. But the best way of purifying water at home is by boiling it. When water is boiled, the bacteria in it one surely killed.

Time Order 9. The steps in preparing fruit salad. First, wash the fruits. Next, peel the fruits. Then, slice the fruits into small cubes or pieces. Mix the fruits together with milk or cream. Lastly, share the salad with everyone.

Take the egg out of the pot and put it in an egg cup. Now the egg is ready to eat. Enjoy it!

EXPLANATION:

Time order texts shows sequence. Some of the signal words used in these texts are first, next, then, after that, finally and lastly.

Emumeration means to list one by one. An example of enumeration text is “The advantages of exercising; A well-planned exercise schedule helps to burn fat, strengthen your muscles, reduce feelings of anxiety and depression, and just helps to improve your over-all physical and mental health.” The words in italics enumerates some of the benefits of exercising regularly.
