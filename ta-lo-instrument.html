24. Activity 2.Identify the materials on which the following instruments are made of.1. Dizi2. Banhu3. Sheng4. Yun-lo5. Kongho6. Bo​


Answer:

1.dizi is commonly carved out of a single piece of bamboo with a cork-lined blow-hole. The body of the flute has six finger-holes at measured distances

2.made from a coconut shell rather than wood, and instead of a snakeskin that is commonly used to cover the faces of huqin instruments, the banhu uses a thin wooden board.

3.Sheng originally consists of several bamboo pipes bound together with ropes or wooden frames.

4.It is made up of a set of gongs of varying sizes held within a frame. It was also called yún'áo (雲璈) in ancient times.

6.Wood: Traditional staffs such as the bo were originally constructed from wood. Oak wood is still the material of choice for traditional practitioners of Japanese martial arts. Hardwood is also a good material for strength training, as it is heavier than many other materials.
