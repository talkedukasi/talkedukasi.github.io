Here are five word problems based on finding percentage, rate, percent of increase/decrease, discounts, original price, rate of sales tax, and simple interest:

John spends $100 on groceries. He buys some items on sale, which are discounted by 15%. What is the total discount John gets on the sale items?

Answer: The total discount John gets on the sale items is $15, because 15% of $100 is $15.

Sarah is paid $20 per hour at her part-time job. She works 25 hours in a week. What is her total earnings for the week?

Answer: Sarah's total earnings for the week is $500, because $20 x 25 hours = $500.

The original price of a jacket is $100. It is now on sale for 20% off. What is the sale price of the jacket?

Answer: The sale price of the jacket is $80, because the jacket is discounted by 20% and 100 - 20 = 80.

The sales tax rate in a certain state is 6%. What is the total cost of a $50 purchase including sales tax?

Answer: The total cost of the $50 purchase including sales tax is $53, because 50 x 6/100 = 3, and 50 + 3 = 53.

An investment of $1000 earns 5% simple interest per year. How much interest will the investment earn after 2 years?

Answer: The investment will earn $100 in interest after 2 years, because 1000 x 5/100 = 50, and 50 x 2 = 100.

Here are five word problems based on finding percentage, rate, percent, percent of increase/decrease, discounts, original price, rate of sales tax and simple interest:

1. Percentage:

A student scored 90 out of 100 on a math test. What is the student's percentage score?

Answer: The student's percentage score is 90%.

2. Rate:

A car travels 400 kilometers in 4 hours. What is the car's average speed in kilometers per hour?

Answer: The car's average speed is 100 kilometers per hour.

3. Percent of increase/decrease:

The price of a shirt is $20. The price is increased by 10%. What is the new price of the shirt?

Answer: The new price of the shirt is $22.

4. Discounts:

A laptop is on sale for $500, with a 20% discount. What is the final price of the laptop after the discount?

Answer: The final price of the laptop is $400.

5. Original price:

A book is on sale for $25, with a discount of 20%. What was the original price of the book before the discount?

Answer: The original price of the book was $31.25.

6. Rate of sales tax:

An item costs $100, and the sales tax rate is 5%. How much is the sales tax on the item?

Answer: The sales tax on the item is $5.

7. Simple interest:

A bank offers a simple interest rate of 5% per year on a deposit of $1000. How much interest will be earned after 3 years?

Answer: The interest earned after 3 years will be $150.
