16. how do frogs reproductive​


Answer:

lay eggs

Explanation:

All frogs reproduce sexually, and all hatch from eggs. In almost all frogs, egg fertilization happens outside the female's body instead of inside. The female releases her eggs and the male releases his sperm at the same time. ... In such species, male frogs often produce a release call when clasped by another male.

Typically, frogs lay eggs. This process usually occurs through external fertilization, where the female releases her eggs from her body into water. Then, the male releases his sperm to fertilize them. ... In this case, the eggs are fertilized inside the female's body before they are released.
