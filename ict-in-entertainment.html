1. specific applications of ICT in entertainment?


Answer:

APPLICATION OF ICT IN ENTERTAINMENT:

ICT refers to telecommunications-based technologies that offer access to information. It's comparable to Information Technology (IT), but it's more focused on communication technology. The Internet, wireless networks, cell phones, and other forms of communication are all examples of this. ICT means "Information and Communication Technologies."

Computers began as relatively simple data storage and transmission devices. The majority of these files were text-based reports from the workplace. As technology progressed, computers grew more diverse in their capabilities. With the advent of the Internet and faster CPUs, file sharing and entertainment have become commonplace on personal computers around the world. Computers can be used in the entertainment sector in a variety of ways.

Information and communication technology have given society a huge array of new communication capabilities during the last few decades.

ICT has influenced entertainment and leisure by improving the various ways in which we spend our free time. The number of ways we can entertain ourselves has expanded tremendously as a result of the employment of these technologies.Because of the advancement of these technologies, many people are opting to stay indoors. Directly from the Internet, one can watch movies and listen to music. ICT also allows people to play games on the Internet in their spare time. ICT items like compact discs and memory cards can be used to store entertainment material for future use. By generating better graphics for music and movies, ICT has improved the quality of entertainment and leisure activities. By bringing immediate and interactive conversing via social sites, ICT has boosted communication during amusement and leisure. Modern ICT-powered social media platforms enable users to interact directly during their spare time without having to meet physically.

Technology has undoubtedly brought about many changes and will continue to do so.
