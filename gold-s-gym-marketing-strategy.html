2. what will be the most effective marketing and promotional strategies in fitness gym ​


Answer:

1. Referral Programs: Offering discounts or incentives for current members who refer new customers can be a powerful tool that creates a sense of community around your gym.

2. Social Media Marketing: Social media is an excellent tool for promoting fitness gyms.

3. Corporate Memberships: You can team up with business companies within your locality to provide discounted gym memberships for their employees.

4. Community Outreach: Participating in local health fairs, partnering with nearby schools, and taking part in charity events are excellent ways to provide local brand awareness and increase brand recognition.

5. Seasonal Promotions: Running deals geared to specific seasons, such as summer weight loss programs or post-holiday detox initiatives, can attract fresh clients from different demographics.

6. Free Trial: Giving exclusive access to members or offer a free trial of your gym’s services can introduce new clients to your business.

7. Partnership with Local Influencers: Partnering with local influencers who have a considerable following on social media platforms can help promote your business.

By implementing these strategies consistently, a fitness gym can establish itself as a leader in its community fitness scene while expanding the business's revenue.

27. What two-word term, which describes a modern-day retail event, was originally used in 1869 in reference to the collapse of the U.S. gold market?


The history behind "Black Friday"

The term "Black Friday" was first used back in September 24, 1869. It's used to describe the crash of U.S. gold market.  Nowadays, people anticipate this because it's the biggest sale event. However, very little do we know about its real history.

If one asks an American about Black Friday history myths and facts, you might hear some interesting ones. But, let me tell you about the dark history of Black Friday and where this term really stemmed out. It was that fateful day of September 24, 1869 when two Wall Street financiers named Jim Fisk and Jay Gould decided to work together to rake as much gold as they can get. They bought up the nation's gold as much as they could and eventually sold it at a very massive price. Due to their ruthless actions, the U.S. gold market and Wall Street, which is the heart of the country's stock market, collapsed. This caused bankruptcy to both barons and farmers alike.

Another dark event took place in the 1950.s that fell on this day. Philadelphia police had used this term to describe the chaos they had to face dealing with both tourists and local shoppers flocking to their city. Many of which go there to watch the famous Army-Navy football game which was held a day after, Saturday, of every year. These officers found it exhausting since they needed to work extra long shifts and had to deal with the crowd, traffic and shoplifters.

If we would solely talk about retailers and sales, what is Black Friday all about? People from retailers and accountants alike would use the term "Black" to symbolize the profit of the company, while "Red" to symbolize the loss. Retailers have soon used this term to describe the success and constant pouring of money during this day. In our modern times, this one day sale bonanza has spread and become a four-day sale event. We would often hear the term "Cyber Monday" wherein many prices of various items, especially online shopping sites, have already started to go down. People now  call this entire event a "Retail Holiday."

PROBLEM: The length of the gym is 12 more than the width. The area of the gym is 325 square meters find the dimension of the gym.

GIVEN:

A= 325 m^2

L= (w + 12) because it is given in the problem

that the length of the gym is 12 more

than the width (let the width be w).

SOLUTION:

A = L × W

325 = (w + 12) w ( substitute )

325 = w^2 +12w ( mutiply )

0 = w^2 + 12w - 325

0 = (w + 25)(w - 13)

0 = w + 25 0 = w - 13

w = -25 w = 13

since w is the width, It can't be negative.

so;

w = 13

if w = 13

Length of the gym = w + 12

= 13 + 12

= 25

width of the gym = w

= 13

ANSWER:

The dimension of the gym with area of 325 is

L = 25m

W = 13m
