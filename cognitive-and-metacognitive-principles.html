6. What is the difference between cognitive and metacognitive?​ Give examples to support your ideas.​


What is the difference between cognitive and metacognitive? The term cognitive refers to the process of acquiring knowledge (cognition) through information received from the environment, also known as learning. Metacognition, on the other hand, refers to people's ability to reflect on their thought processes and how they learn.
