10. injuries? 2. To avoid being a victim of intentional injury, what are the precautionary measures to do? ​


Injury is any physical harm or damage to the body that can occur due to accidents, falls, sports, or intentional acts. Here are some common types of injuries:

1. Traumatic injuries: These are injuries that result from physical force or impact, such as broken bones, burns, or head injuries.

2. Overuse injuries: These are injuries that occur over time due to repetitive motions or activities, such as carpal tunnel syndrome or tennis elbow.

3. Chronic injuries: These are injuries that develop gradually and last a long time, such as back pain or arthritis.

To avoid being a victim of intentional injury, here are some precautionary measures you can take:

1. Be aware of your surroundings: Pay attention to your environment and stay alert for any potential dangers. Avoid walking in isolated areas and be cautious when walking alone at night.

2. Avoid confrontations: If someone is being aggressive or confrontational, try to remove yourself from the situation as quickly and calmly as possible.

3. Learn self-defense: Consider taking a self-defense class to learn techniques for protecting yourself in case of an attack.

4. Stay in well-lit areas: Avoid walking in dark or poorly lit areas, especially at night.

5. Stay connected: Let someone know where you are going and when you expect to return. Carry a cell phone with you at all times in case you need to call for help.

6. Trust your instincts: If something feels off or wrong, trust your instincts and take steps to protect yourself.

26. The best way to avoid the dangers of planned or intentional injuries is through?


The best way to avoid the dangers of planned or intentional injuries is through prevention. There are always dangers out there and the ideal is that criminals are captured and incarcerated. But this is not always the case. Hence, it is also on us to take care of ourselves. There are several ways in which we can do this:

Learn Defensive Moves

If you are walking and someone starts to follow you, walk confidently to a crowded area or a police station. This would hopefully deter anyone from following you further.  

If you are being shot at by a gun, run away in a zigzag pattern while keeping your head down. This will lessen the chances of you being hit.  

Always be alert and never use mobile phones in public unless necessary.

Learn Martial Arts

If possible you may also learn martial arts to be able to defend yourself. This is not advisable though if a gun is used or if you are not in full awareness of who you are dealing with.  

Have defensive equipment ready

Always bring an umbrella which could double as a defensive equipment. You can also bring pepper spray. There are also anti-rape whistles that can be used.  

Note: This is not shifting the responsibility to the would-be victims. Again, it is ideal that there are no criminals and that people are taught not to commit planned and intentional crimes. But it would not hurt to be ready as well.

29. the best way to avoid the dangers of planned or intentional injuries


Answer:

One's security is one's duty. Despite the existence of armed personnels, we should always put in our mind that securing ourselves from all sorts of incidents is one of our priorities. In fact, according to Abraham Maslow's Hierarchy Of Needs, security is included. It is our need and it is our duty be secured.

In our day to day activities, no one is safe. No one knows what is going to happen. This is true unless it is planned. And even though it is planned, still no one knows when it will happen except the one who planned it. That is, regardless of whether an injury is planned or not, safety is never guaranteed. Even with the presence of authorities. Therefore, in our every day activities, we should always be concern of our security.

The best way for us to secure ourselves from any kind of injury, may it be planned or not is to Be Vigilant. Be vigilant of your surroundings. Look for things that are new to you and or suspicious. If you have seen one, report to the concern authorities. The next thing you should do is to report it to the authorities. Moreover, as much as possible stay away from places which are not well-lighted and if it is unavoidable, make sure you have someone accompanying you.

These are just some of the things you can do to avoid dangers of all sorts, may it be planned or not.
