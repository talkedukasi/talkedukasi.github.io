1. Carmelita is 40 years more than that of his daughter. After 5 years, Carmelita's age will be thrice his daughter's age. What is their present ages?​


Given :The age of Carmelita is 40 years more than that of his son.After 5 years, Carmelita's age will be thrice his son's age.

To Find :What are their present ages?

Solution :

Let,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\mapsto \: \bold{ Present\: Age_{(Daughter)} =\: a\: years}\\\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\bigstar[/tex] The age of Carmelita is 40 years more than that of his daughter.

So,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\mapsto \: \bold{Present\: Age_{(Carmelita)} =\: (40 + a)\: years}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

❒ After 5 years, their ages will be :

[tex]\begin{gathered}\leadsto \: \sf Age_{(Daughter)} =\: (a + 5)\: years\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\leadsto \: \sf Age_{(Carmelita)} =\: (40 + a + 5) =\: (a + 45)\: years\\\end{gathered}[/tex]

❒ According to the question :

After 5 years, Carmelita's age will be thrice his daughter's age.

So,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\small\implies \: \sf\boxed{\bold{\bigg\{Age_{(Carmelita)}\bigg\} =\: 3\bigg\{Age_{(Daughter)}\bigg\}}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf a + 45 =\: 3(a + 5)\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf a + 45 =\: 3a + 15\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf a - 3a =\: 15 - 45\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf {\cancel{-}} 2a =\: {\cancel{-}} 30\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf 2a =\: 30\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf a =\: \dfrac{30}{2}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \: \sf\bold{ \pmb{a =\: 15}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

Hence, their required present ages will be ;

Present Age Of Daughter :

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow \: \sf Present\: Age_{(Daughter)} =\: a\: years\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow \: \sf\bold{\underline{Present\: Age_{(Daughter)} =\: \pmb{15\: years}}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

Present Age Of Carmelita :

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow \: \sf Present\: Age_{(Carmelita)} =\: (40 + a)\: years\\\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow \: \sf Present\: Age_{(Carmelita)} =\: (40 + 15)\: years\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow \sf\bold{\underline{Present\: Age_{(Carmelita)} =\: \pmb{55\: years}}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

Thus,

[tex] \qquad \:\boxed{\begin{aligned}& \quad\sf The \: \pmb{present \: age \: } of \: \pmb{Carmelita \: is} \quad \\& \sf \quad \underline{ \pmb{ 55 \: years}} \: and \: the \: \pmb{present \: age } \: of \quad \\ & \sf \quad \pmb{his \: daughter \: is} \: \underline{ \pmb{ 15 \: years}} .\quad\\ \end{aligned}} [/tex]
