Alex and James went hiking along the foot of Mt. Talomo. Alex brought 1.75 liters of water for the whole-day hike. James brought 500 milliliters of water. How many more milliliters of water did Alex bring?

⇒ The amount of water did Alex bring is 1,750 mililiters.

To solve this problem, you can use material about unit of volume or content.

Volume or content can be interpreted as a derivative of the length principal quantity. The unit of volume is usually expressed with a cubic suffix, such as cubic meters or cubic centimeters. The cube is represented by the power of three, for example m³ or cm³.

A. Units of Cubic Volume

The cubic is a unit of volume denoted by the power of 3, for example m³, cm³, mm³. The international (SI) units of volume are m³ (MKS system) and cm³ (CGS system).
