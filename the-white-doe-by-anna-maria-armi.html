Anna's Age = A

Maria's Age = 2A - 1

after four years Ana Age = A + 4

after four years Maria Age = 2A -1 + 4 = 2A + 3

Maria will be 18 less than thrice Ana’s age.

=> 2A + 3 = 3(A + 4) - 18

=> 2A + 3 = 3A + 12 - 18

=> 9 = A

Ana Age = 9

Maria Age = 2(9) - 1 = 17
