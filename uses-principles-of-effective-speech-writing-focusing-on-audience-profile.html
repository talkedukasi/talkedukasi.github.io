Answer:

Audience: The audience for the speech will be a group of high school students.

Purpose: The purpose of the speech is to motivate the students to work hard and achieve their goals.

Topic: The topic of the speech is the importance of setting and working towards goals.

Pattern to use: The pattern that will be used for the speech is chronological, starting with the importance of setting goals and moving on to the steps for achieving them.

Outline:

I. Introduction

Attention-getter: Personal anecdote about the importance of setting and achieving goals

Thesis statement: Setting and working towards goals is crucial for success in both the short and long term.

II. Body

The importance of setting goals

The benefits of setting goals

The steps for setting and achieving goals

III. Conclusion

Summary of the main points

Call to action: Encourage the students to set and work towards their own goals

Output: The output of the speech will be a well-organized and motivating talk that inspires the students to set and achieve their goals.
