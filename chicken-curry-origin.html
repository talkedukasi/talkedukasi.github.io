2. how to cook chicken curry​


Answer:

Method 1 of 4: Making Chicken Curry in a Pan

1.Gather your ingredients.

2.Heat the oil in a deep pan and add the cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf, and cardamom. ...

3.Add the turmeric, coriander and sauté them for a minute before adding the chicken and sauté for about 5 minutes.

4.Add the tomatoes, tomato puree, salt, red chili powder and green chili and sauté for another 5 minutes.

STEP 1:

In a medium-sized pot over medium heat, pour the oil. Sear the chicken pieces and brown on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.

STEP 2:

In the same pot, saute the garlic until browned. Add the onions and ginger. Return the chicken to the pot.

STEP 3:

Add the Knorr Chicken Cube and curry powder. Saute for a few minutes, add the Knorr Ginataang Gulay Mix and water. Add the potatoes and the carrots. Let it simmer for 15 minutes covered. Then let it simmer again for 10 minutes until the sauce is thick. Season with salt and pepper.
