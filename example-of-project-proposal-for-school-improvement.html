12. what is the rational of the project proposal of the school predestrian lane?​


Answer:

The rationale of a project proposal for a school pedestrian lane is to ensure the safety of students and other pedestrians when crossing roads near the school. The project aims to address the issue of road accidents involving students and pedestrians, which can be prevented through the establishment of a dedicated pedestrian lane.

The proposal may include statistical data on road accidents involving students and pedestrians, as well as the benefits of having a pedestrian lane such as reduced risks of accidents, improved traffic flow, and increased accessibility for pedestrians. It may also emphasize the importance of promoting road safety awareness among students and the community.

20. conclusion of projects proposal of the school predestrian lane​


Answer:

After conducting a thorough study and analysis of the existing pedestrian lane situation in the school, it is evident that there is a need for improvements to ensure the safety and convenience of the students, faculty, and staff. With the proposed project of establishing a dedicated pedestrian lane, the school aims to address the issues of traffic congestion, pedestrian accidents, and the overall efficiency of transportation within the school premises.

Through the implementation of the proposed project, the school hopes to achieve the following objectives:

Improve the safety and security of pedestrians within the school premises.

Reduce traffic congestion and promote the smooth flow of traffic.

Increase awareness among the school community regarding road safety and responsible driving.

To achieve these objectives, the school proposes the following specific research questions:

What is the current situation of pedestrian traffic within the school premises?

What are the common causes of pedestrian accidents and traffic congestion within the school?

What are the best practices in establishing pedestrian lanes and how can they be adapted to the school context?

What are the potential challenges in implementing the proposed project and how can they be addressed?

In conclusion, the proposed project of establishing a pedestrian lane in the school is a necessary and important step towards ensuring the safety and convenience of the school community. By addressing the issues of traffic congestion and pedestrian accidents, the school can create a more efficient and secure transportation system within its premises.
