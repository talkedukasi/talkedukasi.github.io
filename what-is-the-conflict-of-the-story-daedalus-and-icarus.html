9. What Is the plot of the story of the story daedalus and icarus????


Answer:Daedalus is a brilliant inventor—the Thomas Edison of his day. Unfortunately, he angers King Minos, the ruler of the island Crete, and he has to hightail it out of there. Desperate to flee the island, Daedalus uses wax to build some wings for himself and his son Icarus. Daddy Daedalus warns his son to fly at a middle height: the seawater will dampen the wings and the sun will melt them. (Not good either way.)

Icarus heeds his father's advice for a bit, but then he gets cocky. He's having so much fun flying that he forgets the warning and flies too close to the sun. Sure enough, his wings melt, and Icarus plummets into the sea and drowns. Daedalus is (of course) devastated by his son's death, but the show must go on. He flies on to Sicily, where he mourns Icarus and builds a temple in honor of the god Apollo.

25. conflict of daedalus and icarus


The conflict of the story of Icarus and Daedalus is when Icarus disobeyed his father. Daedalus warned his son not to fly too low nor too high. But Icarus was excited about his bird-like flight. He flew higher and higher which led to his fall.

COMPONENTS OF A STORY The characters. The characters are individuals that are part of the story. In the story, the main characters are Icarus and Daedalus. The setting. The setting is the location of the story. The setting of the story is Crete, but specifically the top of the tower – their prison. The plot. The plot is the story itself. The plot started with Daedalus and Icarus imprisoned. Thinking of an escape, Daedalus thought of the birds. Using his skills in carpentry, he made two pairs of large wings using wax and bird feathers. As they were about to escape, Daedalus warned Icarus to stay in the middle of the sky. But Icarus flew higher to the sun which made the wax melt. His wings were destroyed and he died. The conflict. The conflict is the dividing issue within the story. The conflict is the struggle of the opposing sides of the story. It continues the story and makes it interesting. The conflict could be external or internal. The conflict in the story is external since it is between the two characters. The resolution. The resolution is the solution to the problem of the story. In the story, the resolution is sad as Daedalus wallows in regret for his son.
