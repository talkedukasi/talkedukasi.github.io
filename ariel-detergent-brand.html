24. There were 112 consumers who responded to a survey regarding laundry detergent. Thirty th cent preferred Brand A, 23% preferred Brand B, 19% preferred Brand C, and the rest were ecided. How many consumers were undecided?​


Answer:

The answer is 33

Step-by-step explanation:

The number of consumers who preferred Brand A is 112 x 30/100 = 33.6 (rounded down to 33)

The number of consumers who preferred Brand B is 112 x 23/100 = 25.76 (rounded down to 25)

The number of consumers who preferred Brand C is 112 x 19/100 = 21.28 (rounded down to 21)

The total number of consumers who preferred a brand is 33 + 25 + 21 = 79

So, the number of consumers who were undecided is 112 - 79 = 33.
