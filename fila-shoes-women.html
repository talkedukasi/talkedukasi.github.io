17. Javier designs leather shoes at his workshop. He charges $45 for women's shoes and $50 for men's shoes. He needs to make $2,500 per week to meet his expenses. This situation can be given by 45x + 50y = 2,500, where x is the number of women's shoes and y is the number of men's shoes designed by Javier. If he does not have any female customers during a particular week, how many male customers does he need to meet his expenses? (x 0, y 0, x and y take only integer values) A. 25 B. 35 C. 45 D. 50


D. 50

When you remove 45x in the equation magiging 50y=2500 nalang yun. So you divide 50y by 50 itself and the remaining nalang is y, and you divide 2500 by 50 and you get the answer 50. Where y=50.

Answer:

50 male customers

Computation:

Given the expression,

[tex]45x+50y=2,500\\\\where,\\x\to number\:of\:female\:customers\\y\to number\:of\:male\:customers[/tex]

Solving for the number of male customers needed to meet Javier's expenses if there are no female customers during a particular week.

[tex]Substitute\:0\:for\:x\\\\45x+50y=2,500\\45(0)+50y=2,500\\0+50y=2,500\\y=2,500/50=\boxed{50}[/tex]
