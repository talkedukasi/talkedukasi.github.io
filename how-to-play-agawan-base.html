How to Play Agawan Base???

Agawan base is a traditional tag game played in the Philippines and in other parts of Southeast Asia. The game is usually played by two teams, each with at least three players. One team is "in" and the other team is "out."

Here are the basic rules of how to play agawan base:

To start the game, one team is "in" and the other team is "out." The team that is "in" tries to tag the players on the other team, who are trying to run to a designated base or safe zone.

The team that is "out" tries to run from one end of the playing area to the other without being tagged by the team that is "in." If a player on the "out" team is tagged, they become part of the "in" team.

The game continues until all players on the "out" team have been tagged, at which point the teams switch roles and the game starts again.

The team that is "in" can also try to tag players on the "out" team who are running back to the base after being tagged. If a player on the "out" team makes it back to the base without being tagged, they are safe and cannot be tagged again until the next round.

The game can be played with as few as three players, but it is more fun with more players. The game can also be played indoors or outdoors, as long as there is a designated base or safe zone for the team that is "out."
