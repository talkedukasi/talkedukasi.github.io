19. Research types and styles of room service trays and trolleys and describe and appropriate storage location​


Answer:

Room service trays and trolleys are essential components of hotel room service and are used to deliver food and beverages to guests in their rooms. They come in various styles and types to accommodate different types of food and beverages, and their design can have a significant impact on the guest's dining experience.

Types of room service trays and trolleys include:

Food Trays: These trays are designed to hold hot food items and are made from materials such as melamine, plastic or stainless steel to keep food at the correct temperature.

Beverage Trolleys: These trolleys are designed to hold drinks and glasses and are often equipped with a refrigerated section to keep drinks cool.

Dining Trolleys: These trolleys are designed to hold both food and drinks and are often used for in-room dining experiences.

Room Service Trolleys: These trolleys are designed to hold a complete room service meal and are often equipped with compartments to separate food items and drinks.

In terms of storage, room service trays and trolleys should be stored in a clean, dry and secure location to prevent damage or contamination. This may include storage rooms, linen closets or designated storage areas within the hotel's kitchen. They should be stored in a manner that allows for easy access for room service staff and for cleaning and maintenance purposes.

It is important for hotels to choose room service trays and trolleys that are durable, easy to clean and appropriate for the type of food and beverages being served. A well-designed and appropriately stored room service tray or trolley can enhance the guest's dining experience and help to maintain high standards of hygiene and cleanliness
