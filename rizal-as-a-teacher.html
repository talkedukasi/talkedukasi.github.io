20. give two contributions of rizal as a teacher​


What are the contributions of Jose Rizal as an educator?

Rizal established his special school in 1893 until 1896. He taught his students reading, writing, language (Spanish and English), geography, history, mathematics arithmetic and geometry. He taught also industrial work, nature study, morals and gymnastics. He trained them how to collect specimens of plants and animals.

Rizal wrote an essay that tackles his social philosophy and philosophy of education and its continuing relevance and influence to the Filipino nation and Philippine Society.

He also believes that a good teacher or professor must have an honourable personality in order to command respect from the students. He also mentioned that the teacher needs prestige, reputation, moral strength and some freedom of action to maintain authority. A teacher should be virtuous in character because a teacher serves as a role model to a student.
