17. what are the advantages and disadvantages of polyester?


Answer:

Polyester absorbs less than one percent of moisture by weight, offers good dimensional stability under a variety of conditions, and has unique filling properties. Fourth, and most important, your customers will love it. Polyester is easy to machine wash and dry clean.

Easily dyed, strong, light weight, and resistant to shrinking, stretching, mildew and creasing. Sun resistant. Main disadvantage is that Polyester does not breathe. Fabric shine can be unattractive.
