1. How literature mirrors life​


Literature has the ability to reflect and illuminate the human experience, often serving as a mirror to society and its values, beliefs, and struggles. Through their writing, authors are able to capture the complexities of life and bring attention to social and political issues. They can also explore universal emotions and experiences, such as love, loss, and personal growth, that are relevant to readers from all walks of life. In this way, literature serves as a powerful tool for understanding both the individual and collective human experience, and for exploring the complexities of the world around us.
