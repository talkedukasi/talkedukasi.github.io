11. what is social and cultural innovation?give real life example.​


Examples of social and cultural innovations:

1. Distance Learning

2. Fair Trade System

3. Microfinancing

4.Open Source

5. Human Rights Organization (HAM)

EXPLANATION:

Innovation is not only about business, but also about having a positive social and cultural impact. The key lies in the values that benefit society, not just individuals. Five social innovations that have changed the world:

1. Distance Learning

Currently, various universities have distance learning or e-learning programs that allow us to learn from all corners of the world.

2. Fair Trade System

The Fair Trade movement has succeeded in promoting equality in commodity trade from developing to developed countries, so that workers in developing countries receive a fairer outcome.

3. Microfinancing

The concept, which was first initiated by Grameen Bank, provides credit to the common people without collateral, so that the small people have incentives to increase their income.

4.Open Source

The open source method that gives every one universal access to contribute has transformed many fields.

5. Human Rights Organization

Once upon a time, there was no such thing as Human Rights. Over time, people have become more aware that there is a need for the concept of Human Rights. Thanks to the development of human rights defender organizations, such as Amnesty International, human rights have become a concept that is commonly understood by today's society.
