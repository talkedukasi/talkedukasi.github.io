2. How will enhanced basic education curriculum araling panlipunan?


Answer:

Enhanced basic education curriculum Araling Panlipunan (EPP) will help students gain a better understanding of the social and cultural structures of the Philippines. It will also provide students with a more holistic view of their nation’s history, culture, and economic development. The EPP curriculum will also equip students with the knowledge and skills to become more active and responsible citizens, as well as to become more aware of their responsibilities to the environment. Furthermore, it will also help to foster critical thinking skills, enabling students to make informed decisions about their own lives and society as a whole
