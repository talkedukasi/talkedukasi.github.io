Answers

21. Gimbal ↬ Palawan

Gimbal is a drum instrument with a wooden body that is covered with an animal skin.

22. Gitgit ↬ Mindoro

Gitgit is an instrument with 3-4 strings with a wooden body and have a bow that is made of human hair.

23. Ulibaw ↬ Cordillera

Ulibaw is a mouth-resonated bamboo instrument played by flicking one end while placed between the lips.

24. Bungkaka/Bilbil ↬ Cordillera

Bungkaka or Bilbil is an instrument played by striking against the palm of one hand.

25. Guitar ↬ Visayas

A guitar is a rondalla instrument classified as a chordophone with 6 strings played by strumming or plucking the strings.

26. Gangsa ↬ Cordillera

Gangsa is a gong instrument that is played in an ensemble of two or more gongs in various sizes and is played by striking with the palm or beaten with a mallet.

27. Kalutang ↬ Mindoro

Kalutang is a pair of equal-sized wood sticks that are plated by strucking the sticks together.

28. Bandurria ↬ Visayas

Bandurria is a rondalla instrument with 14 strings. It is the soprano of the rondalla and usually plays the melodic line of the composition.

29. Pagang ↬ Palawan

Pagang is an instrument that is made of one whole node of bamboo that is partly split with the skin partly out.

30. Tongatong ↬ Cordillera

Tongatong is an intrument made of various sizes of bamboos tubes that is played by stomping each against the ground.
