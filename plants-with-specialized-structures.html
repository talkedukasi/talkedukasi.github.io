11. Specialized structures of terrestrial plants and aquatic plants


Terrestrial plants are plants that grow in or from land.
1. Have woody stems to hold them upright for the absorption of light.
2. Thick leaves to reduce water loss.
3. Have vascular tissue.

Aquatic plants are plants that living in an aquatic environment.
1. Don't have support structure
2. Plants are flexible.
3. Doesn't have vascular tissue
