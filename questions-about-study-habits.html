The first thing Joseph needs to do is to make observations, find an ants colony near his house and observe them. Second, based on Joseph's observations he can come up with a question like "What are the feeding patterns of ants?". Third, he can now develop hypothesis or prediction on the feeding patterns of ants. Fourth, Joseph may now conduct an experiment to test his hypothesis. He can try to put sugar near the ant's colony and find out if his hypothesis is right or wrong. Next, Joseph can now record and analyze the result of his experiment in the feeding patterns of ants.
