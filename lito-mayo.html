1. What is the message of artwork Mukha OF Lito Mayo ?​


Answer:

Lito Mayo (December 17, 1954 – May 4, 1983) was a Filipino graphic artist, print-maker, avantegarde poet, social activist, sculptor, satirist, and art professor. His prolific career was brief; it lasted only a mere decade. He was one of the active young artists who experimented, collaborated, and exhibited art works in the thriving hubs of modernist and contemporary galleries and art associations in Ermita District of Manila. He was also credited by his peers and art writers as the "Original Punk"of Philippine Arts.

1. What is the speed of Lito in going to school?

[tex]\bold{ \frac{distance}{time} = \frac{200}{8} = 200 \div 8 = \: 25 }[/tex]

Therefore Lito's speed is:

25 m/ min

2. What is the speed of Lita?

[tex]\bold{ \frac{distance}{time} = \frac{200}{16} = 200 \div 16= \: 12.5 }[/tex]

Therefore Lita's speed is:

12.5 m/ min

3. How will you convert the speed of Lito and Lita to velocity?

Based on the given situation their school is 200 meters West so:

Lita: 12.5 m/ min West

Lito: 25 m/ min West

4. What are the velocities of Lito and Lita?

Lita: 12.5 m/ min WestLito: 25 m/ min West

5. If Lito and Lita started walking to school at the same time and direction, how far will Lito away from Lita after 8 minutes of walking?

16 ÷ 8 = 2

200 ÷ 2 = 100

Therefore:

After 8 minutes of walking Lito was 100 meters far away from Lita.

27. lito is 12 years older than this wife. if we combine lito's age and his wife, the sum is 60. what is the age of lito's wife?


sum of their age is 60
lito is 12 yrs older, therefore lito's wife is 48 yrs old

Let X be the age of lito's wife.
X+12 be lito's age since he is 12 yrs older than his wife.
We add these equations and equate it to 60.
X+X+12=60
Combine like terms and transpose 12 to the right.
2X=48
Divide both sides by 2
X=24
So, lito's wife is 24 yrs old.
To find lito's age we just add 12 to 24 , lito is 36 yrs old
