29. is tuna and white bean salad main course SALAD?


Explanation:

Yes, a tuna and white bean salad can be considered a main course salad. A main course salad is a type of salad that is intended to be a complete and satisfying meal, typically consisting of a variety of ingredients such as protein, vegetables, grains, and/or legumes. A tuna and white bean salad could be a main course salad if it includes a sufficient amount of these types of ingredients and is served as a main course, rather than as an appetizer or side dish. The specific ingredients and presentation of the salad will determine whether it is considered a main course salad or not.
