28. among the 4 theories which theory is the acceptable for you, explain why? the 4 theories is the Wave Migration Theory, Core Population Theory, Out-of-Taiwan Theory and Nusantao Maritime Trading And Communication Network.PLEASE ANSWER ​


Answer:

For me, the most acceptable theory is the Nusantao Maritime Trading And Communication Network Theory. This theory explains the migration of Austronesian peoples from the South China Sea and Taiwan to the islands of Southeast Asia and the Pacific in prehistoric times through a network of maritime trading and communication. This theory is supported by archaeological evidence as well as linguistic, cultural, and genetic studies. This theory also acknowledges the importance of the oceans and the sea in facilitating human migration and cultural diffusion in prehistoric times. This theory acknowledges the role of seafaring in the spread of Austronesian cultures and languages, and emphasizes the importance of maritime trade in the development of Austronesian societies. This theory is also an important reminder of the dynamic nature of human migration and cultural contact in the Pacific region.
