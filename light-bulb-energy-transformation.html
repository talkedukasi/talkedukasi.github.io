15. What electrical energy can be transformed when we switch on the electrical bulb?A, sound energyB, light and heat energyC. Light and sound energyD. Chemical and sound energy


When a light bulb is switched on, the electrical energy will be transformed into light and heat energy.

Let us define each type of energy one by one:

Electrical energy results from the movement of electrically charged particles. This is the energy that flows from electrical wires into our homes.Sound energy is anything and everything that we can hear. Sound energy is the result of a force creating vibrations within a certain frequency that humans are able to hear.Light energy is that which allows certain types of light to become visible to the human eye.Heat energy or thermal energy is the transfer of energy from one body to another due to the differences in temperature.Chemical energy is a form of energy released when chemicals undergo reactions.

So, when you think of switching a light bulb on, which types of energy do you think are present and in motion? The electrical current will pass through the bulb's filament and heat it up to emit light. Therefore, the correct answer is (B) Light and heat energy.
