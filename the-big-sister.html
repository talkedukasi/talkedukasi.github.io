11. summery of big sister​


Answer:

Inciang, an 18-year-old big sister, aided her brother Itong, 12 years of age, pack stuff for Vigan. There, she remembered the hard times caring for his needs. Their mom died while giving birth to Itong and their dad never remarried and spent his time tilling the land for their needs.

 

Itong needs to go to school and has to pursue the dream of him and his family as the valedictorian of the barrio. The family showed support, love, and care and Inciang felt pain for the first time of being apart of her sibling as he heads to Vigan.

A year after, Itong returned to the barrio. He is more eager to see his friends rather than his family. His attitude towards Inciang changed as he is now distant to her. Inciang felt upset but accept that it is inevitable. That is, until Nena came into her life. Nene might be someone who could be her younger sister.
