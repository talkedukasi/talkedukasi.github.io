18. Dissolution of firm means all the existing partnership of the firm is dissolved whereas dissolution of partnership means the dissolution of partnership among all the partners occurs.​


STEP 1 OF 1

Dissolution of partnership: It refers to an adjustment of the current arrangement between the partners. Business of the firm continues. Assets of the firm are revalued and liabilities of the firm are reconsidered. The gains or loss because of it, is conveyed among the partners in their old-profit sharing ratio.

Dissolution of partnership firm: It refers to the dissolution of partnership between every one of the partner of the firm. Business of the firm arrives at an end.Assets of the firm are acknowledged and liabilities of the firm are settled. The equilibrium sum, assuming any, is conveyed among all the partners.

FINAL ANSWER

→ There is no intervention of court in dissolution of partnership but there is the intervention of court in dissolution of firm.
