The desired gene may be microinjected directly into the cell or it may be inserted into the core of a virus by gene splicing and the virus allowed to infect the cell for replication of the gene. The Scientist is clone their gene of interest into a Ti Plasmid, along with DNA sequences that make sure the gene can be expressed in plants. Recombinant DNA is a technology scientists developed that made it possible to insert a human gene into the genetic material of a common bacterium. This “recombinant” micro-organism could now produce the protein encoded by the human gene.

10. Make a flow chart to show how heat from within the Earth is tapped as a source of energy for human use.help me guys please, thanks!I need the answer right now​


Answer:

HOT WATER-STEAM-TURBINE & GENERATOR-COOLING TOWER-INJECTION WELL

Explanation:

Hot water is pumped from deep underground through a well under high pressure.

When the water reaches the surface, the pressure is dropped, which causes the water to turn into steam.

The steam spins a turbine, which is connected to a generator that produces electricity.

The steam cools off in a cooling tower and condenses back to water.

The cooled water is pumped back into the Earth to begin the process again.

#BrainlyFast

Ovary → Fimbriae → Fallopian tube → Uterus → Vagina

The egg cell will start to develop in the ovarian follicle (ovary). This follicle will get matured and on the 14th day with the highest level of LH (luteinizing hormone) the egg cell will be released from the follicle and the ovary and the term called ovulation. The egg cell will travel going to the Fallopian tube and by entering the fimbriae and stays there waiting for the sperm cell to fertilized it.

If the sperm cell will not be fertilized, the egg cell will die and menstruation will happen. The egg cell will goes out going to the uterus and then goes out on the uterine cervix and vagina.

If egg cell will be fertilized by the sperm cell, it will develop and implanted in the uterus for the fetal growth and development.
