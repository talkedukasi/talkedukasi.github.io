5. 1. Which program should be based upon understanding the needs and problems of the students,competence and interest of the guidance personnel?A. Guidance toolsC. Guidance principlesB. Guidance servicesD. Guidance techniques​


Answer:

Guidance counseling /Guidance services

Explanation:

Guidance counseling also referred to as Guidance and counseling, is the process of helping individuals recognize and develop their potentials. This could be vocational, educational, and even psychological with the sole purpose to attain the best possible result.

Children and students need guidance counseling to bring out the best in them. The program is encouraged in education institutions to help understand the needs and problems of the students, competence, and how to help them become the best version of themselves.

School counselors and parents are great influencers of guidance counseling on children. A school counselor works in educational institutions such as primary, secondary, and tertiary schools so the students can access social-emotional assistance.

Biological foundation of human development includes genetics, heredity, traits, character.

Human grow and development according to their genetic make up. They inherited the traits and characteristics of their parents. Some child have the same attitude and character to their parents as part of its genetic make up. If the parents are both good person, the tendency for the child will also become a good person in the future base on genetics. Environmental foundation of human developments include how the environment nurture the child. The influence of the environment on Human.

Good environment helps the child to control its negative behavior and promotes positive behavior. On the other hand, Negative environment promotes negative behavior of the child.

30. difference between shallow foundation and deep Foundation​


Answer:

Difference between Shallow and Deep Foundation For a Shallow foundation, the depth of foundation is equal to or less than its width. Whereas for Deep foundation, the depth of footing is equal to or greater than its depth. A shallow foundation is located at very low depth, whereas a deep foundation is constructed at more depth.
