15. Give two (2) advantages and two (2) disadvantages of people media. How can we use the advantages to make our lives better and how can we address the advantages?


ANSWER:

People media refers to media that is created and shared by individuals, rather than by professional journalists or media organizations. Some advantages of people media include increased diversity of voices and perspectives, and the ability for individuals to share their own experiences and stories. This can create a more inclusive and diverse public discourse, and give people the opportunity to share their unique perspectives and experiences.

Two disadvantages of people media are that it can be less reliable and trustworthy than professional media, and it can be more difficult to verify the accuracy of the information that is shared. This can lead to the spread of misinformation and false information, which can have negative consequences for individuals and society as a whole.

To make our lives better using the advantages of people media, we can use it to learn about different perspectives and experiences, and to engage with diverse voices. This can help us to better understand the world around us and to develop empathy and understanding for others. We can also use people media to share our own experiences and perspectives, and to connect with others who have similar interests and experiences.

To address the disadvantages of people media, we can take steps to verify the accuracy and reliability of the information that we encounter on social media and other platforms. This can involve checking multiple sources and using critical thinking skills to evaluate the credibility of the information. We can also support professional journalism and media organizations, which can provide more reliable and trustworthy information.
