15. Metropolitan theater during japanese occupation


The Metropolitan TheaterDuring Japanese Occupation

During the Japanese occupation, many theaters in Manila closed down but the Met continued to be the center of cultural entertainment. Badly damaged during World War II, the theater became an ice cream parlor, boxing arena , garage, motel , gay club and eventually a squatter colony of about 50 to 70 families.
