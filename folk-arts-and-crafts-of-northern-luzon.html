11. what specific folk arts and crafts of northern Luzon struck me the most​


Answer:

• ILOCOS REGION

this region is known for inabel fabrics, and also for the centuries-old tradition of burnay pottery

• BENGUET

is a highland province of the philippines located in the southern tip of the cordillera administrative region in the island of luzon, also known as the "salad bowl of the philippines"

• KALINGA

their people are known as great weavers, their art of cloth weaving and basketry are among the finest products in the whole country

• IFUGAO

this term means "people from the hills" and is a landlocked province of the philippines in the cordillera administrative region in luzon, women usually weave a loincloth using a tie

• MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

is located at the center of the cordillera mountains, its capital is bontoc, it is also known for its mummy caves and hanging coffins

• ABRA

known as the "natural dye capital of the philippines" and bontocs and ifugaos are the original incomers of this place

• CAGAYAN VALLEY

also designated as region ii, is the second largest region of the philippines and it is believed that this place is dominated by extinct animals during the early time

• BATANES

often visited by typhoons and the people of this archipelago province are called ivatans, their houses are made of limestones

• PAMPANGA

is internationally known for their culinary arts and christmas lanterns

Beautiful Folk Arts and Crafts of Northern Luzon

(3-stanza Poem)

Northern Luzon rich culture that includes their folk arts and crafts

We can see it when we visit their beautiful land areas

Some of the provinces of Northern Luzon are included in Ilocos Region

Others are in Cordillera Autonomous Region

We can see the finest weaved cloths and baskets in Kalinga

The historic mummy caves and coffins are located in the mountains of Cordillera

Igorots show their artistic expression in their Tinalak basket made from rattan

Bontocs are known for their unique baskets namely Suklang

Kalinga love their textiles with geometric pattern

Ifugaos have this cloth called Ikat which means to bind together

Tattoo is an art that they are also proud of

And the greatest Tattoo Artist is Apo Whang-od.

Northern Luzon has one of the largest ethno-linguistic group in the Philippines. They have rich culture that includes their folk arts and crafts.
