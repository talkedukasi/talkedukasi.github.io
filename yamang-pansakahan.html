26. what is the meaning of yamang pansakahan


Yamang Pansakahan is a Filipino term which refers to a rice field; a landform that usually resembles a plain. Yamang pansakahan is a form of land where farmers plant their crops. It is often referred to as rice field, wherein farmers grow variety of crops like rice, corn, wheat and other crops of the same kind. It is mostly seen on countries in Asia where the weather is perfect in growng such crops. These rice fields are subdivided into sections.what isthe meaning of yamang pangsakahan
