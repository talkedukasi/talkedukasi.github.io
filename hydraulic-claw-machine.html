A hydraulic drilling machine for cutting into rock formations and the like. The machine has a rotating drill rod with a bit at one end and a rotary hydraulic drive motor at the other end. The drill rod is supported on a carriage adapted for linear travel to advance the rotating bit into the rock formation.
