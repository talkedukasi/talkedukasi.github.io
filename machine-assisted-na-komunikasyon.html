Robot is B. a physical machine designed to help a human user to perform a specific task. Can execute specific tasks with little or no human intervention and with speed and precision. Design, engineering, and operation is called robotics. Different types are for them to perform. Can perform some tasks better than humans.

Things Robots Do Better Than Humans:Automate manual or repetitive activities in corporate or industrial settings. Work in unpredictable or hazardous environments to spot hazards like gas leaks. Process and deliver reports for enterprise security. Fill out pharmaceutical prescriptions and prep IVs. Deliver online orders, room service and even food packets during emergencies.
