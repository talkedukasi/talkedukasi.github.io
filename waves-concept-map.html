3. Surface wave                                         3. Radio wave

Parts of the wave:  

1. Wavelength - The distance from trough to  trough or from crest to crest or from any point from the first wave to the same point of the second wave.  

2. Crest - The highest point of the wave on top.  

3. Amplitude - The maximum displacement of the wave and the distance from the equilibrium to the crest or to the trough.  

4. Wavelength - The distance from trough to  trough or from crest to crest or from any point from the first wave to the same point of the second wave.  

5. Trough - The lowest point of the wave.
