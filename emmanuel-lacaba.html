2. Summary of Prometheus Unbound by Jose lacaba​


Answer:

In summary, Prometheus Unbound by Jose Lacaba is a powerful poem that retells the Greek myth of Prometheus in the context of the Philippine revolution of 1896. It is a reflection on the struggles for freedom and independence and the sacrifices made by those who fought for it. 6. Emmanuel is 8 years older than roberto. two years ago emmanuel was twice as old as roberto. how old are they now?


Solution:

Let (x) be Roberto's age
and (x + 8) be Emmanuel's

2(x - 2) = (x + 8) - 2
2x - 4 = x + 6
x = 10

Substitute:

x = 10
x + 8 = 10 + 8 = 18

Answer: Emmanuel is 18 years old while Roberto is 10 years old.

22. Vittorio Emmanuel Orlando


Vittorio Emanuele Orlando (19 May 1860 – 1 December 1952) was an Italian statesman, known for representing Italy in the 1919 Paris Peace Conference with his foreign minister Sidney Sonnino. He was also known as "Premier of Victory" for defeating the Central Powers along with the Entente in World War I.He was also member and president of the Constitutional Assembly that changed the Italian form of government into a Republic. Aside from his prominent political role Orlando is also known for his writings, over a hundred works, on legal and judicial issues; Orlando was a professor of law.


23. buod ng bayan ko laban o bawi ni jose f lacaba


Ang Bayan Ko Laban o Bawi ni Jose F. Lacaba ay isang makatotohanang pagpapahayag ng sitwasyon sa Pilipinas. Ipinapahayag dito kung bakit sinukuan na ng karamihan sa mga Pilipino ang sariling bansa at pinipiling umalis at magpa-alipin sa ibang bansa. Ito ay sa kadahilanang wala na yatang pag-asa pang magbago at umunlad ang Pilipinas mula sa sektor ng edukasyon, sosyal, pang-gobyerno at marami pang iba. Ayaw na yatang lumaban ng mga Pilipino at ang gusto ay bawi nalang kaya lalabas ng bansa para kumita ng mas malaki.
