1. Clay particles are the finest of all the soil particles, measuring fewer than 0.002 mm in size. It consists of microscopic and sub-microscopic particles derived from the chemical decomposition of rocks. Clay is a fine grained cohesive soil.

25. Which type of soil do you usually expect if the community is along the seashore? a. Loam B. Clay c. sand D. silt


Answer:

The type of sand that usually expect if the community is along seashore is c. sand.

Explanation:

Sand is made by weathering of rocks or by erosion which is carried by seas or rivers. Along seashores, sand is sometimes can be made also by broken shells, corals or bones broken up by predators. Quartz is the most important mineral composition of the sand. Quartz is also known as silicon dioxide.  

There are different colors of sand:

1. White Sand

2. Black Sand

3. Pink Sand

4. Red-Orange Color

5. White-grey color

6. Light-brown color

The colors of the sand are from the different minerals and rocks. Like the Bermuda's Pink Sand Beaches the sand became color pink because of broken coral pieces, shells and carbonate materials left behind by foraminifera (tiny marine creatures with red and pink shells). The sea shell can also be a big factor of sand color in the beach. Sea shells are usually white and when these shells comes from erosion it could result to white color sand. The color of the sand depends on what color of rocks it comes from. Sands also form sand dunes during weathering near the beach (https://brainly.ph/question/2567598).
