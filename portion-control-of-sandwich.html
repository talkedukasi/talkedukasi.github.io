23. what are the four tips on how to measure and control portion sizes of sandwiches​


Answer:

1. Measure accurately. For foods and beverages, use gadgets like a measuring cup, tablespoon, teaspoon, or food scale.

2. Learn how to estimate serving sizes. “‘Ballpark’ food portion sizes by estimating serving sizes in comparison to known objects,” says Rose Clifford, RD, clinical dietitian in the department of pharmacy services at the Washington Hospital Center in Washington, DC. “For example, three ounces of cooked meat, fish, or poultry is about the size of a deck of cards.” Other easy measurements to eyeball include:

½ cup is the size of an ice cream scoop

1 cup is the size of a tennis ball

1 ounce of cheese is the size of a domino

3. Use portion control dishware. Pick out smaller plates, bowls, cups, and glassware in your kitchen and measure what they hold. You might find that a bowl you thought held 8 ounces of soup actually holds 16, meaning you’ve been eating twice what you planned.

4. Dish out your servings separately. Serve food from the stove onto plates rather than family-style at the table, which encourages seconds.

5. Make your own single-serving packs. “Re-portion bulk quantities of favorite foods such as pasta, rice, and cereal into individual portions in zipper bags so that when you’re in the mood for some food you’ll instantly see the number of portions you’re preparing,” says Jennifer Nasser, RD, PhD, assistant professor in the department of biology at Drexel University in Philadelphia.

6. Add the milk before the coffee. When possible, put your (fat-free) milk into the cup before adding the hot beverage to better gauge the amount used.

7. Measure oil carefully. This is especially important because oil (even the healthful kinds like olive and safflower) have so many calories; don’t pour it directly into your cooking pan or over food.

27. Why is portion control in sandwich making important?​.


Answer:

Portion control in sandwich making is important for a few reasons:

Cost efficiency: By using the right amount of ingredients, you can ensure that you are using your resources efficiently and not wasting any food. This can help you save money on ingredients and reduce food waste.Consistency: By using consistent portion sizes, you can ensure that all of your sandwiches are made to the same standard and that customers know what to expect when they order.Health: Using appropriate portion sizes can help you manage the nutritional content of your sandwiches. For example, using too much meat or cheese can increase the calorie and fat content of a sandwich.Aesthetics: Using the right portion sizes can help your sandwiches look appealing and well-balanced. For example, using too much filling can make a sandwich difficult to eat and visually unappealing.

Overall, proper portion control is an important aspect of sandwich making that can help you create tasty and satisfying sandwiches that meet the needs and preferences of your customers.

28. how is portion and control of sandwich can expedite preparation and service​


Answer:

Portion and control of sandwich can expedite preparation and service in the following ways:

Consistency - By portioning the ingredients of the sandwich accurately, it ensures that each sandwich has the same amount of ingredients. This results in consistent taste and quality, which can help expedite preparation and service since the staff can prepare sandwiches quickly and efficiently without worrying about inconsistencies.
Speed - By controlling the amount of ingredients used in each sandwich, it can expedite preparation and service. Staff can quickly assemble sandwiches without taking too much time to weigh or measure ingredients, resulting in faster service and shorter wait times for customers.
Waste reduction - Portion control can also help reduce waste. By using only the required amount of ingredients, the staff can ensure that they are not overusing or wasting any ingredients. This can help save costs and improve efficiency in the kitchen.
Customer satisfaction - Portion control can help ensure that the sandwich is not overloaded with ingredients, making it easier for the customer to eat and enjoy. This can result in higher customer satisfaction and repeat business.

Overall, portion and control of sandwich can help expedite preparation and service by ensuring consistency, speed, waste reduction, and customer satisfaction.
