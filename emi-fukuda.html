5. + If king weighted 15 kilograms (kg) and has weight of 78 cm, what is tx a emi and his classification?​


To determine someone's Body Mass Index (BMI), the formula is:

BMI = weight (in kg) / height (in meters)^2

We can convert the height from centimeters to meters by dividing by 100:

BMI = 15 / (0.78/100)^2

BMI = 15 / (0.0078)^2

BMI = 15 / 6.08

BMI = 2.46

The BMI classification categories are:

Less than 18.5: Underweight18.5 to 24.9: Normal weight25 to 29.9: Overweight30 and above: Obese

Based on King's BMI of 2.46, he is classified as "Underweight".

The sentence above is an example of antonym clue because antonym words were used specifically the quiet and loquacious word were used which actually has opposite meanings and words that has opposite meaning are called antonyms.

CONTRAST / ANTONYM CLUE. Antonyms are words with opposite meanings. An opposite meaning context clue contrasts the meaning of an unfamiliar word with the meaning of a familiar term.
