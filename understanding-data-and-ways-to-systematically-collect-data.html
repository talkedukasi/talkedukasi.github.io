27. RESEARCH DESIGN IS A SYSTEMATIC WAY OF COLLECTING DATA


Answer:

False

Explanation:

A research design is the framework of research methods and techniques chosen by a researcher. The design allows researchers to hone in on research methods that are suitable for the subject matter and set up their studies for success.

Thus, Data collection is a process of collecting information from all the relevant sources to find answers to the research problem, test the hypothesis and evaluate the outcomes. Data collecting method and Research design are not equal.
