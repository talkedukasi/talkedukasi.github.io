16. How does First Aid assist emergency situation for unintentional injuries?​


Answer:
First Aid can help to save lives, reduce pain and discomfort, and prevent further injury in an emergency situation for unintentional injuries. It can include basic life support such as calling 911, applying pressure to a wound to stop bleeding, applying a splint to a broken bone, cleaning and dressing a wound, and providing CPR. It can also include more advanced care such as controlling severe bleeding and giving oxygen to those who are having difficulty breathing.
