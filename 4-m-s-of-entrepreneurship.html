12. Enumerate the 5 lessons you want to learn about Entrepreneurship Lessons I want to learn in Entrepreneurship 1. 2. 3. 4. 5.


Answer:

1. Understanding the market: To be a successful entrepreneur, you need to understand your target market and have a deep knowledge of their needs, pain points, and preferences. This information can then be used to develop products or services that solve problems and meet customer demands.

2. Identifying and managing risk: Entrepreneurship involves taking calculated risks. You need to be able to identify potential risks and have a plan in place to manage them effectively.

3. Building a strong team: No entrepreneur can succeed alone. Building a strong and supportive team can be the key to achieving your goals and overcoming challenges.

4.Effective leadership and decision-making: Effective leadership and decision-making skills are crucial for entrepreneurs. You need to be able to motivate and inspire your team, make sound decisions, and navigate complex business situations.

5. Financial management: Entrepreneurs need to be able to manage their finances effectively, including understanding how to raise capital, create a budget, and keep track of cash flow. A strong financial foundation can help ensure the longevity and success of your business.
