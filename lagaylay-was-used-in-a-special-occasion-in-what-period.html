10. 18. Turkmenistan is also the source of keten homespun silk with embroidery of different pattern: that are as unique as a family seal. What is it use for? A. Used for the beautiful dresses worn by Turkmen women on special occasions. B. Used as home decorations on the walls during special occasions. C. Used as a carpet for the floor in front of the main door. D. Used for tablecloths in special occasions.​


Answer:

The keten homespun silk with embroidery of different patterns that are unique as a family seal in Turkmenistan is primarily used for beautiful dresses worn by Turkmen women on special occasions. These dresses are often highly decorated with intricate embroidery and are considered a symbol of cultural heritage and national identity. The embroidery patterns on the silk are often passed down through families and are considered a form of family seal, which adds to the cultural significance of these dresses. Therefore, option A is the correct answer.
