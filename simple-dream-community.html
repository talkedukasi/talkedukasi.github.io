The stanza that links about education is "In community we teach each other about science and art and sport. From this we gain our vitality, and through this our sickness is fought." This part of the poem emphasizes the importance of education in the community, and how it can help people to become healthier and more fulfilled.

This part of the poem talks about how being in a community can offer support, protection, and encouragement from others.
