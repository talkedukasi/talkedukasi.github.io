7. who can help me decipher the rhymes in the poem "Elegy Written in a Country Churchyard"?​


Answer: The rhyme in the poem "Elegy Written in a Country Churchyard" by Thomas Grey is ABAB rhyme pattern in quatrains (divided into 4 lines). The last syllable /measure of line 1 will rhyme with the last syllable /measure of line 3, same with the last syllable /measure of line 2 matching with the last syllable /measure of line 4.

Here's an example from Stanza 2:

Now fades the glimm'ring landscape on the sight,

        And all the air a solemn stillness holds,

Save where the beetle wheels his droning flight,

        And drowsy tinklings lull the distant folds;

Words in bold represents the last syllable /measure of line 1 will rhyme with the last syllable/measure of line 3. While the italicized words gives you the last syllable /measure of line 2 matching with the last syllable /measure of line 4.

9. sino si Queen marie antoinette​


Answer:

Si Reyna Maria Antonieta, o higit na kilala bilang Marie Antoinette, reynang konsorte ng Pransiya at Navarro,  ay isang Reyna ng Pransiya at Arkodukesa ng Austria. Anak siya ng Emperador ng Banal na Imperyong Romano na si Francisco I at ng asawa nitong si Maria Teresa; asawa ni Haring Luis XVI ng Pransiya; at ina ni Luis XVII ng Pransiya. Binitay siya sa pamamagitan ng pagpugot ng ulo gamit ang gilotin noong Himagsikang Pranses at inihimlay kasama ng asawa niya sa Basilika ni San Dionisio sa Paris.


Sa gulang na labing-apat, ipinakasal si Maria Antonieta kay Louis–Auguste, ang Delfin ng Pransiya kung saan siya ay itinanghal bilang Dauphine de France (Delfina ng Pransiya). Noong mamatay ang biyenan niyang si Luis XV noong Mayo 1774, naging hari ng Pransiya ang kanyang asawa bilang Luis XVI at siya naman ang naging reyna ng Pransiya at Navarro. Makalipas ang pitong taon ng pagsasama, isinilang si Maria-Teresa-Carlota ng Pransiya, ang panganay sa kanilang apat na supling.

Noong Pamamayani ng Katatakutan, bago sumiklab ang Himagsikang Pranses, isinibak mula sa trono ang asawa ni Maria Antonieta at ang ipinakulong ang pamilyang maharlika. Nilitis si Maria Antonia, sinampahan ng kataksilan sa bansa at nahatulan ng pagpupugot gamit ang gilotin noong 16 Oktubre 1793, tulad ng paghatol at pagbitay sa kaniyang asawang si Luis siyam na buwan makaraan.

24. What is the message of the poem “ Elegy For My Life by Christopher Tye”


Answer:

"Elegy for My Life" by Christopher Tye is a poem that reflects on the fleeting nature of life and the inevitability of death. The message of the poem is that life is short and we should make the most of the time we have. The speaker of the poem laments the fact that his life has passed by so quickly and that he has not accomplished all that he had hoped to. He reflects on the things he has lost and the things he has gained, and ultimately comes to the realization that life is a precious gift that should be cherished and appreciated. The poem encourages readers to live their lives to the fullest and to make the most of every moment, as time is fleeting and we never know when our time will be up.
