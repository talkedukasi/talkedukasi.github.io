15. give example of fact,convention,opinion and preference assertion


Answer:

FACT:

Examples of fact statements

The leaves of growing plants are usually green. People use their legs to walk. Some people keep dogs as pets. 1 liter of water weighs 1 kilogram.

CONVENTION:

The definition of a convention is a meeting or assembly of people who share a common interest or a convention is a method, practice, rule or custom. An example of convention is a national meeting of English teachers. An example of convention is a rule about comma placement. ... The convention was held in Geneva.

OPINION:

The definition of an opinion is a belief, impression, judgment or prevailing view held by a person. An example of opinion is the San Francisco Giants are the best baseball team. An example of opinion is purple is the best color. An example of opinion is capitalism is better than socialism.

PREFERENCE ASSERTION:

Example: When being interrupted, "Excuse me, I'd like to finish what I'm saying." Empathic Assertion Recognition of other person's situation or feelings followed by another statement standing up for speaker's rights.
