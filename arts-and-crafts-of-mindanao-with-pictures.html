13. Essay: Tell me more about the arts and craft of Mindanao in 1 paragraph with 5 sentences. Write your answer beside the picture.​


The arts and crafts of mindanao have so many specifications depending on the tribe like, B'laan, Maranao, Maguindanaon, Iranon, Tausug and many more. You can differentiate their work of art by its Tribal patterns or Tribal music. Arts and Crafts of Mindanao have beautiful Tribal Patterns and one example is the MALONG.

Answer:

The arts and crafts of Mindanao include their cultural attire, textiles, tapestries, crafts, accessories and body ornaments which are a combination of designs from indigenous people that resides in the regions and the colorful and rich influence from their indigenous belief system. Traditional Maranao arts and crafts is a rich collection. And the “okir” is the design element that brings together all of its folk art into one cohesive visual extravaganza. Okir is a set of leaf, fern or vine motif expressed in most of its art. Whether in woodworks or metalcrafts, okir is a prominent design.

Explanations:

- Sarimanok is a Legendary Bird of the Maranao people, who originate from Mindanao, an island in the Philippines and part of the Philippine mythology

- Okir or okit is the term for rectilinear and curvilinear plat-based design and folk motifs that can be usually found among the Moro and Lumad people the Southern Philippines, as well as parts of Sabah

- Gadur is a Maranao food jar made of brass and has a silver inlay. It is traditionally used as ceremonial containers for rice and tobacco. It shows Maranao art patterns intricately engraved on the surface of the jar
