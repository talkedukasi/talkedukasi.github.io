26. why is it important to use imperatives in giving clear instructions?please answer it right ​


Answer:

We use imperative clauses when we want to tell someone to do something, most commonly for advice, suggestions, requests, commands, orders or instructions. We can use them to tell people to do or not to do things. Giving clear instructions to students can ensure that they fully comprehend what they need to do to achieve in your classroom. It will ease students' nerves, assuage their insecurities, and help them confirm your expectations so that they can be happy and successful in school.
