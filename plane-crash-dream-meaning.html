12. A plane crashes on the border of Canada and the US, 60 people die. Where do they bury the survivors?*


The answer to the riddle is Non.

Because the survivors are those who survived the plane crash. Meaning they are alive, they didn’t die. They are not included in 60 people who died. There is no need to bury them. This is a trick question or riddle.

If this is not a riddle, those who died from the plane crash will neither be buried in US nor in Canada unless they are citizens of that country. If they are not citizens of US or Canada, their bodies will be sent back from their own country by the authorities, and there they will be buried.
