20. 13. This is a kind of electrical joint that is commonly used to join two or more conductors inside the junctionbox? a. Rat tailb. Knotted tap c. Plain tapd. Joints​


The kind of electrical joint that is commonly used to join two or more conductors inside the junction box is a Rat tail. A rat tail is suitable for service where there is no mechanical stress. The rat-tail is can be done with both solid and stranded wire. It is not that strong mechanically that's why it needs electrical tape and also it is not meant to connect wires that will be pulled or stressed that's why the wire needs to be protected inside a junction box.
