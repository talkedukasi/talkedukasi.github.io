1. reflection about Clay by Juan T. Gatbonton?


Answer:

Juan Gatbonton’s short story, Clay, became a landmark piece in Philippine history when it won in the short story category of the prestigious Carlos Palanca Memorial Awards. Published in 1951, the story takes its readers to the far province of Pampanga, back into the time of the American colonization in the country. Narrated by a sixteen year old Filipino boy, the story revolves around the friendship between him and a young American army mechanic named Clay.
