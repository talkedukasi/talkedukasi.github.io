What is a cheerdance?

Cheerdance is the dance part of cheerleading that excludes tumbling and stunts. Each performance lasts around 2.5 minutes and is scored on dancing ability, choreography, and teamwork. The athletes' smiles and acting expressions give this sport a lot of vitality.

What is the importance of cheerdance?

Cheerleaders frequently motivate other students to attend games and participate in school activities. Cheerleading appears to promote group work as well as mental and physical training, and it is a physically demanding sport. Cheerleaders serve as a link between the fans and the team.
