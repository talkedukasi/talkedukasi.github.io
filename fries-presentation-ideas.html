10. prepare a step by step procedure of presenting a fried egg dish with rice and chicken hotdog​


Answer:

Steps:

1. Prepare all the Tools and Ingredients Needed.

2. Peel the Plastic in the Chicken Hotdogs.

3. Cut slits in the Chicken Hotdogs.

4. Put the Cooking Oil in the pan.

5. When the Oil is moderatly hot, fry the Chicken Hotdogs for at least 3 minutes.

6. Take the Hotdogs Out of the Pan.

7. Fry the Egg and Make a Sunny side up at the same oil for at least 3 minutes.

8. Take the Sunny Side up out of the pan.

9. On a Plate, Put a Cup of Rice then place the Chicken Hotdogs and the Sunny side up and set a side.

10. Serve the Dish.
