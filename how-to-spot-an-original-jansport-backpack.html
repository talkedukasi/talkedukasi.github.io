3. Has a very cool back support. The back support and the handles are good, can carry heavy stuff.

4. Waterproof to a certain extent.

Answer:

The location below the earth's surface where the earthquake starts is called the hypocenter, and the location directly above it on the surface of the earth is called the epicenter. - Focus is the place underneath the epicenter, it is the zone underground where earthquake energy originated and released to become seismic waves.
