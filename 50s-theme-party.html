If you want to have 100 guests at your party, you will need to prepare 400 pounds of food. How many guests can you invite if you have 600 pounds of food?

To solve this problem, we need to use the equation for inverse variation: y = k/x, where k is the constant of variation and x and y are the variables in the inverse variation. In this case, k is 400 and x is the number of guests and y is the amount of food.

To find the number of guests we can invite with 600 pounds of food, we can use the equation y = k/x to find x. First, we need to rearrange the equation to solve for x. We can do this by multiplying both sides of the equation by x to get xy = k. Then, we can divide both sides of the equation by y to get x = k/y.

In this problem, we know that y is 600 pounds of food, so we can plug this value into the equation to find the number of guests we can invite: x = 400/600 = 0.67.

Therefore, if you have 600 pounds of food, you can invite 0.67 * 100 = 67 guests to your party.

17. please help me to make a word problem of direct variations, the theme is for Christmas party..thank u in advance .​


Answer:

Sure, here is a possible word problem for direct variations that relates to a Christmas party:

Lara is planning a Christmas party and wants to know how many guests she can invite based on the size of her house. She knows that the number of guests and the size of her house are directly proportional. She also knows that when her house is 500 square feet, she can invite 50 guests. Based on this information, how many guests can Lara invite to her party if her house is 1000 square feet?

To solve this problem, we can use the equation y = kx, where y is the number of guests, x is the size of the house in square feet, and k is the constant of proportionality. Since we know that when x = 500, y = 50, we can set up the equation as follows:

50 = k * 500

Solving for k, we get k = 0.1. This means that for every 100 square feet of house size, Lara can invite 10 guests. Therefore, if her house is 1000 square feet, she can invite 1000 * 0.1 = 100 guests. Answer: \boxed{100}.

30. explaination of party reps 50% of the members of parliament (40 seats)​


Answer:

"Party reps 50% of the members of parliament (40 seats)" means that a political party has been able to secure 50% of the total seats in the parliament, which in this case is 40 seats. This suggests that the political party has a significant presence and influence in the parliament, and they may be able to have a significant impact on decision-making processes and the passing of laws.

It's important to note that the specific details of how this came about would depend on the political system and election laws of the country in question. In some systems, political parties may win a certain number of seats in parliament based on the percentage of votes they receive in an election. In other systems, seats may be allocated based on a combination of direct election of individual candidates and proportional representation of parties.
