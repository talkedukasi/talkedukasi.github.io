6. Which is easier the Philippine Folk Dance or the Foreign Folk Dance? Explain.​


Answer:

The Philippine Folk Dance is generally considered to be easier than the Foreign Folk Dance. This is because Philippine Folk Dances are typically slower and simpler in their movements and do not require as much skill and coordination as the Foreign Folk Dances. Additionally, Philippine Folk Dances often incorporate the use of props, such as bamboo poles or handkerchiefs, which can make the dances easier to learn.

24. how important is folk dance in the foreign countries? ​


Answer:

The reason that ‘foreign’ folk dance is important is because it is only ‘foreign’ geographically. Though there are, of course many wonderful and sometimes unique regional characteristics, there are also broad strands of cultural continuity between the dances of adjacent cultures. National boundaries are usually very permeable to language, music and dance. An overview of folk arts show us that we are all brothers and sisters, we should celebrate our unique differences, but we should rejoice at being part of the same family.
