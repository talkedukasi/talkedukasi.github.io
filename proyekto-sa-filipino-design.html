5. Houses design by early filipinos?​


Answer:

Early filipinos are using woods as their wall, floor and roof. They usually use bamboo, narra, oak etc. They make their own designs and texture depends in their taste or sometimes, they'll just get some ideas from other's designs in their house.

Explanation:

This decade's design is very different from the old designs in the Philippines. We all usually use tiles in our floor and any color of painting in our wall or just a plain wallpaper, we also use doorknobs in our doors and glass windows. But some filipinos like the old designs so it's their decision on what design they will use, many houses has an old design in Ilocos.
