Factors that influence employee compensation and benefits:

Job responsibilities and tasksEducation, skills, and experienceIndustry and market trendsCompany size and revenueGeographic locationCollective bargaining agreementsGovernment regulations and lawsSupply and demand for certain skills and rolesCost of living and inflationCompany culture and values

Different Performance Appraisal Methods:

It provides a relative evaluation of employee performance, rather than an absolute evaluation.Management by Objectives: This method involves setting specific, measurable, and achievable goals for employees and evaluating their performance based on their ability to meet these goals.360 Degree Feedback: This method involves gathering feedback from multiple sources, including managers, peers, subordinates, and customers.
