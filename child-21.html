25. there were 10 1/2 loaves of bread which were equally shared with 21 street children. what part of the bread did each child get?


Answer:

Each child gets 1/2 loaf of bread.

Step-by-step explanation:

Change 10  1/2 to improper fraction:

10 1/2 = 21/2

Loaves of bread: 21/2

Number or children: 21

Divide the loaves of bread by 21:

21/2  ÷  21

In dividing fractions, multiply the dividend by the reciprocal of divisor:

21/2  × 1/21 = 1/2

Each child gets 1/2 loaf of bread.
