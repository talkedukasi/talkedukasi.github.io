14. polis -acropolisagora -​


Answer:

Polis

-plural poleis literally means "city" in Greek. It defined the administrative and religious city center, as distinct from the rest of the city. It can also signify a body of citizens. A polis (plural: poleis) was the typical structure of a community in the ancient Greek world. A polis consisted of an urban centre, often fortified and with a sacred centre built on a natural acropolis or harbour, which controlled a surrounding territory (chora) of land.

Acropolis

-the upper fortified part of an ancient Greek city (such as Athens) also : a usually fortified height of a city or district elsewhere (as in Central America)

agora

-was a central public space in ancient Greek city-states. It is the best representation of a city-state's response to accommodate the social and political order of the polis. The literal meaning of the word "agora" is "gathering place" or "assembly.

25. The sum of Cora's age and Rose's age is 40. Cora is 4 years older than Rose. How old is Cora?


Answer:

Cora is 22 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age Word Problem

Age word problems are problems dealing with the ages of people currently, in the past or in the future.

Let us analyze the problem.

Given:

Rose's age - x

Cora's age - x + 4 (4 years older than Rose)

sum of Cora's age and Rose's age - 40

Equation:

x + x + 4 = 40

Solution:

x + x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 = 40

2x = 40 - 4

2x = 36

2x/2 = 36/2

x = 18

Rose is 18 years old.

x + 4 = 18 + 4 = 22

Cora is 22 years old.

Checking:

x + x + 4 = 40

18 + 18 + 4 = 40

40 = 40 ✔

27. The sum of cora age and rose age is 40 cora is 4 years older than rose how old is cora now


Answer:

Let's use algebra to solve the problem:

Let x be Rose's age.

Then, Cora's age is x + 4 (since Cora is 4 years older than Rose).

We know that the sum of their ages is 40, so we can set up an equation:

x + (x + 4) = 40

Simplifying the equation:

2x + 4 = 40

Subtracting 4 from both sides:

2x = 36

Dividing both sides by 2:

x = 18

So, Rose is currently 18 years old.

To find Cora's age, we can use the equation we set up earlier:

Cora's age = Rose's age + 4 = 18 + 4 = 22

Therefore, Cora is currently 22 years old.
