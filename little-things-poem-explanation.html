HERE ARE THE ANSWER;

1.

The phrase "the little things" refers to the small, seemingly insignificant details or actions that can have a big impact on our lives or relationships. It's often used to talk about the ways in which people show care or appreciation for one another, such as through acts of kindness or thoughtfulness.

2.

It is often said that "actions speak louder than words" because people can say one thing but their actions may reveal a different truth. This phrase suggests that it is more reliable to pay attention to what someone does rather than what they say, because actions are more difficult to fake or deceive with.

3.

It's important to recognize that every day is a new opportunity to make positive changes in your life and to find happiness. Holding onto negative feelings or dwelling on the past can prevent us from moving forward and experiencing joy in the present. By letting go of negative emotions and focusing on the present moment, we can open ourselves up to new experiences and opportunities for happiness. It's also important to remember that finding happiness is a journey, and it's not always easy. It requires effort and a willingness to try new things and to keep an open mind. But with a positive attitude and determination, it is possible to find happiness and to create a fulfilling life.

14. the poem of sad little houses ​


Answer:

The sad little houses, the poor little houses

Crowded by the riverside.

The holes in their roofs, the cracks in their walls

Vainly they try to hide.

The stench of the river, the dark, muddy river

Is with them night and day,

Pure, fresh air may be had for the moving

But they can’t move away.

They can only stand by the bank of the river

Buried knee-deep in clay.

They can only stare at the drifting garbage

Until they, too decay.

And, pass away into ghosts of houses

Killed by burning sun,

Smothered with dust and then forgotten-

Forgotten by everyone.
