4. During the distribution of relief goods, John carried a pack that weighs 4 2/3 kg while Dan, a pack that weighs 4 4/5 kg. What was the total mass carried by the two boys?​


Answer:

9 7/15 kg

Step-by-step explanation:

4 2/3 + 4 4/5=

Find the Least Common Multiple (LCD) of 4 2/3 and 4 4/5

3= 3, 6, 9, 12, 15...

5= 5, 10, 15...

LCD= 15

15 (LCD) divided by the denominator of the fractions, and multiply by it's numerator. The answer will be the new numerator

15÷3= 5

5×2=10

15÷5=3

3×4=12

so it'll be

8 & 10/15 + 12/15 = 8 22/15

But since it is improper fraction, we will divide the numerator (22) to the denominator (15)

22÷15= 1.7

The whole number (1) shall be added to 8 = 9, and the decimal will be the numerator

9 7/15
