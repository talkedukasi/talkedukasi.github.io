9. It takes 20 men to build a house for 60 days. how many men will be needed to built it in 15 days?


Ratio and Proportion:

The answer is 80 men.

Solution:

Express the statement "20 men to build a house for 60 days" as ratio.

20:60

Express the other statement as ratio using x for the number of men.

x:15

Using both ratios, write the proportion.

20/60 = x/15

Since the ratio of 15 to 60 is 4, then we will multiply 20 by 4 to solve for x.

20 x 4 = 80

Therefore, it will need 80 men to finish the house in 15 days.

Definitions:

A ratio is a comparison between two things such as boys to girls, teachers to pupils, chairs to students, books to pupils, and many others.

Examples:

A recipe for pancakes uses 3 cups of flour and 2 cups of milk. This is expressed as 3:2. This means that for every 3 cups of flour, 2 cups of milk must be added.

There are 6 puppies, 3 are boys, and 3 are girls. The ratio of a boy puppy to a girl puppy is 3:3 or 1:1. This means that for every boy puppy there is also a girl puppy.

28. men are born and remain free equal in eights​


Answer:

The phrase "men are born and remain free and equal in rights" is a famous quote from the Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen, a document adopted by the French National Assembly in 1789 during the French Revolution.

The idea behind this statement is that all human beings are born with inherent rights, and that these rights cannot be taken away by any government or institution. It also emphasizes the importance of equality among individuals, regardless of their social status or background.

Today, this phrase is often used as a foundational principle in discussions of human rights and social justice. While it has historically been interpreted as applying only to men, modern interpretations generally extend it to all people, regardless of gender.
