Question:

The ratio of calamansi, dalandan, and pomelo trees in Korina’s farm is 8:3:2. How many pomelo trees are there out of the 780 trees in the farm?

Solution:

get the value of ratio x

8x + 3x + 2x = 780

13x =780

x = 60

8(60) = 480 calamansi

3(60) = 180 dalandan trees

2(60) = 120 pomelo trees

Answer:

There are 120 pomelo trees.
