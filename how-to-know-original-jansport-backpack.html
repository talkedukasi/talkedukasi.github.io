1. how to know jansport is original or fake


Jansport product is not fake if it has the following characteristics: (In bag)

1. Zips There should be only one fabric hanging at one zip, not two zips.

2. Logo Stitching must be very pretty.

3. Has a very cool back support. The back support and the handles are good, can carry heavy stuff.

4. Waterproof to a certain extent.
