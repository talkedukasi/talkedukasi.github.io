3. 12 little things that you can do to help your country


12 Little Things That You Can Do to Help Your Country

Follow the rules and laws of the country, for example, traffic rules.Ask for official receipts when buying or paying anything. These receipts are proofs that help both the consumer and seller.Support Filipino products buy locally instead of imported and smuggled products. This will help boost the local economy.Always speak of the positive aspects of the country when talking to foreigners. It was never good to speak ill of something. Respect the authorities like traffic officers, police officers, and soldiers.Keep the surroundings clean and tidy. Avoid littering and practice segregation, recycling, and conservation.Show support and help the church, like spreading the word of God.Be an honest and wise voter during elections. Practice voting seriously and help solve problems that arise in the voting precincts.Pay people the right amount for the product or service that you are availing.Always pay the taxes on time.Help poor child avail education. Share tips on how to have a scholarship, and if you have extra money, give them a little of it which they can use to buy food or learning materials. Be a good example to the younger generations and teach them these little things about loving a country.
