1. how to write excuse letter


Set up the margin.

Put the date on the upper right part of the paper.

Write"To Whom It May Concern" or "Dear________" on the leftmost part of the margin to the right.

Write your sayings about what,why,how,when,because etc. as the BODY OF THE LETTER.Make sure that capitalization and indentation is correct.

Write your name or your parents' name on the lower right part of the paper with a signature.
