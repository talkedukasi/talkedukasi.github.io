1. what is a democratic intervention?What are the processes of democratic intervention?Why is there a democratic intevention?​


Answer:

Answer:

A democratic intervention is a foreign intervention in a country's affairs with the aim of promoting democracy and human rights. Democratic intervention is justified based on the belief that every individual has a right to freedom, democracy, and respect for human rights. Democratic intervention can be carried out through diplomatic measures, sanctions, and military operations.

The processes of democratic intervention include:

1. Diplomatic pressure: Interventionists may pressure foreign governments through diplomatic channels and international organizations, such as the United Nations.

2. Economic sanctions: Interventionists may impose economic sanctions on a country with the aim of destabilizing its government and pressuring it to change its behavior.

3. Military intervention: In extreme cases, interventionists may resort to military intervention. This may involve air strikes, ground troops, or peacekeeping forces.

There are several reasons for democratic intervention, including:

1. Human rights abuses: Interventionists may intervene in a country with a history of human rights abuses to protect citizens from repression and violence.

2. Genocide or ethnic cleansing: Interventionists may intervene in cases of genocide or ethnic cleansing to deter these crimes against humanity and protect the targeted group.

3. Threats to international security: Interventionists may intervene in a country when its actions pose a threat to international peace and security. This may include cases involving the proliferation of weapons of mass destruction or terrorist activities.

7. Ano ba Ang Democratic Intervention?


Answer:

Ang isang demokratikong interbensyon ay isang interbensyon ng militar ng mga panlabas na puwersa na may layuning tulungan ang demokrasya ng bansa kung saan nagaganap ang interbensyon. Kabilang sa mga halimbawa ang interbensyon sa Afghanistan at Iraq.

Explanation:

Ang lohika sa likod ng demokratikong interbensyon ay sapat na malinaw: bihirang ang mga demokrasya kung magkasama sa digmaan. Ang matatag na demokrasya ay nakakaranas din ng ilang mga digmaang sibil. Kung ang isang digmaang sibil ay nangyayari gayunpaman sa isang matatag na demokrasya, bilang isang pangkalahatang tuntunin ang alitan ay hindi gaanong madugo kaysa sa isang bansang may awtoridad. Bihira din ang genocide at politika sa mga demokrasya. Ang isang pagtaas sa bilang ng mga matatag na demokrasya, tumatakbo ang argumento, ay mag-aambag sa mas mababang antas ng karahasan sa parehong mga domestic at panlabas na salungatan. Ang mga bansang demokratiko sa pangkalahatan ay lumitaw mula sa mga digmaan. Ang nawawalang partido ay madalas na nakakaranas ng pagbabago ng rehimen, na mas madalas kaysa sa hindi sa isang demokratikong direksyon. Alinsunod dito ay maaaring makatutukso na gumamit ng puwersang militar upang pasiglahin ang paglaki ng demokrasya sa mga dating estado ng awtoridad. Kung ang isang sumusunod sa linyang ito ng pag-iisip, ang digmaan ay maaaring humantong sa demokrasya - at sa gayon ay kapayapaan.

8. environmental ethics ppt


Answer:

What is environmental ethics/what does it mean?

It is the discipline in philosophy that people study about the truthful relationship of human beings, and also the value/importance of the environment.

What is an example of environmental ethics?

Things like water and air pollution, the use of natural resources, loss of biodiversity, destruction of the community of living creatures, and global climate change are all part of the environmental ethics.

What is the purpose of environmental ethics?

The purpose of environmental ethics is that they maintain to provide moral grounds for social policies aimed at protecting the earth's environment and setting environmental humiliation/shame right.

Democracy - means a form of government in which the common people hold political power and can rule either directly or through elected representatives. Example: United States, Philippines  

Intervention - is the act or fact of becoming involved intentionally in a difficult situation  

Military intervention is used as a means of building democracy in a particular country. The reason behind democratic intervention is because democratic countries rarely to war with each other.
