2. which of the following dance steps has step pattern of step, close, step and a counting of 1 2 3 A.waltz B.cross-waltz C.Mazurka D.Redoba


Which of the following dance steps has step pattern of step, close, step and a counting of 1 2 3 A.waltz B.cross-waltz C.Mazurka D. Redoba

The answer is A. Here are the explanation.

Cross Waltz-count to one scale 1, 2, 3.Step R across L in front and raise the L foot slightly backwards  (ct.1), step L near to R in rear (ct. 2), step R forward (ct. 3). On ct. 1. Knees are slightly bent.  Step on rear foot ball at ct. 2. Typically this step is taken in either the forward or the sideward direction.  3/4 Signing time

Waltz balance-count 1, 2, 3 to calculate. Step R forward (ct. 1), near L to R in third rear or first place  and lift both heels (ct. 2), lower heels with body weight on R foot (ct. 3). This can happen in any direction.3/4 Signing time

Waltz step – Count 1, 2, 3 to the measure Step R sideward (ct. 1) then close L to R (ct. 2) and step R to position  (ct. 3) 3/4 Time signature.
