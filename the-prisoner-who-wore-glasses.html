25. Three-fifths of the students who attended the Christmas Party wore red. The rest of the students wore green. If 12 wore green, how many students attended the Christmas Party?


Problem:

Three-fifths of the students who attended the Christmas Party wore red. The rest of the students wore green. If 12 wore green, how many students attended the Christmas party?

Let:

x = no. of students who attended the Christmas party

⅗x = wore red

⅖x = wore green

⅖x = 12

x = 12(5/2)

x = 30 students

To check:

⅗(30) = 18 --> wore red

⅖(30) = 12 --> wore green

18 + 12 = 30 students
