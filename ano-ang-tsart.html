1. ano ang tsart? sagutin ang tsart​


Answer:

A chart or tsart in tagalog is a graphical representation for data visualization, in which "the data is represented by symbols, such as bars in a bar chart, lines in a line chart, or slices in a pie chart". A chart can represent tabular numeric data, functions or some kinds of quality structure and provides different info.
