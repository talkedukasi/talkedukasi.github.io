29. patricia and dorothy have 80 bookmarks altogether. dorothy has 3/5 of the number patricia has. how many bookmarks does patricia have?


Patricia's bookmark: x
Dorothy's bookmark:  (3/5)x      Three-fifths of what Patricia has.
Total Bookmarks: 80

Equation:

(Patricia's) + (Dorothy's) = 80

x +  3/5(x) = 80

(5) [x + 3/5(x) = 80](5)     Multiply each term by LCD 5

5x + 3x = 400

8x = 400

8x/8 = 400/8

x = 50 

Substitute, x = 50:

Patricia's bookmarks: x = 50 bookmarks
Dorothy's bookmarks: 3/5(x) = 3/5 (50) = 30 bookmarks

ANSWER:  Patricia has 50 bookmarks.

Check:

Patricia's bookmarks + Dorothy's bookmarks = 80
 50 + 30 = 80
80 = 80   (True)
