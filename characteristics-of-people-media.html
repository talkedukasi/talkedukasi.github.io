1. What is meant by the term "people in media"?what are the characteristics of "people as media​


Answer:

Persons active in the usage, analysis, assessment, and creation of media and information are referred to as media professionals. Those who work in the press are media practitioners, which implies they are media-related professionals. At the same time, people who consume media are not media practitioners but rather media consumers or consumers of media.

People as media means that anybody such as a normal citizen is well versed in using media. They don’t need to be experts or professional to share the information they want to give. An example is a blogger.

People in media however are experts or professional who are well-versed in the information they provide. An example is a news anchor.

You are holding your thoughts because you consider the different people around you. Characteristic of communication which is shown in this situation is contextual.

About context of communication

The context of communication is the ease of understanding processes in more complex communication. There are many types of context in communication, ranging from intrapersonal to cross-cultural.

The context referred to here is situational, this means that the communication process will be limited by a number of factors. Starting from the number of people communicating, the feedback, the distance between people who are communicating, and the channel used.

Understanding Communication Context

The context of communication can be interpreted as a place or environment for communication to occur.

The communication context serves not only to facilitate humans but also to learn the communication process. But it becomes one of the backgrounds for researchers and theorists to analyze a phenomenon.

The Importance of Communication Context

Learning communication also has many advantages and various benefits that can be felt when socializing in society. Communication plays an important role in human life, both socially and personally.

Therefore, studying communication theory and its application is very important for us. Communication is very effective in helping you solve various problems in your social, personal and professional life and also improve relationships in society.

Many problems occur in social life due to failures when communicating such as artist conflicts, political conflicts, and so on.

Therefore, the more a person withdraws from communication, the more he or she cannot communicate, so that person cannot have a good relationship with fellow human beings.

Then there are many advantages that we get when we study communication science, such as, being able to communicate effectively, so as not to be misperceived, and to get a lot of relationships.

In addition, in the world of work, communication skills are needed and rarely owned by everyone. That is why communication science is very important for us to learn.
