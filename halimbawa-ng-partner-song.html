18. ano ang pagkakaiba ng round songs at partner songs sa tagalog​


Answer:

Rounds are when two (or more) groups sing the same song at different times to create harmony.

Partner songs are when two different songs are sung at the same time to create harmony.

Explanation:

CARRY ON LEARNING :)

Answer:

English Explanation:

Since some of the rounds have longer lines between parts, they require two systems. ... In partner songs, the systems (groups of staves) are bracketed together, indicating that, after the initial singing of each part separately, the parts each start at the beginning of their own line and at the same time.

Tagalog Explanation:

Dahil ang ilan sa mga pag-ikot ay may mas mahahabang linya sa pagitan ng mga bahagi, nangangailangan sila ng dalawang mga system. ... Sa mga kasosyo na kanta, ang mga system (mga grupo ng mga staves) ay magkakabit, na nagpapahiwatig na, pagkatapos ng paunang pag-awit ng bawat bahagi nang magkahiwalay, ang mga bahagi ay nagsisimula bawat isa sa simula ng kanilang sariling linya at sa parehong oras.

28. SimulanPanuto: Isulat ang pagkakaiba ng round songs at partner songs. Isulat angsagot sa loob ng organizerRound SongsPartner SongsPagkakaiba​


Answer:

Round songs

Start the round simply. Ask the first part to sing the entire song two times; the first time, they will sing it alone. Ask the second part to listen to the song the first time through, listening for the point where the second part starts. During the second pass, help the second part students jump in on cue.

partner songs

Partner songs are melodies which fit together in such a way that they sound good when performed together. They are melodies performed as independent musical voices. Because two or more melodic voices are performed at the same time, harmony is created.
