7. In the poem Get Up and Bar the Door, What was the couple's behavior like? Do they seem like an ideal couple? Why or why not?​


"Get Up and Bar the Door" humorously depicts the stubbornness of a wife and husband. The wife is hardworking and busy, while the husband is simply giving her orders. When a wind blows the door open, the husband orders the wife to "bar the door." The wife tells him off, as she is busy working, and he isn't doing anything. They make a deal that the first one to speak will be the one who has to bar the door.
