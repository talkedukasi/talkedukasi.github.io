A short story is not the same as flash fiction. This is because flash fiction is a shorter version of a short story, though the length can vary depending on the magazine or journal. So, it's somewhere between 100 and 1000 words. Also, the story shouldn't have more than one plot and one main idea. On the other hand, a short story is a fictional story told in prose that is shorter than a novel. It often contains a small number of characters and multiple major themes. Additionally, its length might range from 1,000 to 10,000 words.

3 Things that makeup flash fiction1. Brevity

In flash fiction, a whole story is told in just a few paragraphs.

2. A complete plot

There is a beginning, middle, and end to a flash fiction story.

3. Surprise ←

Surprise is often a part of good flash fiction. This usually comes in the form of a twist ending or the last line that you didn't expect. This isn't a trick; the point is to make the reader think deeply about what the story is really about.

25. the ratio of the number of fiction books to the number of non-fiction books in a library is 5:2. If there are 1421 fiction and non-fiction books altogethee, how many more fiction than non fiction books are there in the library?​


Answer:

There are 609 more fiction books than non fiction books in the library.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio Word Problem

What is ratio?

Ratio indicates how many times one number contains another. It shows the the relative sizes of two or more values.

The problem above is a ratio word problem since it is asking for how many more fiction than non fiction books there are in the library based on the ratio of the books.

Given:

ratio of fiction books to non-fiction books - 5:2

total number of books - 1421

Equation:

Let x be the number.

5x + 2x = 1421

Solution:

5x + 2x = 1421

7x = 1421

7x/7 = 1421/7

x = 203

Fiction Books

5x = 5 (203) = 1015

There are 1015 fiction books.

Non-Fiction Books

2x = 2 (203) = 406

There are 406 non-fiction books.

To know how many more fiction than non fiction books there are in the library, we need to subtract.

1015 - 406 = 609

Final Answer:

There are 609 more fiction books than non fiction books in the library.

Checking:

5x + 2x = 1421

1015 + 406 = 1421

1421 = 1421 ✔

Cross multiply.

5:2 = 1015:406

2 × 1015 = 5 × 406

2030 = 2030 ✔
