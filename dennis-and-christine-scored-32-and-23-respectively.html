Answer:

The sum of their ages is 32, that means that the sum of their present ages is 32.

In algebraic expression,

1)JD+C=32    ,    C stands for christine

JD is four years older than christine in five years

it can be expressed as

JD=C+4 but since there is a five years, means we will add 5 years to their present ages, Then

JD+5=(C+4)+5 simplify,

2)JD=C+4  

Equate a and b :

Since JD is equal to c+4, substitute to equation number 1

(c+4)+c=32   simplifying,

2c=28  or C=14

To find the value of age of JD, substitute the age of christine(c) in the equation number 2, Then

JD=14+4

JD=18

Thus, the present age of JD and Christine are respectively 18 and 14
