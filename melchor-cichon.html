2. A Letter by Melchor Cichon literary analysis​


Answer:

I think the wife is sharing her sentiments to her husband that she does not want to live or stay in the city anymore. She misses the simplicity of their provincial life that is why she likes to go home already. However, they cannot go home just yet because they still have an obligation to pay. They have to stay in the city to work and earn and of course, pay her earrings which she loaned from Mama San. 18. it was engineered by fr. melchor de vera a jesuit priest​


Answer:

Fort Pilar

Explanation:

The royal fort of Our Virgin Lady of the Pillar is one of Zamboanga's most important historical sites. The Fort Pillar Shrine dates back to the 17th century. It was a strategic defensive fortress built in Zamboanga by the Spanish colonial government. According to legend, the fort was founded in 1635 by Fr. Melchor de Vera, a Jesuit priest-engineer, to defend the city from invaders such as the Moros, the Dutch, and the British. Devotees congregate to hear and attend mass, as well as to light candles. The fortress is widely regarded as the most magnificent in the world.

22. Sino si haring melchor sa el filibusterismo


 Ang kabanata 5 sa El Filibusterismo ay pagpapakita ng tradisyon ng mga Pilipino sa panahon ng kapaskuhan. Sa kabanatang ito matatagpuan ang pag kakaroon ng mga prusisyon ng mga santo at pagpapakilala sa kanilang ambag sa pananampalataya. Sa eksenang ito dumaan ang tatlong Mago ito ay sina Haring Melchor, Haring Gaspar, at Haring Baltazar.

  Si haring Melchor ay isa sa mga tatlong hari na bumisita sa panahon ng pag silang ni Kristo. Isa siya sa pinakamagiting na hari ng Persia kinilala siya bilang santo ng mga maysakit na Epylepsy. Sa paglalakad ng prusisyon napansin ni Sinong ang itsura ni Haring Melchor na tila maiitim ang itsura ng nasabing estatwa.

  Pagkatapos ng prusisyon na pansin ng mapang aping guardiya sibil na walang ilaw ang karitela ng mahal na birhen. kaya't agad na pinarusahan ang kaawa awang si Sinong.
