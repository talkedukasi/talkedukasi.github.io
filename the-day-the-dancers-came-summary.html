30. Modern dance was developed in the early 1900’s the time when dancers came out against the stiffness and restraints of classical ballet.


Modern dance was developed in the early 1900s, the time when dancers came out against the stiffness and restraints of classical ballet. [TRUE]

It created its own techniques, costumes, shoes, and gave more attention to self-expression and individual creativity rather than technical expertise.

Modern dance has its own characteristics that make this type of dance growing. Generally, the influence of the development of modern dance is based on the creativity and innovation of the art activists.

Types of Modern Dance

Here are the types of modern dance:

1. Ballet dance

Ballet dance developed in the 18th century AD and comes from the Italian language, namely ballo which means dance. When viewed in more detail, the movement in ballet is similar to fencing. The difference is that this movement combines artistic elements, such as music, costumes, and stage decorations.

2. Tango dance

According to the book Art of Culture by Yoyok RM, et al, the tango dance is a social dance originating from Latin America and has been popular all over the world.

The rhythm is based on a 2/4 or 4/4 count, while the tempo is moderate. This dance movement is very attractive because it is done with steps that give the impression and feeling of floating.

3. Flamenco dance

Flamenco dance is a type of Spanish dance. Its distinctive feature is the rhythmic pattern of various strokes of the dancer's feet, namely the pounding of the toe, heel, or the entire sole. This dance is usually accompanied by guitar, applause, pounding feet, and shouts from the audience.

4. Salsa dance

Salsa is a dance in pairs related to salsa music. The word salsa comes from Spanish which means sauce which means taste or style.

5. Break dance

Break dance is a dance that comes from a group of black American workers. The form of the dance is in the form of broken movements and is acrobatic.

This dance was also born at the same time as the development of rap music which was famous in the black working American community.

6. Contemporary dance

This dance is accompanied by a mix of traditional musical instruments with the addition of unusual modern musical instruments, for example songs from simple ones to using computer music programs, such as midi, Nuendo, and even orchestras.

7. Waltz dance

Waltz is a type of dance in pairs that are danced in 3/4 bars with different tempos. In Germany, for example, this dance is performed at a slow tempo. Meanwhile in Vienna, this dance is performed at a faster and more vibrant tempo.
